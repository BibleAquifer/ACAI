# Uz (Genesis 22:21)
**Uz (Genesis 22:21)**. 
Uz, also known as Huz or Buz, was a man living during the time of the patriarchs, mentioned in Genesis 22:21, and was the son of Nahor and Milcah (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Buz*, *Huz*.
