# Absalom
**Absalom**. 
Son of King David and his wife Maacah, who rebelled against David and tried to become king (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Abishalom*. 
Roles include: 
_Royal Family Member_. 
Absalom is also referred to as: 
[Abishalom (Absalom)](Abishalom.md).
