# Oholah
**Oholah**. 
A woman who personifies the city of Samaria and the northern kingdom of Israel (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Aholah*. 




**Familial Relationships of Oholah**


* Gender: female
* Sibling(s): [Oholibah](Oholibah.md)
