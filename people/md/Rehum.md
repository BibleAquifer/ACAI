# Rehum
**Rehum**. 
An Israelite leader who returned with Zerubbabel to the land during the time of Nehemiah (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Harim*, *Nehum*. 
Roles include: 
_Leader_. 
Rehum is also referred to as: 
[Harim (Rehum)](Harim.5.md), [Nehum (Rehum)](Nehum.md). 




**Familial Relationships of Rehum**


* Gender: male
