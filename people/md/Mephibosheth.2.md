# Mephibosheth (2 Samuel 21:8)
**Mephibosheth (2 Samuel 21:8)**. 
A man of the tribe of Benjamin during Israel's united kingdom, son of Saul and Rizpah, mentioned only in 2 Samuel 21:8 (acai).
