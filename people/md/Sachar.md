# Sachar
**Sachar**. 
Sachar, also known as Sacar or Sakar, was a Levite and son of Obed-edom, living during Israel's united kingdom and mentioned in 1 Chronicles 26:4 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Sacar*, *Sakar*.
