# Shadrach (Hananiah)
**Shadrach (Hananiah)**. 
Daniel's compatriot living at the time of Exile and Return (tipnr-short). 
Other forms include: 
*Hananiah*. 
Roles include: 
_Prisoner_, _Royal Family Member_.

[Hananiah](Hananiah.15.md).

[Hananiah](Hananiah.15.md).
