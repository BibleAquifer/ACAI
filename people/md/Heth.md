# Heth
**Heth**. 
Son of the patriarch Noah's grandson Canaan and regarded as the founder of the Hittites (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Hittite*, *of Heth*. 
Roles include: 
_Leader_, _Patriarch_. 
Heth is also referred to as: 
group:Hittite (Group).
