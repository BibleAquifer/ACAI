# Meshelemiah
**Meshelemiah**. 
A gatekeeper of the Temple during the time of the Israelite exile and return (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Shelemiah*. 
Roles include: 
_Priest_. 
Meshelemiah is also referred to as: 
[Shelemiah (Meshelemiah)](Shelemiah.md).
