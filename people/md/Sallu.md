# Sallu
**Sallu**. 
Sallu, also called Sallai, was a man living during the exile and return, mentioned in Nehemiah 12:7 and Nehemiah 12:20 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Sallai*. 
Sallu is also referred to as: 
[Sallai (Sallu)](Sallai.2.md).
