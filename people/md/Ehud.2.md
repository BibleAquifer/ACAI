# Ehud (1 Chronicles 7:10)
**Ehud (1 Chronicles 7:10)**. 
Ehud, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 7:10, was a man of the tribe of Benjamin living during the time of the divided kingdom, son of Bilhan and brother to several siblings (acai).
