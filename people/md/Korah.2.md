# Korah (Genesis 36:16)
**Korah (Genesis 36:16)**. 
An Edomite man living during the time of the patriarchs, mentioned only in Genesis 36:16, son of Eliphaz and brother of Teman, Omar, Zepho, Gatam, Kenaz, and Amalek (acai).
