# Shilhi
**Shilhi**. 
Shilhi was a man living during the divided kingdom, first mentioned in 1 Kings 22:42 and was the father of Azubah (acai). 




**Familial Relationships of Shilhi**


* Gender: male
* Offspring: [Azubah](Azubah.md)
