# Merodach-baladan
**Merodach-baladan**. 
A king of Babylon during the time of the divided kingdom, first mentioned in 2 Kings 20:12 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Berodach-baladan*, *Marduk-Baladan*. 




**Familial Relationships of Merodach-baladan**


* Gender: male
* Father: [Baladan](Baladan.md)
