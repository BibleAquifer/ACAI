# Ami
**Ami**. 
A man living during the time of exile and return, mentioned in Ezra 2:57 and also called Amon in Nehemiah 7:59 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Amon*. 
Ami is also referred to as: 
[Amon (Ami)](Amon.md).
