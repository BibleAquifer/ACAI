# Kedemah
**Kedemah**. 
A man of the Arabs, son of Ishmael, mentioned in Genesis 25:15, also called Nodab in 1 Chronicles 5:19 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Nodab*. 
Kedemah is also referred to as: 
[Nodab (Kedemah)](Nodab.md).
