# Eleasah (1 Chronicles 8:37)
**Eleasah (1 Chronicles 8:37)**. 
Eleasah, also called Eshek, was a man of the tribe of Benjamin who lived during the time of the divided kingdom, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 8:37 (acai). 
Other forms include:
