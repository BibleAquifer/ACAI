# Nicodemus
**Nicodemus**. 
A member of the Jewish Sanhedrin and secret disciple of Jesus who buried Jesus with Joseph of Aramathea (acai). 
Roles include: 
_Leader_, _Teacher_. 




**Familial Relationships of Nicodemus**


* Gender: male
