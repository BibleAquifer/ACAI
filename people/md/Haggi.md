# Haggi
**Haggi**. 
Haggi, also known as Haggite, was a man from the tribe of Gad living during the time of Egypt and the wilderness, first mentioned in Genesis 46:16 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Haggite*.

* Gender: male
