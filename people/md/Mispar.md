# Mispar
**Mispar**. 
Mispar, also called Mispereth, was a man who lived during the time of exile and return, mentioned in Ezra 2:2 and Nehemiah 7:7 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Mispereth*. 
Mispar is also referred to as: 
[Mispereth (Mispar)](Mispereth.md).
