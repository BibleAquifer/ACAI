# Zedekiah (1 Chronicles 3:16)
**Zedekiah (1 Chronicles 3:16)**. 
Zedekiah, son of Jehoiachin and brother of Shealtiel, was a man of the tribe of Judah living during the time of the divided kingdom, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 3:16 (acai).
