# Seraiah (2 Kings 25:23)
**Seraiah (2 Kings 25:23)**. 
Seraiah, mentioned in 2 Kings 25:23, was a man living during the time of the divided kingdom and the son of Tanhumeth (acai). 




**Familial Relationships of Seraiah (2 Kings 25:23)**


* Gender: male
