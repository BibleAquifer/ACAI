# Dedan (Genesis 25:3)
**Dedan (Genesis 25:3)**. 
Dedan, mentioned in Genesis 25:3, was the son of Jokshan, brother of Sheba, and father of Asshurim, Letushim, and Leummim, living during the time of the patriarchs (acai).
