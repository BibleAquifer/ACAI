# Zedekiah (Nehemiah 10:1)
**Zedekiah (Nehemiah 10:1)**. 
Zedekiah, also known as Zidkijah, was a man living during the time of exile and return, mentioned in Nehemiah 10:1 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Zidkijah*.
