# Zebadiah (1 Chronicles 8:17)
**Zebadiah (1 Chronicles 8:17)**. 
Zebadiah was a man of the tribe of Benjamin, living at the time of the divided kingdom, and the son of Elpaal, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 8:17 (acai).
