# Micah (Jeremiah 26:18)
**Micah (Jeremiah 26:18)**. 
Micah, also known as Micaiah, was a prophet living during the divided kingdom, first mentioned in Jeremiah 26:18 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Micaiah*. 
Roles include: 
_Prophet_.
