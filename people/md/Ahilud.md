# Ahilud
**Ahilud**. 
An official (and perhaps a recorder) during the reign of King David. He was the father of Jehoshaphat (acai). 
Roles include: 
_Official_. 




**Familial Relationships of Ahilud**


* Gender: male
* Offspring: [Jehoshaphat](Jehoshaphat.md), [Baana](Baana.md)
