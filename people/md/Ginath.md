# Ginath
**Ginath**. 
Ginath was a man living during the time of the divided kingdom and the father of Tibni, mentioned in 1 Kings 16:21 (acai). 




**Familial Relationships of Ginath**


* Gender: male
* Offspring: [Tibni](Tibni.md)
