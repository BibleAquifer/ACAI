# Jeiel
**Jeiel**. 
A liturgical musician and gatekeeper for the Ark of the Covenant who lived during the divided kingdom (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Jehiah*. 
Roles include: 
_Priest_. 
Jeiel is also referred to as: 
[Jehiah (Jeiel)](Jehiah.md).
