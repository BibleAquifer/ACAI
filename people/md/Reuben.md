# Reuben
**Reuben**. 
The patriarch Jacob’s oldest son who founded the tribe of Reuben (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Reubenite*. 
Roles include: 
_Leader_, _Matriarch_, _Patriarch_. 
Reuben is also referred to as: 
group:Reuben (Group).
