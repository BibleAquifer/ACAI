# Tattenai
**Tattenai**. 
A Persian ruler who opposed the rebuilding of the Temple at Jerusalem during the time of Ezra the scribe (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Tatnai*. 
Roles include: 
_Governor_, _Official_. 




**Familial Relationships of Tattenai**


* Gender: male
