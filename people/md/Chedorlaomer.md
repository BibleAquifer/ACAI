# Chedorlaomer
**Chedorlaomer**. 
A king of Elam living during the time of Abram (Abraham). After Chedorlaomer captured Abram's nephew Lot, he was defeated by Abram's forces at Hobah (acai). 
Roles include: 
_King_.
