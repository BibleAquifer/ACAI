# Jeziel
**Jeziel**. 
A warrior from the tribe of Benjamin during the time of the divided kingdom, son of Azmaveth and brother of Pelet, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 12:3 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Jezuel*.
