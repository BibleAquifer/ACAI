# Simon
**Simon**. 
Simon was a man living at the time of the New Testament and is first mentioned in John 6:71 as the father of Judas Iscariot (acai). 




**Familial Relationships of Simon**


* Gender: male
* Offspring: [Judas](Judas.2.md)
