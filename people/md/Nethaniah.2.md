# Nethaniah (1 Chronicles 25:2)
**Nethaniah (1 Chronicles 25:2)**. 
Nethaniah, a Levite and son of Asaph, was a temple singer during the time of King David, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 25:2 (acai).
