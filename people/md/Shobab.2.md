# Shobab (1 Chronicles 2:18)
**Shobab (1 Chronicles 2:18)**. 
Shobab, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 2:18, was a man of the tribe of Judah living during the time of Egypt and the wilderness, and the son of Caleb and Jerioth (acai).
