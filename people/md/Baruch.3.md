# Baruch (Nehemiah 11:5)
**Baruch (Nehemiah 11:5)**. 
A man from the tribe of Judah, living at the time of exile and return, son of Col-Hozeh and father of Maaseiah, mentioned in Nehemiah 11:5 (acai).
