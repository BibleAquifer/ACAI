# Tola
**Tola**. 
A son of the patriarch Issachar and father of: Uzzi, Rephaiah, Jeriel, Jahmai, Ibsam, and Shemuel. He was founder of the Tolaite clan (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Tolaite*. 
Roles include: 
_Leader_, _Patriach_. 
Tola is also referred to as: 
group:Tola (Group).
