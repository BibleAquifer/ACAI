# Sabteca
**Sabteca**. 
Sabteca, also known as Sabteka or Sabtechah, son of Cush and descendant of Ham, lived during the time of the patriarchs, mentioned in Genesis 10:7 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Sabtecha*, *Sabtechah*, *Sabteka*.
