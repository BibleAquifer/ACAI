# Puah
**Puah**. 
Son of the patriarch Issachar and clan chief of the Punites (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Phuvah*, *Punite*, *Puvah*. 
Puah is also referred to as: 
group:Punite (Group), [Puvah (Puah)](Puvah.md).
