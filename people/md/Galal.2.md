# Galal (1 Chronicles 9:16)
**Galal (1 Chronicles 9:16)**. 
Galal, a Levite living at the time of exile and return, is the son of Jeduthun and father of Shemaiah, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 9:16 (acai).
