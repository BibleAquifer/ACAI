# Zichri (1 Chronicles 26:25)
**Zichri (1 Chronicles 26:25)**. 
Zichri, also called Zikri, was a Levite during the divided kingdom, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 26:25, and was the son of Joram and the father of Shelomoth (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Zikri*.
