# Uzzi (1 Chronicles 7:7)
**Uzzi (1 Chronicles 7:7)**. 
Uzzi, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 7:7, was a man of the tribe of Benjamin living at the time of Egypt and the wilderness, the son of Bela (acai).
