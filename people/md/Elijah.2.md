# Elijah (1 Chronicles 8:27)
**Elijah (1 Chronicles 8:27)**. 
Elijah, also called Eliah, was a leader from the tribe of Benjamin and the son of Jeroham, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 8:27 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Eliah*.
