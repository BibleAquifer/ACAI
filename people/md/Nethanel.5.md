# Nethanel (1 Chronicles 26:4)
**Nethanel (1 Chronicles 26:4)**. 
Nethanel, also known as Nethaneel, was a Levite and son of Obed-Edom during the time of Israel's united kingdom, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 26:4 (acai).
