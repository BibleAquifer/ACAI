# Rephaiah (1 Chronicles 4:42)
**Rephaiah (1 Chronicles 4:42)**. 
Rephaiah was a man from the tribe of Simeon during the divided kingdom and the son of Ishi, mentioned only in 1 Chronicles 4:42 (acai).
