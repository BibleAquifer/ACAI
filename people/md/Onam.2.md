# Onam (1 Chronicles 2:26)
**Onam (1 Chronicles 2:26)**. 
Onam is a man from the tribe of Judah living during Israel's united kingdom period, first mentioned in 1 Chronicles 2:26, and is the son of Jerahmeel and Atarah (acai).
