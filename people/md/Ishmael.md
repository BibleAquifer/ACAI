# Ishmael
**Ishmael**. 
The patriarch Abraham’s first-born son by Hagar. He was father to twelve sons and half-brother to Isaac (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Ishmaelite*, *Ishmeelite*. 
Roles include: 
_Patriarch_. 
Ishmael is also referred to as: 
group:Ishmael (Group).
