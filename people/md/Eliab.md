# Eliab
**Eliab**. 
Son of Helon, who was the chief of the tribe of Zebulun during the wilderness wandering of the Israelites (acai). 
Roles include: 
_Leader_, _Patriarch_, _Tribal Chief_. 




**Familial Relationships of Eliab**


* Gender: male
* Father: [Helon](Helon.md)
