# Elasah (Jeremiah 29:3)
**Elasah (Jeremiah 29:3)**. 
Elasah, son of Shaphan and brother of Ahikam and Gemariah, was an official during the time of the divided kingdom, mentioned in Jeremiah 29:3 (acai).
