# Bar-jesus
**Bar-jesus**. 
A man living at the time of the New Testament, also called Elymas, mentioned in Acts 13:6 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Elymas*. 
Bar-jesus is also referred to as: 
[Elymas (Bar-jesus)](Elymas.md).
