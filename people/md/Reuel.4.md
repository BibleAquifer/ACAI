# Reuel (1 Chronicles 9:8)
**Reuel (1 Chronicles 9:8)**. 
Reuel, mentioned only in 1 Chronicles 9:8, was a man living at the time of exile and return, the son of Ibnijah and the father of Shephatiah (acai).
