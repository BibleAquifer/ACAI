# Ahimelech (Son of Abiathar)
**Ahimelech (Son of Abiathar)**. 
A priest living during the time of King David (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Abimelech*, *Ahimelek*. 
Roles include: 
_Priest_. 




**Familial Relationships of Ahimelech (Son of Abiathar)**


* Gender: male
* Father: [Abiathar](Abiathar.md)
* Sibling(s): [Jonathan](Jonathan.4.md)
