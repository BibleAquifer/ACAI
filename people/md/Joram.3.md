# Joram
**Joram**. 
A king of Israel during the divided kingdom. He was a son of Ahab by Jezebel (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Jehoram*. 
Roles include: 
_King_. 
Joram is also referred to as: 
[Jehoram (Joram)](Jehoram.3.md).

* Gender: male
