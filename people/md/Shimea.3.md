# Shimea (1 Chronicles 6:39)
**Shimea (1 Chronicles 6:39)**. 
Shimea, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 6:39, was a man from the tribe of Levi living during the divided kingdom and the son of Michael and father of Berechiah (acai).
