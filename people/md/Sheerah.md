# Sheerah
**Sheerah**. 
Sheerah, also known as Sherah, was a woman of the tribe of Ephraim during the time of the divided kingdom, daughter of Beriah and sister of Rephah and Resheph, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 7:24 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Sherah*.
