# Shua
**Shua**. 
Shua, also called Shuah, was a man living during the time of the patriarchs, first mentioned in Genesis 38:2 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Shuah*. 




**Familial Relationships of Shua**


* Gender: male
* Offspring: [Bath-shua](Bath-shua.md)
