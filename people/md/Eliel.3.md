# Eliel (1 Chronicles 8:22)
**Eliel (1 Chronicles 8:22)**. 
Eliel, a man of the tribe of Benjamin and son of Shashak, lived during the time of the divided kingdom and is mentioned in 1 Chronicles 8:22 (acai).
