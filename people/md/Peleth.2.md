# Peleth (1 Chronicles 2:33)
**Peleth (1 Chronicles 2:33)**. 
Peleth was a man of the tribe of Judah during the time of the divided kingdom, son of Jonathan and brother of Zaza, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 2:33 (acai).
