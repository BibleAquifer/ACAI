# Elihu (1 Chronicles 27:18)
**Elihu (1 Chronicles 27:18)**. 
Elihu, the son of Jesse and brother of David, was an officer from the tribe of Judah during Israel's united kingdom, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 27:18 (acai).
