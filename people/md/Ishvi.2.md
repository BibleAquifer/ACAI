# Ishvi (Genesis 46:17)
**Ishvi (Genesis 46:17)**. 
Grandson of the patriarch Jacob and son of Asher. He was founder of the clan of the Ishvites (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Ishuai*, *Ishvite*, *Isui*, *Jesui*. 
Roles include: 
_Leader_, _Patriarch_. 
Ishvi (Genesis 46:17) is also referred to as: 
group:Ishvi (Group).
