# Penuel (1 Chronicles 8:25)
**Penuel (1 Chronicles 8:25)**. 
Penuel was a man of the tribe of Benjamin during the time of the divided kingdom, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 8:25 as the son of Shashak and brother of many siblings (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Peniel*.
