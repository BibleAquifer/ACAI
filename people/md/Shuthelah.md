# Shuthelah
**Shuthelah**. 
A son of the patriarch Ephraim and father of Eran and Bered. He was founder of the clan of the Shuthelahites (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Shuthelahite*. 
Roles include: 
_Leader_, _Patriarch_. 
Shuthelah is also referred to as: 
group:Shuthelah (Group).
