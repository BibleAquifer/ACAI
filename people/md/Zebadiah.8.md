# Zebadiah (Ezra 8:8)
**Zebadiah (Ezra 8:8)**. 
Zebadiah, the son of Michael and a descendant of Shephatiah, was one of the family heads who returned to Jerusalem with Ezra after the exile, mentioned in Ezra 8:8 (acai).
