# Eber
**Eber**. 
A descendant of Noah's son Shem, and ancestor of the Hebrew people. He was a son of Shelah and the father of Peleg and Joktan (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Heber*. 
Roles include: 
_Leader_, _Patriarch_, _Relative of Jesus_.
