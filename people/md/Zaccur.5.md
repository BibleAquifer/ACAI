# Zaccur (Ezra 8:14)
**Zaccur (Ezra 8:14)**. 
Zaccur, also known as Zakkur or Zabbud, was a man living during the time of the exile and return, mentioned in Ezra 8:14 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Zabbud*, *Zakkur*.
