# Meshezabel
**Meshezabel**. 
A man living during the time of exile and return, father of Berechiah, mentioned in Nehemiah 3:4, also known as Meshezabeel (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Meshezabeel*. 




**Familial Relationships of Meshezabel**


* Gender: male
