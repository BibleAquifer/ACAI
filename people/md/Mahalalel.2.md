# Mahalalel (Nehemiah 11:4)
**Mahalalel (Nehemiah 11:4)**. 
A man from the tribe of Judah during the time of exile and return, father of Shephatiah, mentioned in Nehemiah 11:4 (acai).

*Mahalaleel*.
