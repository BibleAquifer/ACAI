# Shallum (1 Chronicles 2:40)
**Shallum (1 Chronicles 2:40)**. 
Shallum, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 2:40, was a man of the tribe of Judah during the divided kingdom, the son of Sismai, and the father of Jekamiah (acai).
