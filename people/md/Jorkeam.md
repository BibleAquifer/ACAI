# Jorkeam
**Jorkeam**. 
A man from the tribe of Judah during the divided kingdom, son of Raham and descendant of Caleb, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 2:44 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Jorkoam*, *Raham*.
