# Gedaliah (1 Chronicles 25:3)
**Gedaliah (1 Chronicles 25:3)**. 
Gedaliah, a temple singer and son of Jeduthun, is mentioned in 1 Chronicles 25:3 during the time of King David in Israel's united kingdom (acai).
