# Jaaziah
**Jaaziah**. 
A Levite man from the tribe of Merari during the time of Egypt and the wilderness, son of Merari and father of Beno, Shoham, Zaccur, and Ibri, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 24:26 (acai).
