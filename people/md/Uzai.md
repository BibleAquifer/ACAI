# Uzai
**Uzai**. 
Uzai, mentioned in Nehemiah 3:25, was a man living at the time of exile and return and the father of Palal (acai). 




**Familial Relationships of Uzai**


* Gender: male
* Offspring: [Palal](Palal.md)
