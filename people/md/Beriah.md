# Beriah
**Beriah**. 
A son of the patriarch Asher, who was a son of the patriarch Jacob. Beriah was the chief of the Beriite clan (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Beriite*. 
Roles include: 
_Leader_. 
Beriah is also referred to as: 
group:Beriah (Group).
