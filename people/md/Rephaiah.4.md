# Rephaiah (1 Chronicles 7:2)
**Rephaiah (1 Chronicles 7:2)**. 
Rephaiah was a man from the tribe of Issachar during the time of Egypt and the wilderness and the son of Tola, mentioned only in 1 Chronicles 7:2 (acai).
