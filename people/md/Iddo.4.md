# Iddo (1 Chronicles 6:21)
**Iddo (1 Chronicles 6:21)**. 
A man of the tribe of Levi, son of Joah, father of Zerah, also called Adaiah, living during the time of the divided kingdom, and first mentioned in 1 Chronicles 6:21 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Adaiah*.
