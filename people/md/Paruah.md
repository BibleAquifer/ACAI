# Paruah
**Paruah**. 
Paruah, mentioned in 1 Kings 4:17, lived during Israel's united kingdom and was the father of Jehoshaphat (acai). 




**Familial Relationships of Paruah**


* Gender: male
* Offspring: [Jehoshaphat (1 Kings 4:17)](Jehoshaphat.2.md)
