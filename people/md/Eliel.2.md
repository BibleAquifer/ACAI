# Eliel (1 Chronicles 8:20)
**Eliel (1 Chronicles 8:20)**. 
Eliel, a man of the tribe of Benjamin and son of Shimei, lived during the time of the divided kingdom and is mentioned in 1 Chronicles 8:20 (acai).
