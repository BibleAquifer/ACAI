# Micaiah (Nehemiah 12:41)
**Micaiah (Nehemiah 12:41)**. 
Micaiah, also known as Michaiah, was a Levite and priest living during the exile and return period, mentioned in Nehemiah 12:41 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Michaiah*.
