# Pedaiah (Nehemiah 11:7)
**Pedaiah (Nehemiah 11:7)**. 
Pedaiah, mentioned in Nehemiah 11:7, lived during the time of exile and return, was the son of Kolaiah, and the father of Joed (acai).
