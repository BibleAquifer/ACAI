# Hashum (Nehemiah 8:4)
**Hashum (Nehemiah 8:4)**. 
Hashum was a man who lived during the time of exile and return and stood to the left of Ezra when he read from the book of the Law, mentioned in Nehemiah 8:4 (acai).
