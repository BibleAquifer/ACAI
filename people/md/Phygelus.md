# Phygelus
**Phygelus**. 
Phygelus, also known as Phygellus, was a man in the New Testament who, along with Hermogenes, turned away from Paul as mentioned in 2 Timothy 1:15 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Phygellus*.
