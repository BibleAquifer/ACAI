# Bigthan
**Bigthan**. 
A eunuch in the court of King Ahasuerus during the exile and return, mentioned in Esther as Bigthan in 2:21 and Bigthana in 6:2 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Bigthana*. 
Roles include: 
_Official_.
