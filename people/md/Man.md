# Man (Adam)
**Man (Adam)**. 
Man living at the time before the Flood (tipnr-short). 
Other forms include: 
*Adam*, *mankind*, *others*. 
Roles include: 
_Patriarch_, _Relative of Jesus_. 
Man (Adam) is also referred to as: 
[Adam](Adam.md). 
Man (Adam) is a subentry. The primary entry is 
[Adam](Adam.md).
