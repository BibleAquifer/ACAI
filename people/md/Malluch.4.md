# Malluch (Nehemiah 10:4)
**Malluch (Nehemiah 10:4)**. 
A man living during the time of exile and return, mentioned in Nehemiah 10:4 and 12:14, also called Mallichi (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Mallichi*, *Malluk*, *Melicu*.
