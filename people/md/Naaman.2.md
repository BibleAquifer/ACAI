# Naaman (Numbers 26:40)
**Naaman (Numbers 26:40)**. 
A son of Bela from the tribe of Benjamin who lived during the time of the Egyptian slavery and the wilderness wandering of the Israelites (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Naamite*. 
Roles include: 
_Leader_.
