# Cheluhi
**Cheluhi**. 
Cheluhi, also known as Chelluh, Keluhi, or Kelahai, was a man living at the time of exile and return, mentioned in Ezra 10:35 as one who married a foreign wife (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Chelluh*, *Kelahai*, *Keluhi*.
