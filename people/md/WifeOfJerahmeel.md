# Wife of Jerahmeel
**Wife of Jerahmeel**. 
The Wife of Jerahmeel is an unnamed woman from the tribe of Judah during Israel's united kingdom, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 2:26 as the mother of Bunah, Ram, Oren, Ozem, and Ahijah (acai).
