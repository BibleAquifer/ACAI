# Caleb
**Caleb**. 
A son of Hezron, an ancestor of King David. He is sometimes called Karmi, Carmi, or Chelubai (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Carmi*, *Chelubai*, *Karmi*. 
Caleb is also referred to as: 
[Carmi (Caleb)](Carmi.md), [Chelubai (Caleb)](Chelubai.md).
