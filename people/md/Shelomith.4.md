# Shelomith (Ezra 8:10)
**Shelomith (Ezra 8:10)**. 
Shelomith, only mentioned in Ezra 8:10, was a woman living during the time of exile and return, and the daughter of Josiphiah (acai). 




**Familial Relationships of Shelomith (Ezra 8:10)**


* Gender: female
