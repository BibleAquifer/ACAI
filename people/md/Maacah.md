# Maacah
**Maacah**. 
A man living at the time of the patriarchs, mentioned only in Genesis 22:24, son of Nahor and Reumah, and brother of Tebah, Gaham, and Tahash (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Maachah*, *Maakah*.
