# Uzziel
**Uzziel**. 
A son of the patriarch Kohath and father of Mishael, Elizaphan, Sithri, Micha, Isshiah. He was uncle of the high priest Aaron (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Uzzielite*. 
Roles include: 
_Leader_, _Patriarch_. 
Uzziel is also referred to as: 
group:Uzziel (Group).
