# Tabitha
**Tabitha**. 
A Christian woman in the New Testament known for her good works and charity. The apostle Peter raised her from the dead (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Dorcas*. 
Roles include: 
_Disciple_. 
Tabitha is also referred to as: 
[Dorcas (Tabitha)](Dorcas.md).
