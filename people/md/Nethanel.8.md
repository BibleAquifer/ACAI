# Nethanel (Ezra 10:22)
**Nethanel (Ezra 10:22)**. 
Nethanel, also known as Nethaneel, was a descendant of Pashhur who lived after the exile and married a foreign wife, mentioned in Ezra 10:22 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Nethaneel*.
