# Japhlet
**Japhlet**. 
A son of Heber from the tribe of Asher who lived before Israel became a kingdom (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Japhletite*. 
Roles include: 
_Leader_, _Patriarch_. 
Japhlet is also referred to as: 
group:Japhlet (Group).
