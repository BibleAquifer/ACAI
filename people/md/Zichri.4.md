# Zichri (1 Chronicles 8:27)
**Zichri (1 Chronicles 8:27)**. 
Zichri, also called Zikri, was a man from the tribe of Benjamin during the divided kingdom, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 8:27, and was the son of Jeroham (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Zikri*.
