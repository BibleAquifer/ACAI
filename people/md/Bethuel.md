# Bethuel
**Bethuel**. 
An ancestor of the patriarch Abraham. Bethuel was a son of Nahor (Abraham's brother) and Milcah, and the father of Laban and Rebekah (the patriarch Isaac's wife) (acai). 
Roles include: 
_Leader_, _Patriarch_.
