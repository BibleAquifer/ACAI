# Jeshua
**Jeshua**. 
Son of Jedaiah and priest living during the Israelite return from exile (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Jeshuah*. 
Roles include: 
_Priest_. 




**Familial Relationships of Jeshua**


* Gender: male
* Father: [Jozadak (Ezra 10:18)](Jozadak.2.md)
