# Og
**Og**. 
A king of Bashan during the time of Israelite conquest of Canaan (acai). 
Roles include: 
_King_, _Warrior_. 




**Familial Relationships of Og**


* Gender: male
* Tribe/Nationality: [Ammonites](../../../groups/md/acai/Ammon.md)
