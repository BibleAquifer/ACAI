# Dan (Patriarch)
**Dan (Patriarch)**. 
One of the twelve sons of the patriarch Jacob by the slave Bilhah. Dan was the founder of the tribe of Dan and father of Hushim (acai). 
Roles include: 
_Leader_, _Patriarch_.
