# Delaiah
**Delaiah**. 
Delaiah, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 3:24, was a descendant of Zerubbabel from the tribe of Judah and lived during the time of the divided kingdom (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Dalaiah*.
