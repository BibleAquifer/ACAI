# Tahan
**Tahan**. 
Tahan, mentioned in Numbers 26:35, was a man of the tribe of Ephraim, son of Ephraim, and lived during the time of Egypt and the wilderness (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Tahanite*. 
Tahan is also referred to as: 
group:Tahan (Group).
