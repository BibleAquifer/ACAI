# Uzzi (1 Chronicles 7:2)
**Uzzi (1 Chronicles 7:2)**. 
Uzzi, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 7:2, was a man of the tribe of Issachar living at the time of Egypt and the wilderness, the son of Tola and father of Izrahiah (acai).
