# Isshiah (1 Chronicles 23:20)
**Isshiah (1 Chronicles 23:20)**. 
A Levite from the time of Egypt and the wilderness, son of Uzziel, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 23:20 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Ishiah*, *Jesiah*.
