# Hillel
**Hillel**. 
Hillel, first mentioned in Judges 12:13, was the father of Abdon, a judge of Israel, and lived before Israel's monarchy (acai). 




**Familial Relationships of Hillel**


* Gender: male
* Offspring: [Abdon](Abdon.md)
