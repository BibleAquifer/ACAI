# Jeuel (Ezra 8:13)
**Jeuel (Ezra 8:13)**. 
A descendant of Adonikam and a family head who returned to Jerusalem with Ezra after the exile, mentioned in Ezra 8:13 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Jeiel*.
