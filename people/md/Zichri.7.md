# Zichri (1 Chronicles 27:16)
**Zichri (1 Chronicles 27:16)**. 
Zichri, also called Zikri, was a man from the tribe of Reuben during the divided kingdom, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 27:16, and was the father of Eliezer (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Zikri*.
