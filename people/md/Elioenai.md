# Elioenai
**Elioenai**. 
Elioenai, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 3:23, was a man from the tribe of Judah during the time of the divided kingdom and a descendant of Zerubbabel (acai). 




**Familial Relationships of Elioenai**


* Gender: male
