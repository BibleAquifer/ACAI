# Chesed
**Chesed**. 
Chesed, also known as Kesed, was a man living at the time of the patriarchs, the son of Nahor and Milcah, and possibly an ancestor of the Chaldeans, mentioned in Genesis 22:22 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Kesed*.
