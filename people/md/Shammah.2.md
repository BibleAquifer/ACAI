# Shammah (Genesis 36:13)
**Shammah (Genesis 36:13)**. 
Shammah was a man living during the time of the patriarchs, son of Reuel and brother of Nahath, Zerah, and Mizzah, mentioned in Genesis 36:13 (acai).
