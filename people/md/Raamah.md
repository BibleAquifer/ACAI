# Raamah
**Raamah**. 
Grandson of the patriach ham, a son of Cush, and father of Sheba and Dedan (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Raama*. 
Roles include: 
_Leader_, _Patriarch_. 




**Familial Relationships of Raamah**


* Gender: male
