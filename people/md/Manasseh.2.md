# Manasseh (King of Judah)
**Manasseh (King of Judah)**. 
A king of Judah during the divided kingdom. He was a son of Hezekiah by Hephzibah, husband of Meshullemeth, and father of Amon (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Manasses*. 
Roles include: 
_King_.
