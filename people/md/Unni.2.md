# Unni (Nehemiah 12:9)
**Unni (Nehemiah 12:9)**. 
Unni, also known as Unno, was a chief living during the time of the exile and return, mentioned only in Nehemiah 12:9 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Unno*.
