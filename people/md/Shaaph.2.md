# Shaaph (1 Chronicles 2:49)
**Shaaph (1 Chronicles 2:49)**. 
Shaaph, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 2:49, was a man from the tribe of Judah, the son of Caleb and Maacah, and had many siblings and half-siblings (acai).
