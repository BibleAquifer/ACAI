# Medan
**Medan**. 
A son of the patriarch Abraham by Keturah (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Mede*, *Medes*, *Median*. 
Roles include: 
_Leader_, _Patriarch_. 
Medan is also referred to as: 
group:Mede (Group).
