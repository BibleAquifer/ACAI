# Malchijah (Ezra 10:31)
**Malchijah (Ezra 10:31)**. 
A man who lived during the time of exile and return, mentioned in Ezra 10:31 for marrying a foreign wife (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Malchiah*, *Malkijah*.
