# Izhar (1 Chronicles 4:7)
**Izhar (1 Chronicles 4:7)**. 
A man from the tribe of Judah during the divided kingdom, son of Ashhur and Helah, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 4:7 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Jezoar*, *Zohar*.
