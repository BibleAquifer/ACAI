# Binnui (Nehemiah 3:24)
**Binnui (Nehemiah 3:24)**. 
A Levite and son of Henadad, who participated in the rebuilding efforts and signed the covenant in Nehemiah's time, mentioned in Nehemiah 3:24 (acai).
