# Zebadiah (2 Chronicles 19:11)
**Zebadiah (2 Chronicles 19:11)**. 
Zebadiah, the son of Ishmael, was a commander from the tribe of Judah during the reign of King Jehoshaphat, mentioned in 2 Chronicles 19:11 (acai).
