# Balak
**Balak**. 
A king of Moab during the time of the Israelite leader Joshua, who attempted to curse and destroy the Israelites as they invaded Canaan (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Balac*. 
Roles include: 
_King_. 




**Familial Relationships of Balak**


* Gender: male
* Tribe/Nationality: [Moabites](../../../groups/md/acai/Moab.md)
* Father: [Zippor](Zippor.md)
