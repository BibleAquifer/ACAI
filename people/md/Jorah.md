# Jorah
**Jorah**. 
A man living during the time of the exile and return, also called Hariph, mentioned in Ezra 2:18 and Nehemiah 7:24 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Hariph*. 
Jorah is also referred to as: 
[Hariph (Jorah)](Hariph.2.md).
