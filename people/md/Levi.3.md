# Levi
**Levi**. 
One of the patriarch Jacob’s sons and founder of the tribe of Levi (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Levite*, *Levitical*. 
Roles include: 
_Patriarch_, _Priest_. 
Levi is also referred to as: 
group:Levi (Group).
