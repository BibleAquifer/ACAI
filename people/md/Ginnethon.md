# Ginnethon
**Ginnethon**. 
Ginnethon, also called Ginnethoi or Ginnetho, was a man living during the time of exile and return, mentioned in Nehemiah 10:6 and 12:4 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Ginnetho*, *Ginnethoi*. 




**Familial Relationships of Ginnethon**


* Gender: male
