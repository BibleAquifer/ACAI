# Zepho
**Zepho**. 
Zepho, also called Zephi, was an Edomite man living during the time of the patriarchs, first mentioned in Genesis 36:11 as the son of Eliphaz and brother of Teman, Omar, Gatam, Kenaz, Amalek, and Korah (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Zephi*.
