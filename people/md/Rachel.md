# Rachel
**Rachel**. 
Daughter of Laban, and the second and favorite wife of Jacob. She was the mother of Joseph and Benjamin. Her slave Bilhah was the mother of Dan and Naphtali (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Rahel*, *she*. 
Roles include: 
_Matriarch_, _Shepherd_.
