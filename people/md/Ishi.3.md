# Ishi (1 Chronicles 4:42)
**Ishi (1 Chronicles 4:42)**. 
A man from the tribe of Simeon living during the time of the divided kingdom, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 4:42 as the father of Pelatiah, Neariah, Rephaiah, and Uzziel (acai).
