# Ela
**Ela**. 
Ela, also known as Elah, was the father of Shimei and an official during the time of King Solomon, mentioned in 1 Kings 4:18 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Elah*. 




**Familial Relationships of Ela**


* Gender: male
* Offspring: [Shimei (1 Kings 1:8)](Shimei.6.md)
