# Gera
**Gera**. 
A man from the tribe of Benjamin and father of Shimei, who lived during the time of Israel’s united kingdom (acai). 




**Familial Relationships of Gera**


* Gender: male
* Offspring: [Shimei](Shimei.5.md)
