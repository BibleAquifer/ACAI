# Shobach
**Shobach**. 
Syrian military commander of Hadadezer who was defeated by King David (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Shobak*, *Shophach*, *Shophak*. 
Roles include: 
_Military Commander_. 
Shobach is also referred to as: 
[Shophach (Shobach)](Shophach.md).
