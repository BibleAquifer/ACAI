# Jephthah
**Jephthah**. 
A son of Gilead and judge of Israel (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Jephthae*. 
Roles include: 
_Judge_. 




**Familial Relationships of Jephthah**


* Gender: male
* Father: [Gilead (Judges 11:1)](Gilead.2.md)
