# Joah
**Joah**. 
A recorder during King Hezekiah’s reign, son of Asaph (acai). 
Roles include: 
_Official_, _Scribe_. 




**Familial Relationships of Joah**


* Gender: male
* Father: [Asaph](Asaph.md)
