# Jeiel (1 Chronicles 11:44)
**Jeiel (1 Chronicles 11:44)**. 
A warrior from Aroer, son of Hotham and brother of Shama, living during the time of King David, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 11:44 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Jehiel*, *Jeuel*.
