# Zemirah
**Zemirah**. 
Zemirah, also known as Zemira, was a man of the tribe of Benjamin who lived during the time of Egypt and the wilderness, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 7:8 as the son of Becher (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Zemira*.
