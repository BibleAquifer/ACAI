# Zimmah (2 Chronicles 29:12)
**Zimmah (2 Chronicles 29:12)**. 
Zimmah was a Levite during the time of the divided kingdom, mentioned in 2 Chronicles 29:12 as the father of Joah and lived during King Hezekiah's reign (acai).
