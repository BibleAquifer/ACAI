# Beriah (1 Chronicles 23:10)
**Beriah (1 Chronicles 23:10)**. 
A man from the tribe of Levi, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 23:10 as the son of Shimei and a descendant of Gershom, living during the time of Egypt and the wilderness (acai).
