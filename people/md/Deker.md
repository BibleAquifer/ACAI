# Deker
**Deker**. 
Deker, mentioned in 1 Kings 4:9, was the father of Ben-deker and lived during the time of Israel's united kingdom under King Solomon (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Dekar*. 




**Familial Relationships of Deker**


* Gender: male
