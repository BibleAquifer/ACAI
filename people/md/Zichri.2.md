# Zichri (1 Chronicles 8:19)
**Zichri (1 Chronicles 8:19)**. 
Zichri, also called Zikri, was a man from the tribe of Benjamin during the divided kingdom, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 8:19, and was the son of Shimei (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Zikri*.
