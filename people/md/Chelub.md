# Chelub
**Chelub**. 
Chelub, also known as Kelub, was a man from the tribe of Judah who lived during the time of the divided kingdom, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 4:11 as the brother of Shuhah and father of Mehir (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Kelub*.
