# Hashabneiah
**Hashabneiah**. 
Hashabneiah was a man living during the time of exile and return, mentioned in Nehemiah 3:10, and father of Hattush (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Hashabniah*. 




**Familial Relationships of Hashabneiah**


* Gender: male
* Offspring: [Hattush (Nehemiah 3:10)](Hattush.3.md)
