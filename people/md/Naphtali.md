# Naphtali
**Naphtali**. 
A son of the patriarch Jacob by Bilhah. He is the founder of the tribe of Naphtali (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Nephtali*. 
Roles include: 
_Leader_, _Patriarch_. 
Naphtali is also referred to as: 
group:Naphtali (Group).
