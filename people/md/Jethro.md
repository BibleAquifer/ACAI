# Jethro
**Jethro**. 
A priest of Midian and father-in-law of Moses (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Raguel*, *Reuel*. 
Roles include: 
_Priest_. 
Jethro is also referred to as: 
[Reuel (Jethro)](Reuel.2.md). 




**Familial Relationships of Jethro**


* Gender: male
* Offspring: [Zipporah](Zipporah.md), [Hobab](Hobab.md)
