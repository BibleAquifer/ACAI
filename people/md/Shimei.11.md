# Shimei (1 Chronicles 8:21)
**Shimei (1 Chronicles 8:21)**. 
Shimei, also known as Shimhi, was a man from the tribe of Benjamin living during the divided kingdom and the father of nine sons including Jakim, Zichri, and Zabdi (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Shimhi*.
