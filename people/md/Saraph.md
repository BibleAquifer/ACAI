# Saraph
**Saraph**. 
Saraph was a man of the tribe of Judah living during the time of Egypt and the wilderness, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 4:22, and was the son of Shelah and brother of Er, Laadah, Jokim, and Joash (acai).
