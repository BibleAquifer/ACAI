# Hodaviah (1 Chronicles 5:24)
**Hodaviah (1 Chronicles 5:24)**. 
Hodaviah was a man of the tribe of Manasseh living during the time of the divided kingdom, mentioned only in 1 Chronicles 5:24, and was a family head (acai).
