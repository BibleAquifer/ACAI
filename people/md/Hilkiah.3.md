# Hilkiah (1 Chronicles 6:45)
**Hilkiah (1 Chronicles 6:45)**. 
Hilkiah, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 6:45, was a man from the tribe of Levi during the time of the divided kingdom, son of Amzi and father of Amaziah (acai).
