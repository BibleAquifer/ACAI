# Azaniah
**Azaniah**. 
A man of the tribe of Levi, living after the exile, father of Jeshua, and only mentioned in Nehemiah 10:9 (acai). 




**Familial Relationships of Azaniah**


* Gender: male
* Offspring: [Jeshua](Jeshua.4.md)
