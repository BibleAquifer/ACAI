# Meshullam (1 Chronicles 5:13)
**Meshullam (1 Chronicles 5:13)**. 
A man from the tribe of Gad during the divided kingdom, son of Abihail and brother to Michael, Sheba, Jorai, Jacan, Zia, and Eber, mentioned only in 1 Chronicles 5:13 (acai).
