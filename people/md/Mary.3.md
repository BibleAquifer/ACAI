# Mary (of Bethany)
**Mary (of Bethany)**. 
A friend of Jesus, and sister of Martha and Lazarus from Bethany (acai). 
Roles include: 
_Disciple_. 




**Familial Relationships of Mary (of Bethany)**


* Gender: female
* Father: [Father of Lazarus](FatherOfLazarus.md)
* Sibling(s): [Martha](Martha.md), [Lazarus](Lazarus.2.md)
