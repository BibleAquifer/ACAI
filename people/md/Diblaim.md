# Diblaim
**Diblaim**. 
Diblaim, mentioned in the time of the divided kingdom, was the father of Gomer, the wife of the prophet Hosea (acai). 




**Familial Relationships of Diblaim**


* Gender: male
* Offspring: [Gomer (Hosea 1:3)](Gomer.2.md)
