# Imnah
**Imnah**. 
A son of the patriarch Asher, who lived during the time of Egyptian slavery and the wilderness wandering of the Israelites. He was founder of the clan of the Imnites (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Imnite*, *Jimna*, *Jimnah*. 
Roles include: 
_Leader_, _Patriarch_.
