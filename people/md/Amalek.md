# Amalek
**Amalek**. 
A man from the Edomites, son of Eliphaz and Timna, and brother of Teman, Omar, Zepho, Gatam, Kenaz, and Korah, first mentioned in Genesis 36:12 (acai). 




**Familial Relationships of Amalek**


* Gender: male
