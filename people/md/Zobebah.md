# Zobebah
**Zobebah**. 
Zobebah, also known as Hazzobebah, was a man from the tribe of Judah during the divided kingdom, son of Koz and brother of Anub and Harum, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 4:8 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Hazzobebah*.
