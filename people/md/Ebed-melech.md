# Ebed-melech
**Ebed-melech**. 
An Ethiopian eunuch who served as an official to King Zedekiah during the divided kingdom of Israel. He advocated for the prophet Jeremiah (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Ebed-Melek*. 
Roles include: 
_Official_. 




**Familial Relationships of Ebed-melech**
