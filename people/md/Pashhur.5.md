# Pashhur (Jeremiah 38:1)
**Pashhur (Jeremiah 38:1)**. 
Pashhur, father of Gedaliah and first mentioned in Jeremiah 38:1, lived during the time of the divided kingdom (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Pashur*.
