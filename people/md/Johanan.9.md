# Johanan (Nehemiah 12:11)
**Johanan (Nehemiah 12:11)**. 
A priest and a son of Joiada and father of Jaddua during the time of the return from Israelite exile (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Jonathan*. 
Roles include: 
_Priest_.
