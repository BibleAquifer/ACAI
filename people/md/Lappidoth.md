# Lappidoth
**Lappidoth**. 
A man mentioned in Judges 4:4 as the husband of Deborah, the prophetess and judge (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Lapidoth*. 




**Familial Relationships of Lappidoth**


* Gender: female
* Partner(s): [Deborah](Deborah.2.md)
