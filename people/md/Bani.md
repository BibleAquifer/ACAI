# Bani
**Bani**. 
A man living at the time of exile and return, also called Binnui, mentioned in Ezra 2:10 and Nehemiah 7:15 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Binnui*. 




**Familial Relationships of Bani**


* Gender: male
