# Shelemiah (Jeremiah 37:13)
**Shelemiah (Jeremiah 37:13)**. 
Shelemiah, mentioned in Jeremiah 37:13, was a man living at the time of the divided kingdom and the son of Hananiah and the father of Irijah (acai).
