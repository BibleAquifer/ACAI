# Iscah
**Iscah**. 
A woman from the time of the patriarchs, daughter of Haran, and sister of Lot and Milcah, mentioned in Genesis 11:29 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Iskah*.

* Gender: female

* Father: [Haran](Haran.md)
