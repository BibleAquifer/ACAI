# Pedaiah (1 Chronicles 3:18)
**Pedaiah (1 Chronicles 3:18)**. 
Pedaiah, from the tribe of Judah mentioned in 1 Chronicles 3:18, lived during the divided kingdom, and was the father of Zerubbabel and Shimei (acai).
