# Mijamin (Ezra 10:25)
**Mijamin (Ezra 10:25)**. 
Mijamin was a man living at the time of exile and return, mentioned in Ezra 10:25 as a descendant of Parosh (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Miamin*.
