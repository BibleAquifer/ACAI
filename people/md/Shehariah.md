# Shehariah
**Shehariah**. 
Shehariah was a man of the tribe of Benjamin during the time of the divided kingdom, son of Jeroham and brother of Shamsherai, Athaliah, Jaareshiah, Elijah, and Zichri, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 8:26 (acai).
