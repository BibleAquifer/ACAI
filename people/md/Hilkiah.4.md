# Hilkiah (1 Chronicles 26:11)
**Hilkiah (1 Chronicles 26:11)**. 
Hilkiah, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 26:11, was the second son of Hosah and a gatekeeper from the tribe of Levi during the time of King David (acai).
