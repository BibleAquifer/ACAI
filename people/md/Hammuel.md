# Hammuel
**Hammuel**. 
Hammuel, also called Hamuel, was a man from the tribe of Simeon during the time of the divided kingdom, only mentioned in 1 Chronicles 4:26, and was the son of Mishma and the father of Zaccur (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Hamuel*.
