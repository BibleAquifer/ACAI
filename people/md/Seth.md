# Seth
**Seth**. 
Third son of Adam and Eve, the younger brother of Abel. He is listed in the family line of Jesus (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Sheth*, *buttock*. 
Roles include: 
_Patriarch_. 
Seth is also referred to as: 
[Sheth (Seth)](Sheth.md).
