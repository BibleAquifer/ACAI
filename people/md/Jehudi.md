# Jehudi
**Jehudi**. 
An official and son of Nethaniah who lived during the time of King Jehoiakim of the divided kingdom (acai). 
Roles include: 
_Official_. 




**Familial Relationships of Jehudi**


* Gender: male
* Father: [Nethaniah (Jeremiah 36:14)](Nethaniah.4.md)
