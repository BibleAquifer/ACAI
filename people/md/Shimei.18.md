# Shimei (Esther 2:5)
**Shimei (Esther 2:5)**. 
Shimei, mentioned in Esther 2:5, was a man living during the time of exile and return, son of Kish and father of Jair (acai). 




**Familial Relationships of Shimei (Esther 2:5)**


* Gender: male
