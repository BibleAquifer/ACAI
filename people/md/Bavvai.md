# Bavvai
**Bavvai**. 
A man, son of Henadad, who helped Nehemiah repair Jerusalem's wall, mentioned in Nehemiah 3:18, also called Binnui or Bevai (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Bavai*, *Bevai*, *Binnui*. 




**Familial Relationships of Bavvai**


* Gender: male
* Father: [Henadad (Nehemiah 3:18)](Henadad.2.md)
