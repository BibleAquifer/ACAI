# Zichri (Nehemiah 11:9)
**Zichri (Nehemiah 11:9)**. 
Zichri, also called Zikri, was a man living during the exile and return, mentioned in Nehemiah 11:9, and was the father of Joel (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Zikri*. 




**Familial Relationships of Zichri (Nehemiah 11:9)**
