# Chenaniah
**Chenaniah**. 
Chenaniah, also known as Kenaniah, was a Levite and chief of the Levites during King David's time, first mentioned in 1 Chronicles 15:22 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Kenaniah*. 
Roles include: 
_Leader_, _Priest_.
