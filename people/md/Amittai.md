# Amittai
**Amittai**. 
A man living during the time of the divided kingdom, known as the father of the prophet Jonah, mentioned in 2 Kings 14:25 (acai). 




**Familial Relationships of Amittai**


* Gender: male
* Offspring: [Jonah](Jonah.2.md)
