# Hagabah
**Hagabah**. 
Hagabah, also known as Hagaba, was a man who lived during the time of exile and return, as mentioned in Ezra 2:45 and Nehemiah 7:48 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Hagaba*. 
Hagabah is also referred to as: 
[Hagaba (Hagabah)](Hagaba.md).
