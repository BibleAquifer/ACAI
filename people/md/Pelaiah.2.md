# Pelaiah (1 Chronicles 3:24)
**Pelaiah (1 Chronicles 3:24)**. 
Pelaiah was a man of the tribe of Judah, the son of Elioenai and brother to Hodaviah, Eliashib, Akkub, Johanan, Delaiah, and Anani, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 3:24 (acai).
