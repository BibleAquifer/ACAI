# Nemuel (Numbers 26:9)
**Nemuel (Numbers 26:9)**. 
Nemuel was a man of the tribe of Reuben living during the time of Egypt and the wilderness, son of Eliab, and brother of Dathan and Abiram, mentioned in Numbers 26:9 (acai).
