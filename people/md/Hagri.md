# Hagri
**Hagri**. 
The father of Mibhar, one of King David's mighty men. Hagri was founder of the Hagrites (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Haggeri*, *Hagrite*. 




**Familial Relationships of Hagri**


* Gender: male
* Offspring: [Mibhar](Mibhar.md)
