# Temah
**Temah**. 
Temah, also known as Thamah or Tamah, was a man living at the time of the exile and return, mentioned in Ezra 2:53 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Tamah*, *Thamah*. 




**Familial Relationships of Temah**


* Gender: male
