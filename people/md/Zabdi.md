# Zabdi
**Zabdi**. 
From the tribe of Judah, a son of Zerah and grandfather of Achan who took the devoted things of God during the Israelite invasion of Ai (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Zimri*. 
Roles include: 
_Leader_. 
Zabdi is also referred to as: 
[Zimri (Zabdi)](Zimri.md).
