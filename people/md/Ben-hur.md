# Ben-hur
**Ben-hur**. 
Man from the time of Israel's united kingdom, mentioned in 1 Kings 4:8, son of Hur, and an official during King Solomon's reign (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Hur*, *son of Hur*. 




**Familial Relationships of Ben-hur**


* Gender: male
* Father: [Hur](Hur.md)
