# Jael
**Jael**. 
The wife of Heber the Kenite who killed Sisera the commander of King Jabin's forces during the time of Israel's judge Deborah (acai). 
Roles include: 
_Warrior_. 




**Familial Relationships of Jael**


* Gender: female
