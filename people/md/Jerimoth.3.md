# Jerimoth (1 Chronicles 23:23)
**Jerimoth (1 Chronicles 23:23)**. 
A Levite during the time of Egypt and the wilderness, son of Mushi and brother of Mahli and Eder, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 23:23 and also called Jeremoth (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Jeremoth*.
