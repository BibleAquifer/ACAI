# Jahzeiah
**Jahzeiah**. 
A man living at the time of the exile and return, son of Tikvah, mentioned in Ezra 10:15 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Jahaziah*. 




**Familial Relationships of Jahzeiah**


* Gender: male
* Father: [Tikvah (Ezra 10:15)](Tikvah.2.md)
