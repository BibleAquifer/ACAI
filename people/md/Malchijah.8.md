# Malchijah (Nehemiah 3:31)
**Malchijah (Nehemiah 3:31)**. 
A man who lived during the time of exile and return, mentioned in Nehemiah 3:31 as a goldsmith who helped repair the wall of Jerusalem (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Malchiah*, *Malkijah*.
