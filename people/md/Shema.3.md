# Shema (1 Chronicles 8:13)
**Shema (1 Chronicles 8:13)**. 
Shema, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 8:13, was a man of the tribe of Benjamin living during the time of the divided kingdom, the son of Elpaal and brother of Eber, Misham, Shemed, Beriah, Zebadiah, Meshullam, Hizki, Heber, Ishmerai, Izliah, and Jobab (acai).
