# Samgar
**Samgar**. 
Samgar, also known as Samgar-nebo or Nebu-sar-sekim, was a man living at the time of the divided kingdom, mentioned only in Jeremiah 39:3 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Samgar, Nebu-sar-sekim*, *Samgar-nebo*.
