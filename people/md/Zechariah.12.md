# Zechariah (2 Chronicles 24:20)
**Zechariah (2 Chronicles 24:20)**. 
Zechariah, also known as Zacharias, was a priest and son of Jehoiada living during the divided kingdom, mentioned in 2 Chronicles 24:20 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Zacharias*. 
Roles include: 
_Prophet_.
