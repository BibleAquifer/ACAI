# Alphaeus
**Alphaeus**. 
A man living during the New Testament, father of the disciple James and perhaps Matthew Levi (acai). 




**Familial Relationships of Alphaeus**


* Gender: male
* Offspring: [James](James.2.md), [Judas](Judas.md)
