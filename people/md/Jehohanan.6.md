# Jehohanan (Nehemiah 6:18)
**Jehohanan (Nehemiah 6:18)**. 
A man called Johanan, an Ammonite, son of Tobiah, married to the daughter of Meshullam, living during the time of exile and return, mentioned in Nehemiah 6:18 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Johanan*.
