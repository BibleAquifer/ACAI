# Cushan-rishathaim
**Cushan-rishathaim**. 
Cushan-rishathaim was a king of Mesopotamia during the time of the judges who enslaved the Israelites. When Othniel the judge defeated him, the Israelites gained freedom for forty years (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Chushan-rishathaim*. 
Roles include: 
_King_.
