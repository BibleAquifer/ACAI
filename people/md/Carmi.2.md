# Carmi
**Carmi**. 
A son of the Reuben, the first-born son of the patriarch Jacob. He is also known as Karmi (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Carmite*, *Karmi*, *Karmite*. 
Roles include: 
_Leader_. 
Carmi is also referred to as: 
group:Carmi (Group).
