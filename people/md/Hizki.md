# Hizki
**Hizki**. 
Hizki, also known as Hezeki, was a man from the tribe of Benjamin during the divided kingdom, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 8:17, and was the son of Elpaal (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Hezeki*.
