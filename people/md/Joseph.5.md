# Joseph (Son of Mary)
**Joseph (Son of Mary)**. 
A man called Joses in the New Testament, brother of James and Salome, first mentioned in Matthew 27:56 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Joses*. 
Joseph (Son of Mary) is also referred to as: 
[Joses (Joseph)](Joses.3.md).
