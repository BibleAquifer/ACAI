# Seraiah (1 Chronicles 4:35)
**Seraiah (1 Chronicles 4:35)**. 
Seraiah, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 4:35, was a man of the tribe of Simeon living during the divided kingdom, the son of Asiel and father of Joshibiah (acai).
