# Shalmai
**Shalmai**. 
Shalmai, also known as Shamlai, was a man living during the time of exile and return, mentioned in Ezra 2:46 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Shamlai*. 
Shalmai is also referred to as: 
[Shamlai (Shalmai)](Shamlai.md).
