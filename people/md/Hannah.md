# Hannah
**Hannah**. 
Wife of Elkanah and mother of Samuel, the prophet and judge (acai). 
Roles include: 
_Matriarch_. 




**Familial Relationships of Hannah**


* Gender: female
* Partner(s): [Elkanah](Elkanah.2.md)
* Offspring: [Samuel](Samuel.md)
