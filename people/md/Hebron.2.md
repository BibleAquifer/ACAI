# Hebron (1 Chronicles 2:42)
**Hebron (1 Chronicles 2:42)**. 
Hebron from 1 Chronicles 2:42 was a man of the tribe of Judah, son of Mareshah, and a descendant of Caleb, living during the divided kingdom (acai).
