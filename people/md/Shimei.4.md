# Shimei
**Shimei**. 
Grandson of Moses, son of Gershon, and father of Jahath, Zizah, Jeush, and Beriah. He was founder of the clan of the Shimeites (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Shimeite*, *Shimi*, *Shimite*. 
Roles include: 
_Leader_, _Patriarch_. 
Shimei is also referred to as: 
group:Shimei (Group).
