# Dodo
**Dodo**. 
Dodo, mentioned in Judges 10:1, was the father of Puah and an ancestor of Tola, a judge of Israel, living before Israel's monarchy (acai). 




**Familial Relationships of Dodo**


* Gender: male
* Offspring: [Puah (Judges 10:1)](Puah.2.md)
