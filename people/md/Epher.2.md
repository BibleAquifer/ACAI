# Epher (1 Chronicles 4:17)
**Epher (1 Chronicles 4:17)**. 
Epher, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 4:17, was a man of the tribe of Judah, son of Ezrah, and brother of Jether, Mered, and Jalon (acai).
