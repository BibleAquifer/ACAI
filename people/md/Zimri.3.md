# Zimri (Numbers 25:14)
**Zimri (Numbers 25:14)**. 
Zimri, son of Salu from the tribe of Simeon, was killed by Phinehas for committing adultery with a Midianite woman, as mentioned in Numbers 25:14 (acai).
