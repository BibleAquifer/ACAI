# Shammah
**Shammah**. 
Shammah, son of Agee, was a man during Israel's united kingdom mentioned in 2 Samuel 23:11 and also a king of Zeboiim during the time of Abraham (acai). 
Roles include: 
_Warrior_. 




**Familial Relationships of Shammah**


* Gender: male
* Father: [Agee](Agee.md)
