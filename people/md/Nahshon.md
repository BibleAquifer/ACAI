# Nahshon
**Nahshon**. 
A son of Amminadab, father of Salmon, and ancestor of King David (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Naashon*, *Naasson*. 
Roles include: 
_Leader_, _Patriarch_. 




**Familial Relationships of Nahshon**


* Gender: male
* Tribe/Nationality: [Tribe of Judah](../../../groups/md/acai/Judah.md)
* Father: [Amminadab](Amminadab.md)
* Offspring: [Salmon](Salmon.md)
* Sibling(s): [Elisheba](Elisheba.md)
