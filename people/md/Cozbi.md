# Cozbi
**Cozbi**. 
Cozbi was a Midianite woman, daughter of Zur, living during the time of Egypt and the wilderness (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Kozbi*. 




**Familial Relationships of Cozbi**


* Gender: female
* Father: [Zur](Zur.md)
