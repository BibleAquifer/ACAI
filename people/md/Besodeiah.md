# Besodeiah
**Besodeiah**. 
A man mentioned in Nehemiah 3:6, who lived during the time of exile and return, and father of Meshullam, a repairer of the wall of Jerusalem (acai). 




**Familial Relationships of Besodeiah**


* Gender: male
