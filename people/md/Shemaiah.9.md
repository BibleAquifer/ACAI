# Shemaiah (1 Chronicles 26:4)
**Shemaiah (1 Chronicles 26:4)**. 
Shemaiah, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 26:4, was a Levite living during Israel's united kingdom, son of Obed-edom, and father of several children including Othni and Rephael (acai).
