# Ashhur
**Ashhur**. 
A man from the tribe of Judah during the time of Egypt and the wilderness, son of Hezron and Abiah, and founder of Tekoa, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 2:24 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Ashur*.
