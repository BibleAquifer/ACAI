# Hasupha
**Hasupha**. 
Hasupha, also known as Hashupha, was a family head and temple servant living after the exile, first mentioned in Ezra 2:43 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Hashupha*. 




**Familial Relationships of Hasupha**


* Gender: male
