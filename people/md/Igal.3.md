# Igal (1 Chronicles 3:22)
**Igal (1 Chronicles 3:22)**. 
A man of the tribe of Judah, descendant of Zerubbabel, living during the divided kingdom, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 3:22 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Igeal*.
