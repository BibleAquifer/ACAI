# Zebulun
**Zebulun**. 
One of the patriarch Jacob’s sons who founded the tribe of Zebulun (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Zabulon*, *Zebulunite*. 
Roles include: 
_Leader_, _Patriarch_. 
Zebulun is also referred to as: 
group:Zebulun (Group).
