# Arod
**Arod**. 
A son of Gad from the tribe of Gad, also called Arod or Arodi, mentioned in Genesis 46:16 and Numbers 26:17 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Arodi*, *Arodite*. 
Arod is also referred to as: 
[Arodi (Arod)](Arodi.md), [Arodite (Arod)](Arodite.md). 




**Familial Relationships of Arod**
