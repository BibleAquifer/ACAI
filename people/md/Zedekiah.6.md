# Zedekiah (Jeremiah 29:21)
**Zedekiah (Jeremiah 29:21)**. 
Zedekiah, son of Maaseiah, was a man living during the time of the divided kingdom, mentioned in Jeremiah 29:21 (acai). 
Roles include: 
_Prophet_.
