# Micah (1 Chronicles 5:5)
**Micah (1 Chronicles 5:5)**. 
Micah, a man of the tribe of Reuben during the divided kingdom, is mentioned in 1 Chronicles 5:5 as the son of Shimei and father of Reaiah (acai).
