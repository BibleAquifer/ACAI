# Pahath-moab
**Pahath-moab**. 
The father of Jeshua and Joab who lived during the time of Israelite exile and return (acai). 
Roles include: 
_Leader_. 




**Familial Relationships of Pahath-moab**


* Gender: male
* Offspring: [Jeshua (Ezra 2:6)](Jeshua.3.md), [Joab (Ezra 2:6)](Joab.3.md)
