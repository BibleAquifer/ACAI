# Omri (1 Chronicles 9:4)
**Omri (1 Chronicles 9:4)**. 
Omri, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 9:4, is a man of the tribe of Judah living during the time of exile and return, who is the son of Imri and father of Ammihud (acai).
