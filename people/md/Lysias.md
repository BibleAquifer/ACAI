# Lysias
**Lysias**. 
A Roman commander in the New Testament, also known as Claudius, who played a role in the events involving Paul (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Claudius*, *Claudius Lysias*. 
Roles include: 
_Official_. 
Lysias is also referred to as:
