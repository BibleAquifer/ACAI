# Pallu
**Pallu**. 
A son of Reuben and father of Eliab (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Palluite*, *Phallu*. 
Roles include: 
_Leader_, _Patriarch_. 
Pallu is also referred to as: 
group:Pallu (Group).
