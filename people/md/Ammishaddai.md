# Ammishaddai
**Ammishaddai**. 
Father of Ahiezer, the chief of the tribe of Dan at the time of Israel’s wilderness wandering (acai). 
Roles include: 
_Patriarch_, _Tribal Chief_. 




**Familial Relationships of Ammishaddai**


* Gender: male
* Offspring: [Ahiezer](Ahiezer.md)
