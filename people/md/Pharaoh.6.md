# Pharaoh
**Pharaoh**. 
A ruler of the Egyptians during the time of Moses's ministry in Egypt (acai). 
Roles include: 
_King_, _Ruler_. 




**Familial Relationships of Pharaoh**


* Gender: male
* Tribe/Nationality: [Egyptians](../../../groups/md/acai/Egypt.md)
