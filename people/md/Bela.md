# Bela
**Bela**. 
A son of the patriach Benjamin and chief of the clan of the Belaites. He was father of Addar and Naaman (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Belah*, *Belaite*. 
Roles include: 
_Leader_, _Patriarch_. 
Bela is also referred to as: 
group:Bela (Group).
