# Hepher (1 Chronicles 4:6)
**Hepher (1 Chronicles 4:6)**. 
Hepher, a man from the tribe of Judah during the time of the divided kingdom, is mentioned only in 1 Chronicles 4:6 as the son of Ashhur and Naarah (acai).
