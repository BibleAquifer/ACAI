# Hakkatan
**Hakkatan**. 
Hakkatan was a man living during the time of exile and return, only mentioned in Ezra 8:12, and the father of Johanan (acai). 




**Familial Relationships of Hakkatan**


* Gender: male
* Offspring: [Johanan (Ezra 8:12)](Johanan.8.md)
