# Barzillai (Ezra 2:61)
**Barzillai (Ezra 2:61)**. 
A descendant of priests from the tribe of Levi who adopted the name Barzillai after marrying a descendant of Barzillai from Rogelim (acai). 
Roles include: 
_Priest_.
