# Nahash
**Nahash**. 
An Ammonite king who lived during the time of Israel’s king Saul (acai). 
Roles include: 
_King_. 




**Familial Relationships of Nahash**


* Gender: male
* Tribe/Nationality: [Ammonites](../../../groups/md/acai/Ammon.md)
