# Col-hozeh
**Col-hozeh**. 
Col-hozeh was a man after the exile, mentioned in Nehemiah, and father of Shallun (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Kol-Hozeh*. 




**Familial Relationships of Col-hozeh**


* Gender: male
* Offspring: [Shallum (Nehemiah 3:15)](Shallum.15.md)
