# Simon
**Simon**. 
One of the twelve apostles of Jesus (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Simon Zelotes*, *Zealot*, *Zelotes*. 
Roles include: 
_Apostle_. 
Simon is also referred to as: 
[Zealot (Simon)](Zealot.md).
