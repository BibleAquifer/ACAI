# Dodavahu
**Dodavahu**. 
Dodavahu, also called Dodavah, was the father of the prophet Eliezer and lived during the time of the divided kingdom, mentioned in 2 Chronicles 20:37 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Dodavah*. 




**Familial Relationships of Dodavahu**


* Gender: male
