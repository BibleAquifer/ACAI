# Eliphelehu
**Eliphelehu**. 
Eliphelehu, also known as Elipheleh, was a Levite singer from the tribe of Levi living during the time of King David, first mentioned in 1 Chronicles 15:18 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Elipheleh*.
