# Nethanel (1 Chronicles 24:6)
**Nethanel (1 Chronicles 24:6)**. 
Nethanel, also known as Nethaneel, was a Levitical father of Shemaiah during the time of King David, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 24:6 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Nethaneel*.
