# Machir
**Machir**. 
Grandson of the patriarch Joseph, son of Manasseh, and father of Gilead (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Machirite*, *Makir*, *Makirite*. 
Roles include: 
_Leader_, _Patriarch_. 
Machir is also referred to as: 
group:Machir (Group).
