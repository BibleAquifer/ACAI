# Eliashib (1 Chronicles 24:12)
**Eliashib (1 Chronicles 24:12)**. 
Eliashib was a family head in charge of a division of priests from the tribe of Levi during the time of the divided kingdom, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 24:12 (acai).
