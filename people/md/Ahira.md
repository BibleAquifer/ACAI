# Ahira
**Ahira**. 
The chief of the tribe of Naphtali during the time of Moses. He was a son of Enan (acai). 
Roles include: 
_Patriarch_, _Tribal Chief_. 




**Familial Relationships of Ahira**


* Gender: male
* Father: [Enan](Enan.md)
