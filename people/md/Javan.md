# Javan
**Javan**. 
A grandson of the patriarch Noah, a son of Japheth, and ancestor of the Greeks (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Gentile*, *Greek*, *Greek, Gentile*. 
Roles include: 
_Patriarch_. 
Javan is also referred to as: 
group:Greek (Group).
