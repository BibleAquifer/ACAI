# Seraiah (1 Chronicles 4:13)
**Seraiah (1 Chronicles 4:13)**. 
Seraiah, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 4:13, was a man of the tribe of Judah living before Israel's Monarchy, the son of Kenaz, brother of Othniel, and father of Joab (acai).
