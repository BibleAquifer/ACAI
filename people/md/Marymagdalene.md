# Mary Magdalene
**Mary Magdalene**. 
A disciple of Jesus who was one of the myrrhbearing women (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Mary*. 
Roles include: 
_Disciple_. 




**Familial Relationships of Mary Magdalene**


* Gender: female
