# Mahalath
**Mahalath**. 
A daughter of the patriarch Ishmael and wife of Esau (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Basemath*, *Bashemath*. 
Roles include: 
_Concubine_, _Matriarch_. 
Mahalath is also referred to as: 
[Basemath (Mahalath)](Basemath.3.md).
