# Eliphelet (2 Samuel 23:34)
**Eliphelet (2 Samuel 23:34)**. 
Eliphelet, mentioned in 2 Samuel 23:34, was a warrior and the son of Ahasbai, who lived during the time of Israel's united kingdom under King David (acai).
