# Sisera
**Sisera**. 
Leader of the army of the Canaanite king Jabin who was killed by the woman Jael during the time of the judge and prophetess Deborah (acai). 
Roles include: 
_Military Commander_. 




**Familial Relationships of Sisera**


* Gender: male
