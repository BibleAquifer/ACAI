# Zerah
**Zerah**. 
A son of the patriarch Simeon and brother of Jemuel, Jamin, Ohad, Jachin, Zohar, and Shaul (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Zerahite*, *Zohar*. 
Roles include: 
_Leader_, _Patriarch_. 
Zerah is also referred to as: 
group:Zerah (Group), [Zohar (Zerah)](Zohar.2.md).
