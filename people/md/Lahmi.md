# Lahmi
**Lahmi**. 
A giant living during the time of Israel's united kingdom, mentioned in 2 Samuel 21:19 as the brother of Goliath and son of Rapha (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*[brother of] Goliath*. 
Lahmi is also referred to as: 
[Bethlehemite (Lahmi)](Bethlehemite.md).
