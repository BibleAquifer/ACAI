# Meshech (Genesis 10:2)
**Meshech (Genesis 10:2)**. 
A son of Japheth and brother of Gomer, Magog, Madai, Javan, Tubal, and Tiras, mentioned in Genesis 10:2 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Meshek*.
