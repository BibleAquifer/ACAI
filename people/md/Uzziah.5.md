# Uzziah (Nehemiah 11:4)
**Uzziah (Nehemiah 11:4)**. 
Uzziah, mentioned in Nehemiah 11:4, was a man living during the time of exile and return, son of Zechariah and father of Athaiah (acai). 




**Familial Relationships of Uzziah (Nehemiah 11:4)**
