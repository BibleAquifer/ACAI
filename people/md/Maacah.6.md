# Maacah (1 Chronicles 8:29)
**Maacah (1 Chronicles 8:29)**. 
A woman from the tribe of Benjamin during the united kingdom of Israel, married to Abiel, and mother of Kish, Ner, Abdon, Zur, Baal, Nadab, Gedor, Ahio, Zecher, and Mikloth (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Maachah*, *Maakah*.
