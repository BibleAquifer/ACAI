# Shomer (1 Chronicles 7:32)
**Shomer (1 Chronicles 7:32)**. 
Shomer, also called Shamer or Shemer, was a man of the tribe of Asher living before Israel's Monarchy, first mentioned in 1 Chronicles 7:32 (acai).
