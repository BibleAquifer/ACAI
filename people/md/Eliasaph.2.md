# Eliasaph (Numbers 3:24)
**Eliasaph (Numbers 3:24)**. 
Eliasaph, son of Lael, was a leader of the Gershonite family from the tribe of Levi during the time of Egypt and the wilderness, mentioned in Numbers 3:24 (acai).
