# Meraioth
**Meraioth**. 
A son of Zerahiah and father of Amariah. He was a priest during the time of the divided kingdom (acai). 
Roles include: 
_Priest_. 




**Familial Relationships of Meraioth**


* Gender: male
* Tribe/Nationality: [Tribe of Levi](../../../groups/md/acai/Levi.md)
* Father: [Zerahiah](Zerahiah.md)
* Offspring: [Amariah](Amariah.md)
