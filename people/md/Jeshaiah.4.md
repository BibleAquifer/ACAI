# Jeshaiah (Ezra 8:7)
**Jeshaiah (Ezra 8:7)**. 
A man who lived during the time of exile and return, son of Athaliah and descendant of Elam, who accompanied Ezra back to Jerusalem as mentioned in Ezra 8:7 (acai).
