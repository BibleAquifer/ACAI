# Christ (Jesus)
**Christ (Jesus)**. 
Man living at the time of the New Testament (tipnr-short). 
Other forms include: 
*Christ Jesus*, *Jesus*. 
Roles include: 
_God_, _Messiah_. 
Christ (Jesus) is also referred to as: 
[Jesus](Jesus.2.md). 
Christ (Jesus) is a subentry. The primary entry is 
[Jesus](Jesus.2.md).
