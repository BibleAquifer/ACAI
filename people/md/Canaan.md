# Canaan (Son of Ham)
**Canaan (Son of Ham)**. 
The youngest son of Ham, and a grandson of Noah. Canaan is thought to be the ancestor of the Canaanites (acai). 
Roles include: 
_Leader_, _Patriarchl_.
