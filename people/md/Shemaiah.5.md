# Shemaiah (1 Chronicles 9:14)
**Shemaiah (1 Chronicles 9:14)**. 
Shemaiah, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 9:14, was a man of the tribe of Levi living during the time of exile and return, and he was the son of Hasshub (acai).
