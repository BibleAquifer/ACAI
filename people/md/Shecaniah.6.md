# Shecaniah (Ezra 8:5)
**Shecaniah (Ezra 8:5)**. 
Shecaniah, also known as Shekaniah or Shechaniah, was a man living during the exile and return, mentioned only in Ezra 8:5 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Shechaniah*, *Shekaniah*.
