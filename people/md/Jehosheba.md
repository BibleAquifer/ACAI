# Jehosheba
**Jehosheba**. 
A woman from the tribe of Judah during the divided kingdom, daughter of Jehoram and Athaliah, sister of Ahaziah, and wife of Jehoiada, also called Jehoshabeath, mentioned in 2 Kings 11:2 and 2 Chronicles 22:11 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Jehoshabeath*. 
Jehosheba is also referred to as: 
[Jehoshabeath (Jehosheba)](Jehoshabeath.md).
