# Nergal-shar-ezer (Jeremiah 39:3b)
**Nergal-shar-ezer (Jeremiah 39:3b)**. 
Nergal-shar-ezer, also known as Nergal-sar-ezer, was a Babylonian official during the time of the divided kingdom and King Nebuchadnezzar, mentioned in Jeremiah 39:3b (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Nergal-sar-ezer*.
