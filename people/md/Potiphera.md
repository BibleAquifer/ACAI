# Potiphera
**Potiphera**. 
Potiphera, an Egyptian priest during the time of the patriarchs and father of Asenath, is first mentioned in Genesis 41:45 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Poti-pherah*. 




**Familial Relationships of Potiphera**


* Gender: male
* Offspring: [Asenath](Asenath.md)
