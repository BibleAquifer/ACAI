# Amaziah (1 Chronicles 6:45)
**Amaziah (1 Chronicles 6:45)**. 
A man of the tribe of Levi living at the time of the divided kingdom, mentioned only in 1 Chronicles 6:45, the son of Hilkiah, and the father of Hashabiah (acai).
