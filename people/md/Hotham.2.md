# Hotham (1 Chronicles 11:44)
**Hotham (1 Chronicles 11:44)**. 
Hotham, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 11:44, was the father of Jeiel and Shama, and lived during the time of King David (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Hothan*.
