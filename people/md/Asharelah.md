# Asharelah
**Asharelah**. 
A Levite from the divided kingdom, son of Asaph, also called Jesharelah, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 25:2 and 1 Chronicles 25:14 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Asarelah*, *Jesharelah*. 
Asharelah is also referred to as: 
[Jesharelah (Asharelah)](Jesharelah.md).
