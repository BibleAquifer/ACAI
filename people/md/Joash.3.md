# Joash
**Joash**. 
King of Judah during the divided kingdom, and a son of Ahaziah and Zibiah (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Jehoash*. 
Roles include: 
_King_. 
Joash is also referred to as: 
[Jehoash (Joash)](Jehoash.md).
