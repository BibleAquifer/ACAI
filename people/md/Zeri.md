# Zeri
**Zeri**. 
Zeri, also called Izri, was a Levite living during Israel's united kingdom, first mentioned in 1 Chronicles 25:3 as the son of Jeduthun (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Izri*. 
Zeri is also referred to as: 
[Izri (Zeri)](Izri.md).
