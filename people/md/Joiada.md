# Joiada
**Joiada**. 
A man, son of Paseah, who helped repair the wall of Jerusalem after the exile, mentioned in Nehemiah (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Jehoiada*. 




**Familial Relationships of Joiada**


* Gender: male
* Father: [Paseah (Nehemiah 3:6)](Paseah.3.md)
