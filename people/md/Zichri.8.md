# Zichri (2 Chronicles 17:16)
**Zichri (2 Chronicles 17:16)**. 
Zichri, also called Zikri, was a man during the divided kingdom, mentioned in 2 Chronicles 17:16, and was the father of Amasiah (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Zikri*.
