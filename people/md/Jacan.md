# Jacan
**Jacan**. 
A man of the tribe of Gad during the divided kingdom, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 5:13, son of Abihail and brother to Michael, Meshullam, Sheba, Jorai, Zia, and Eber (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Jachan*, *Jakan*.
