# Zechariah (2 Kings 18:2)
**Zechariah (2 Kings 18:2)**. 
Zechariah, mentioned in 2 Kings 18:2, was a man living during the divided kingdom and the father of Abi (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Zachariah*. 




**Familial Relationships of Zechariah (2 Kings 18:2)**


* Gender: male
