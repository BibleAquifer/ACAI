# Zillethai
**Zillethai**. 
Zillethai was a man from the tribe of Benjamin living during the time of the divided kingdom, mentioned only in 1 Chronicles 8:20 as the son of Shimei and brother to several siblings (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Zilthai*.
