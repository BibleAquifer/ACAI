# Enan
**Enan**. 
Father of Ahira, who was chief of the tribe of Napthali during the time of the wilderness wandering of the Israelites (acai). 
Roles include: 
_Leader_, _Patriarch_. 




**Familial Relationships of Enan**


* Gender: male
* Offspring: [Ahira](Ahira.md)
