# Abihail (Son of Huri)
**Abihail (Son of Huri)**. 
The son of Huri from the tribe of Gad and father of Michael, Meshullam, Sheba, Jorai, Jacan, Zia, and Eber, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 5:14 (acai).
