# Zelophehad
**Zelophehad**. 
Zelophehad was a man from the tribe of Manasseh who lived during the time of Egypt and the wilderness. He fathered five daughters: Mahlah, Noah, Hoglah, Milcah, and Tirzah (acai). 
Roles include: 
_Leader_.
