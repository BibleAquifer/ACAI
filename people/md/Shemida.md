# Shemida
**Shemida**. 
A clan leader of the tribe of Manasseh who lived during the time of the Egyptian slavery and the wilderness wandering of the Israelites (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Shemidaite*. 
Roles include: 
_Leader_. 
Shemida is also referred to as: 
group:Shemida (Group).
