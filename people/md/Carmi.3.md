# Carmi (Joshua  7:1)
**Carmi (Joshua  7:1)**. 
Carmi (Joshua 7:1), also known as Karmi, was a man from the tribe of Judah, father of Achan, and first mentioned in Joshua 7:1 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Karmi*. 




**Familial Relationships of Carmi (Joshua  7:1)**
