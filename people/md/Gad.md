# Gad (Patriarch)
**Gad (Patriarch)**. 
One of the twelve sons of the patriarch Jacob by the slave Zilpah, and founder of the tribe of Gad (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Gadite*. 
Roles include: 
_Leader_, _Patriarch_. 
Gad (Patriarch) is also referred to as: 
group:Gad (Group).
