# Abimelech (King of Gerar)
**Abimelech (King of Gerar)**. 
King of Gerar living at the time of the Patriarchs (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Abimelek*. 
Roles include: 
_King_. 




**Familial Relationships of Abimelech (King of Gerar)**


* Gender: male
* Tribe/Nationality: [Canaanites](../../../groups/md/acai/Canaan.md)
