# Shemaiah (2 Chronicles 29:14)
**Shemaiah (2 Chronicles 29:14)**. 
Shemaiah, mentioned in 2 Chronicles 29:14, was a Levite living during the time of the divided kingdom, the son of Jeduthun, and the brother of Uzziel (acai).
