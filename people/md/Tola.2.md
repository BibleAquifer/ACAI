# Elon (Judges 12:11)
**Elon (Judges 12:11)**. 
Elon, also known as Tola, was a judge living before Israel's Monarchy, son of Puah, mentioned in Judges 10:1 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Tola*. 
Roles include: 
_Judge_. 




**Familial Relationships of Elon (Judges 12:11)**


* Gender: male
* Father: [Puah (Judges 10:1)](Puah.2.md)
