# Nebu-sar-sekim
**Nebu-sar-sekim**. 
Nebu-sar-sekim, also known as Sarsechim or Nebo-Sarsekim, was a man living during the time of the divided kingdom, mentioned in Jeremiah 39:3 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Nebo-Sarsekim*, *Samgar*, *Samgar, Nebu-sar-sekim*, *Sarsechim*.
