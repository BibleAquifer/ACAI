# Meshullam (1 Chronicles 3:19)
**Meshullam (1 Chronicles 3:19)**. 
A man of the tribe of Judah living during the divided kingdom, mentioned only in 1 Chronicles 3:19, son of Zerubbabel and brother of several siblings (acai).
