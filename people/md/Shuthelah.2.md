# Shuthelah (1 Chronicles 7:21)
**Shuthelah (1 Chronicles 7:21)**. 
Shuthelah was a man from the tribe of Ephraim living during the time of the divided kingdom, mentioned only in 1 Chronicles 7:21 as a son of Zabad (acai).
