# Jeshua
**Jeshua**. 
A son of Azaniah and Levite living after the Israelite return from exile (acai). 
Roles include: 
_Priest_. 




**Familial Relationships of Jeshua**


* Gender: male
* Father: [Azaniah](Azaniah.md)
