# Felix
**Felix**. 
A governor of Judaea and husband of Drusilla, who conducted a trial of the apostle Paul and kept him under house arrest at Caesarea (acai). 
Roles include: 
_Governor_. 




**Familial Relationships of Felix**


* Gender: male
* Partner(s): [Drusilla](Drusilla.md)
