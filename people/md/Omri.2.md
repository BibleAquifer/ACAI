# Omri (1 Chronicles 7:8)
**Omri (1 Chronicles 7:8)**. 
Omri, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 7:8, is a man of the tribe of Benjamin living at the time of the exodus from Egypt, who is the son of Becher and has many brothers (acai).
