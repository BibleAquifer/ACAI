# Tarshish (1 Chronicles 7:10)
**Tarshish (1 Chronicles 7:10)**. 
Tarshish, also known as Tharshish, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 7:10, was a man from the tribe of Benjamin and the son of Bilhan during the time of the divided kingdom (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Tharshish*.
