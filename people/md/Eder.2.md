# Eder (1 Chronicles 23:23)
**Eder (1 Chronicles 23:23)**. 
Eder was a Levite man living during the time of Egypt and the wilderness, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 23:23 as the son of Mushi and brother of Mahli and Jerimoth (acai).
