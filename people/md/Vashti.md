# Vashti
**Vashti**. 
The queen and wife of King Ahasuerus (Xerxes) of Persia who was replaced by Esther (acai). 
Roles include: 
_Queen_. 




**Familial Relationships of Vashti**


* Gender: female
* Partner(s): [Ahasuerus](Ahasuerus.md)
