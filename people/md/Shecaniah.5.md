# Shecaniah (Ezra 8:3)
**Shecaniah (Ezra 8:3)**. 
Shecaniah, also known as Shekaniah or Shechaniah, was a man from the tribe of Levi living during the exile and return, mentioned only in Ezra 8:3 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Shechaniah*, *Shekaniah*.
