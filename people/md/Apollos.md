# Apollos
**Apollos**. 
A Christian teacher from Alexandria in Egypt, who worked in Ephesus and Corinth (acai). 
Roles include: 
_Evangelist_, _Missionary_. 




**Familial Relationships of Apollos**


* Gender: male
