# Judas (Acts 15:22)
**Judas (Acts 15:22)**. 
A New Testament leader and prophet (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Barsabas*, *Barsabbas*, *Judas Barsabas*. 
Roles include: 
_Disciple_, _Prophet_. 
Judas (Acts 15:22) is also referred to as: 
[Barsabbas (Judas)](Barsabbas.2.md).
