# Anak
**Anak**. 
Ancestor of a race of giants in Canaan. He was the son of Arba (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Anakim*. 
Roles include: 
_Patriarch_. 
Anak is also referred to as: 
group:Anak (Group). 




**Familial Relationships of Anak**


* Gender: male
* Offspring: [Ahiman](Ahiman.md), [Sheshai](Sheshai.md), [Talmai](Talmai.md)
