# John
**John**. 
The father of the apostles Peter and Andrew (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Jona*, *Jonah*, *Jonas*. 
Roles include: 
_Disciple_. 
John is also referred to as: 
[Jonah (John)](Jonah.md). 




**Familial Relationships of John**


* Gender: male
* Offspring: [Peter](Peter.md), [Andrew](Andrew.md)
