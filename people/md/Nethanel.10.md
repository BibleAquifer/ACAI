# Nethanel (Nehemiah 12:36)
**Nethanel (Nehemiah 12:36)**. 
Nethanel, also known as Nethaneel, was a Levitical priest living during the time of the exile and return, mentioned in Nehemiah 12:36 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Nethaneel*.
