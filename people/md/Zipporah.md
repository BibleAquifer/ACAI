# Zipporah
**Zipporah**. 
Zipporah was the wife of Moses during the time of Egypt and wilderness, daughter of Jethro, and mother of Gershom and Eliezer, mentioned in Exodus 2:21 (acai). 
Roles include: 
_Matriarch_. 




**Familial Relationships of Zipporah**


* Gender: female
* Father: [Jethro](Jethro.md)
* Partner(s): [Moses](Moses.md)
* Offspring: [Gershom](Gershom.md), [Eliezer](Eliezer.3.md)
* Sibling(s): [Eliasaph](Eliasaph.md), [Hobab](Hobab.md)
