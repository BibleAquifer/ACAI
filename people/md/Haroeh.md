# Haroeh
**Haroeh**. 
Haroeh, also called Reaiah, was a man from the tribe of Judah living during the divided kingdom, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 2:52 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Lahad*, *Reaiah*. 
Haroeh is also referred to as: 
[Reaiah (Haroeh)](Reaiah.md).
