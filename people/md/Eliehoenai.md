# Eliehoenai
**Eliehoenai**. 
Eliehoenai, also known as Elioenai, was a gatekeeper and son of Meshelemiah, living during the time of the divided kingdom and is mentioned in 1 Chronicles 26:3 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Elioenai*.
