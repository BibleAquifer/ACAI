# Maacah (1 Chronicles 2:48)
**Maacah (1 Chronicles 2:48)**. 
A woman from the tribe of Judah during the divided kingdom, married to Caleb, and mother of Sheber, Tirhanah, Shaaph, and Sheva (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Maachah*, *Maakah*.
