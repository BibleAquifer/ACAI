# Shemaiah (1 Chronicles 5:4)
**Shemaiah (1 Chronicles 5:4)**. 
Shemaiah, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 5:4, was a man of the tribe of Reuben living during the time of the divided kingdom, the son of Joel, and the father of Gog (acai).
