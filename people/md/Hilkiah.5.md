# Hilkiah (Nehemiah 8:4)
**Hilkiah (Nehemiah 8:4)**. 
Hilkiah, mentioned in Nehemiah 8:4, was a man who stood to the right of Ezra when he read from the Book of the Law during the time of exile and return (acai).
