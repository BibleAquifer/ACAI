# Eliehoenai (Ezra 8:4)
**Eliehoenai (Ezra 8:4)**. 
Eliehoenai, also known as Elihoenai, was a man from the clan of Pahath-Moab and son of Zerahiah, living during the time of exile and return and is mentioned in Ezra 8:4 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Elihoenai*.
