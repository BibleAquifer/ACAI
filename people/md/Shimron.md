# Shimron
**Shimron**. 
A son of the patriarch Issachar. He was founder of the clam of the Shimronites (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Shimronite*. 
Roles include: 
_Leader_, _Patriarch_. 
Shimron is also referred to as: 
group:Shimron (Group).
