# Huppim (1 Chronicles 7:12)
**Huppim (1 Chronicles 7:12)**. 
Huppim, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 7:12, was a son of Ir from the tribe of Benjamin living during the time of Egypt and the wilderness (acai).
