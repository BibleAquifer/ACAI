# Eber (1 Chronicles 8:12)
**Eber (1 Chronicles 8:12)**. 
Eber, from the tribe of Benjamin during the divided kingdom, was the son of Elpaal and is mentioned in 1 Chronicles 8:12 alongside his eleven siblings (acai).
