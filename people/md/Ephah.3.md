# Ephah (1 Chronicles 2:47)
**Ephah (1 Chronicles 2:47)**. 
Ephah was a man of the tribe of Judah, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 2:47 as the son of Jahdai and Caleb, and brother of Regem, Jotham, Geshan, Pelet, and Shaaph (acai).
