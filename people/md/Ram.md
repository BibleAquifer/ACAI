# Ram
**Ram**. 
A man from the tribe of Judah. He is listed in the famiy line of Jesus (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Admin*, *Aram*. 
Roles include: 
_Patriarch_, _Relative of Jesus_. 
Ram is also referred to as: 
[Admin (Ram)](Admin.md), [Arni (Ram)](Arni.md).
