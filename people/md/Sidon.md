# Sidon
**Sidon**. 
Sidon, also known as Zidon, was a Canaanite man living at the time of the patriarchs, first mentioned in Genesis 10:15 as the son of Canaan and the brother of various tribes (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Zidon*.
