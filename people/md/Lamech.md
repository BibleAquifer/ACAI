# Lamech
**Lamech**. 
A son of Methushael, descendant of Adam's son Cain, and father of Jabal, Jubal, Tubal-cain, and Naamah (acai). 
Roles include: 
_Leader_, _Patriarch_. 




**Familial Relationships of Lamech**


* Gender: male
