# Tahath (1 Chronicles 7:20a)
**Tahath (1 Chronicles 7:20a)**. 
Tahath, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 7:20, was a man of the tribe of Ephraim, son of Bered, and father of Eleadah, living during the divided kingdom period (acai).
