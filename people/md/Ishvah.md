# Ishvah
**Ishvah**. 
A member of the tribe of Asher and son of Asher, who lived during the time of Egypt and the wilderness, mentioned in Genesis 46:17 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Ishuah*, *Isuah*. 




**Familial Relationships of Ishvah**


* Gender: male
