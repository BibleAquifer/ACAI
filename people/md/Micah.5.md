# Micah (1 Chronicles 23:20)
**Micah (1 Chronicles 23:20)**. 
A son of Uzziel and father of Shamir from the tribe of Levi who lived during the time of the Egyptian slavery and the wilderness wandering of Israel (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Michah*. 
Roles include: 
_Priest_.
