# Michael (1 Chronicles 6:40)
**Michael (1 Chronicles 6:40)**. 
Michael was a man from the tribe of Levi, living during the time of the divided kingdom, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 6:40 as the son of Baaseiah and father of Shimea (acai).
