# Imlah
**Imlah**. 
Father of the Prophet Micaiah who lived during the divided kingdom of Israel (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Imla*. 




**Familial Relationships of Imlah**


* Gender: male
* Offspring: [Micaiah](Micaiah.5.md)
