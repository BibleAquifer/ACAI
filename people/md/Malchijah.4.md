# Malchijah (Ezra 10:25)
**Malchijah (Ezra 10:25)**. 
A man who lived during the time of exile and return, mentioned in Ezra 10:25 for having married a foreign wife (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*ESV*, *Hashaviah*, *Malkijah*.
