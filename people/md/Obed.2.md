# Obed (1 Chronicles 2:37)
**Obed (1 Chronicles 2:37)**. 
Obed, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 2:37, was a man of the tribe of Judah living during the time of the divided kingdom, son of Ephlal, and father of Jehu (acai).
