# Eliashib (Nehemiah 3:1)
**Eliashib (Nehemiah 3:1)**. 
Eliashib was the high priest living during the time of Nehemiah who headed the rebuilding the Sheep Gate of the wall of Jerusalem (acai). 
Roles include: 
_High Priest_.
