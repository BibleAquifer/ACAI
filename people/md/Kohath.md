# Kohath
**Kohath**. 
A son of the patriarch Levi who lived during the time of the Egyptian slavery and wilderness wandering of the Israelites (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Kohathite*. 
Roles include: 
_Priest_. 
Kohath is also referred to as: 
group:Kohath (Group).
