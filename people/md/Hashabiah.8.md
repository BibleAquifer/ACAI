# Hashabiah (Nehemiah 3:17)
**Hashabiah (Nehemiah 3:17)**. 
Hashabiah was a chief Levite who helped repair the wall of Jerusalem during the time of exile and return, mentioned in Nehemiah 3:17 (acai).
