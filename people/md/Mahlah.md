# Mahlah
**Mahlah**. 
A daughter of Zelophehad from the tribe of Manasseh, mentioned during the time of the Egyptian slavery and wilderness wandering of the Israelites (acai). 




**Familial Relationships of Mahlah**


* Gender: female
* Tribe/Nationality: [Tribe of Manasseh](../../../groups/md/acai/Manasseh.md)
* Father: [Zelophehad](Zelophehad.md)
* Sibling(s): [Noah](Noah.2.md), [Hoglah](Hoglah.md), [Milcah](Milcah.2.md), [Tirzah](Tirzah.md)
