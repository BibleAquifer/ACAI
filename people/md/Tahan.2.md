# Tahan (1 Chronicles 7:25)
**Tahan (1 Chronicles 7:25)**. 
Tahan, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 7:25, was a man of the tribe of Ephraim, son of Telah, and father of Ladan during the divided kingdom period (acai).
