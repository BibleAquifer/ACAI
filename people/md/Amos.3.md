# Amos (Prophet)
**Amos (Prophet)**. 
A prophet to the northern kingdom of Israel during the divided kingdom. A book of the Old Testament is named for him (acai). 
Roles include: 
_Prophet_, _Shepherd_. 




**Familial Relationships of Amos (Prophet)**
