# Ehud
**Ehud**. 
A son of Gera from the tribe of Benjamin who was judge of Israel when Eglon was king of Moab (acai). 
Roles include: 
_Judge_. 




**Familial Relationships of Ehud**


* Gender: male
* Tribe/Nationality: [Tribe of Benjamin](../../../groups/md/acai/Benjamin.md)
