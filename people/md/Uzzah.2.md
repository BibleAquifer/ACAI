# Uzzah (1 Chronicles 6:29)
**Uzzah (1 Chronicles 6:29)**. 
Uzzah, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 6:29, was a man of the tribe of Levi living at the time of the divided kingdom and the son of Shimei (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Uzza*.
