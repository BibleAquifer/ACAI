# Simon (Acts 8:9)
**Simon (Acts 8:9)**. 
Simon, also known as Simon the Sorcerer, was a magician living in Samaria during the New Testament, first mentioned in Acts 8:9 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Simon the Sorcerer*.
