# Shechem (1 Chronicles 7:19)
**Shechem (1 Chronicles 7:19)**. 
Shechem was a Manasseh tribe member living during the time of Egypt and the wilderness, son of Shemida and brother of Ahian, Likhi, and Aniam, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 7:19 (acai).
