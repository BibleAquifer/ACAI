# Jeshaiah (1 Chronicles 3:21)
**Jeshaiah (1 Chronicles 3:21)**. 
A man from the tribe of Judah during the divided kingdom, son of Hananiah, and brother to Pelatiah, Rephaiah, Arnan, Obadiah, and Shecaniah, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 3:21 (acai).

*Jesaiah*.
