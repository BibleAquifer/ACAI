# Luke
**Luke**. 
A companion of Paul in the New Testament, traditionally regarded as the author of the Gospel of Luke and Acts of the Apostles (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Lucas*. 
Roles include: 
_Disciple_, _Missionary_.
