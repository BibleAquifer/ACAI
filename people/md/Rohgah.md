# Rohgah
**Rohgah**. 
Rohgah, also known as Rohagah, son of Shomer and a man of the tribe of Asher, lived during the time of the divided kingdom, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 7:34 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Rohagah*.
