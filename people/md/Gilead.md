# Gilead
**Gilead**. 
Son of Machir, grandson of Manasseh, and great-grandson of the patriarch Joseph. Gilead lived during the time of the Egyptian slavery and wilderness wandering of the Israelites (acai). 
Roles include: 
_Leader_.
