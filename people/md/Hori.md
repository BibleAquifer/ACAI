# Hori
**Hori**. 
A son of Lotan and brother of Hemam, who lived during the time of the patriarchs (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Horite*. 
Hori is also referred to as: 
group:Hori (Group).

* Tribe/Nationality: [Edomites](../../../groups/md/acai/Edom.md)
