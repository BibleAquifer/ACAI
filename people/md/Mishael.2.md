# Mishael (Nehemiah 8:4)
**Mishael (Nehemiah 8:4)**. 
Mishael, mentioned in Nehemiah 8:4, was a man who lived during the time of exile and return and stood to the left of Ezra when he read from the book of the Law (acai).
