# Ithran (1 Chronicles 7:37)
**Ithran (1 Chronicles 7:37)**. 
A man from the tribe of Asher, son of Zophah, and father of Jephunneh, Pispa, and Ara, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 7:37-38 during the time of the divided kingdom (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Jether*.
