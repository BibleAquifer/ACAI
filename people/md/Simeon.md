# Simeon (Peter)
**Simeon (Peter)**. 
Apostle living at the time of the New Testament (tipnr-short). 
Other forms include: 
*Peter*, *Simon*. 
Roles include: 
_Apostle_, _Disciple_, _Fisher_, _Martyr, _. 
Simeon (Peter) is also referred to as: 
[Peter](Peter.md). 
Simeon (Peter) is a subentry. The primary entry is 
[Peter](Peter.md).
