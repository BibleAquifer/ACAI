# Jude
**Jude**. 
A New Testament man, brother of Jesus, also called Judas, mentioned in Matthew 13:55 and Mark 6:3 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Juda*, *Judas*. 
Roles include: 
_Relative of Jesus_. 
Jude is also referred to as: 
[Judas (Jude)](Judas.6.md).
