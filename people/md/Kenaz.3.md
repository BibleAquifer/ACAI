# Kenaz
**Kenaz**. 
A Kenizzite and son of Jephunneh, brother of the warrior Caleb, and father of the judge Othniel and Seraiah (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Kenizzite*. 
Roles include: 
_Leader_. 
Kenaz is also referred to as: 
group:Kenaz (Group).

* Sibling(s): [Caleb](Caleb.md)
