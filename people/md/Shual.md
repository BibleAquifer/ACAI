# Shual
**Shual**. 
Shual, also known as Ulla or Suah, was a man from the tribe of Asher living during the time of the divided kingdom, first mentioned in 1 Chronicles 7:36 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Suah*, *Ulla*. 
Shual is also referred to as: 
[Ulla (Shual)](Ulla.md).
