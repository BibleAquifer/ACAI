# Basemath (Adah)
**Basemath (Adah)**. 
Woman living at the time before the Flood (tipnr-short). 
Other forms include: 
*Adah*, *Bashemath*, *Beeri*. 
Basemath (Adah) is also referred to as: 
[Adah](Adah.2.md). 
Basemath (Adah) is a subentry. The primary entry is 
[Adah](Adah.2.md).
