# Raphu
**Raphu**. 
Raphu is a man from the time of Egypt and the wilderness, mentioned in Numbers 13:9, and the father of the spy Palti (acai). 




**Familial Relationships of Raphu**


* Gender: male
* Offspring: [Palti](Palti.md)
