# Nahbi
**Nahbi**. 
Nahbi, son of Vophsi from the tribe of Naphtali, was one of the twelve spies sent by Moses to explore Canaan, mentioned in Numbers 13:14 (acai). 




**Familial Relationships of Nahbi**


* Gender: male
* Father: [Vophsi](Vophsi.md)
