# Hashbaddanah
**Hashbaddanah**. 
Hashbaddanah was a man living during the time of exile and return, who stood to the left of Ezra when he read from the book of the Law, mentioned in Nehemiah 8:4 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Hashbadana*.
