# Habaiah
**Habaiah**. 
Habaiah, also known as Hobaiah, was a man living during the time of exile and return, mentioned in Ezra 2:61 and Nehemiah 7:63 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Hobaiah*. 
Habaiah is also referred to as: 
[Hobaiah (Habaiah)](Hobaiah.md).
