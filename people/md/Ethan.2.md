# Ethan (1 Chronicles 2:6)
**Ethan (1 Chronicles 2:6)**. 
Ethan, first mentioned in 1 Chronicles 2:6, was a man from the tribe of Judah before Israel's Monarchy and the son of Zerah (acai).
