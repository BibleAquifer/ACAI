# Shallum (1 Chronicles 4:25)
**Shallum (1 Chronicles 4:25)**. 
Shallum, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 4:25, was a man of the tribe of Simeon during the time of Egypt and the wilderness, the son of Shaul, and the father of Mibsam (acai).
