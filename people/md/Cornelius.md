# Cornelius
**Cornelius**. 
A Roman officer (Centurion) whose family was baptized by the apostle Peter (acai). 
Roles include: 
_Army Commander_, _Disciple_, _Military Commander_. 




**Familial Relationships of Cornelius**


* Gender: male
