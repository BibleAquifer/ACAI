# Amon (Son of Manasseh)
**Amon (Son of Manasseh)**. 
King of Judah during the divided kingdom. He was a son of Manasseh and Meshullemeth and father of King Josiah (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Amos*. 
Roles include: 
_King_. 
Amon (Son of Manasseh) is also referred to as: 
[Amos (Amon)](Amos.md).
