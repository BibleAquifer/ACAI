# Eliada
**Eliada**. 
Eliada, also known as Beeliada, was one of David's sons during Israel's united kingdom, mentioned in 2 Samuel 5:16 and 1 Chronicles 14:7 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Beeliada*. 
Eliada is also referred to as: 
[Beeliada (Eliada)](Beeliada.md).
