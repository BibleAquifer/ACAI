# Nethanel (Nehemiah 12:21)
**Nethanel (Nehemiah 12:21)**. 
Nethanel, also known as Nethaneel, was a Levite chief of a priestly clan who lived after the exile, mentioned in Nehemiah 12:21 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Nethaneel*.
