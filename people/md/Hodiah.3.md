# Hodiah (Nehemiah 10:13)
**Hodiah (Nehemiah 10:13)**. 
Hodiah, also called Hodijah, was a Levite living at the time of exile and return, mentioned in Nehemiah 10:13 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Hodijah*.
