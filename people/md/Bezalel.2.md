# Bezalel (Ezra 10:30)
**Bezalel (Ezra 10:30)**. 
A man from the clan of Pahat-moab, living during the time of exile and return, mentioned in Ezra 10:30 as one who married a foreign woman (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Bezaleel*.
