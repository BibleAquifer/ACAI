# Micah
**Micah**. 
Micah, a priest from Ephraim living during the time of Israel's judges (acai). 
Roles include: 
_Priest_. 




**Familial Relationships of Micah**


* Gender: male
* Tribe/Nationality: [Tribe of Levi](../../../groups/md/acai/Levi.md)
