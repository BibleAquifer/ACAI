# Elijah (Ezra 10:26)
**Elijah (Ezra 10:26)**. 
Elijah, also called Eliah, was a descendant of Elam who married a non-Israelite woman during the time of exile and return, mentioned in Ezra 10:26 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Eliah*.
