# Osnappar
**Osnappar**. 
Osnappar, also known as Ashurbanipal, was the king of Assyria during the exile and return, mentioned in Ezra 4:10 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Ashurbanipal*, *Asnappar*, *Asnapper*.
