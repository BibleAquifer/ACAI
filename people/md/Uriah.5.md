# Uriah (Nehemiah 8:4)
**Uriah (Nehemiah 8:4)**. 
Uriah was a man living during the time of exile and return who stood to the right of Ezra when he read from the book of the Law, mentioned in Nehemiah 8:4 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Urijah*.
