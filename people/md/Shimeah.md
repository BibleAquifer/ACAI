# Shimeah
**Shimeah**. 
Shimeah (also called Shimeam), a man from the tribe of Benjamin, lived during the divided kingdom and was the son of Mikloth (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Shimeam*. 
Shimeah is also referred to as: 
[Shimeam (Shimeah)](Shimeam.md). 




**Familial Relationships of Shimeah**


* Gender: male
* Tribe/Nationality: [Tribe of Benjamin](../../../groups/md/acai/Benjamin.md)
* Father: [Mikloth](Mikloth.md)
