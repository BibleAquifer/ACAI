# Beriah (1 Chronicles 8:13)
**Beriah (1 Chronicles 8:13)**. 
A man from the tribe of Benjamin, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 8:13 as the son of Elpaal and a family head living during the time of Egypt and the wilderness (acai).
