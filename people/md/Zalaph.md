# Zalaph
**Zalaph**. 
Zalaph was a man living during the time of exile and return, mentioned only in Nehemiah 3:30 as the father of Hanun (acai). 




**Familial Relationships of Zalaph**


* Gender: male
* Offspring: [Hanun (Nehemiah 3:30)](Hanun.3.md)
