# Nahash (2 Samuel 17:25)
**Nahash (2 Samuel 17:25)**. 
Nahash, a woman living during the time of Israel's united kingdom and mentioned in 2 Samuel 17:25, was the wife of Jesse and possibly the mother of David and his siblings (acai).
