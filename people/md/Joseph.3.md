# Joseph (Brother of Jesus)
**Joseph (Brother of Jesus)**. 
A brother of Jesus, also called Joses, mentioned in Matthew 13:55 and Mark 6:3; 15:47 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Joses*. 
Roles include: 
_Relative of Jesus_.
