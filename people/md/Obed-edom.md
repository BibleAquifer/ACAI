# Obed-edom
**Obed-edom**. 
A Gittite in whose house the ark of the covenat was stored during King David's reign (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Shimei*. 
Obed-edom is also referred to as: 
[Shimei (Obed-edom)](Shimei.md).
