# Hodaviah (1 Chronicles 9:7)
**Hodaviah (1 Chronicles 9:7)**. 
Hodaviah was a man of the tribe of Benjamin living during the time of exile and return, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 9:7, and was the son of Hassenuah (acai).
