# Perez
**Perez**. 
A son of Judah by Tamar, and brother to Zerah. He is listed in the family line of Jesus (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Perezite*, *Phares*, *Pharez*. 
Perez is also referred to as: 
group:Perez (Group).
