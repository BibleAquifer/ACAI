# Mezahab
**Mezahab**. 
Mezahab, living at the time of the patriarchs, is the parent of Matred and is first mentioned in Genesis 36:39 (acai). 




**Familial Relationships of Mezahab**


* Gender: male
* Tribe/Nationality: [Edomites](../../../groups/md/acai/Edom.md)
* Offspring: [Matred](Matred.md)
