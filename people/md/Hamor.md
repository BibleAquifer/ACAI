# Hamor
**Hamor**. 
A Hivite and father of Schechem who lived during the time of the patriarchs (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Emmor*. 
Roles include: 
_Ruler_. 




**Familial Relationships of Hamor**


* Gender: male
* Offspring: [Shechem](Shechem.md)
