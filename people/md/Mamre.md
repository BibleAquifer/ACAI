# Mamre
**Mamre**. 
An Amorite man living during the time of the patriarchs, mentioned in Genesis 14:13, brother of Eshkol and Aner, and ally of Abram (acai).

* Sibling(s): [Eshcol](Eshcol.md), [Aner](Aner.md)
