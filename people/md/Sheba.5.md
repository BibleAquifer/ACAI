# Sheba (1 Chronicles 5:13)
**Sheba (1 Chronicles 5:13)**. 
Sheba was a man of the tribe of Gad living at the time of the divided kingdom, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 5:13 as the son of Abihail (acai).
