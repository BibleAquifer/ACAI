# Hizkiah
**Hizkiah**. 
Hizkiah, also known as Hezekiah, was a man from the tribe of Judah during the divided kingdom, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 3:23, and was the son of Neariah (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Hezekiah*.
