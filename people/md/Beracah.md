# Beracah
**Beracah**. 
A man from the tribe of Benjamin, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 12:3 as one of the ambidextrous warriors related to King Saul during the time of the divided kingdom (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Berachah*, *Berakah*.
