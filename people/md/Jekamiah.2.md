# Jekamiah (1 Chronicles 3:18)
**Jekamiah (1 Chronicles 3:18)**. 
A man of the tribe of Judah during the divided kingdom, son of Jehoiachin and mentioned in 1 Chronicles 3:18 (acai).

*Jecamiah*.
