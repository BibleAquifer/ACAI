# Ephah (1 Chronicles 2:46)
**Ephah (1 Chronicles 2:46)**. 
Ephah was a woman of the tribe of Judah, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 2:46 as the concubine of Caleb and mother of Haran, Moza, and Gazez (acai).
