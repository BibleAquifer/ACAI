# Sophereth
**Sophereth**. 
Sophereth, also called Hassophereth, was a man living during the time of the exile and return, mentioned in Ezra 2:55 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Hassophereth*. 
Sophereth is also referred to as: 
[Hassophereth (Sophereth)](Hassophereth.md).
