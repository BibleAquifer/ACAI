# Ahiman
**Ahiman**. 
A giant and descendant of Anak living during the time of Egypt and the wilderness, mentioned in Numbers 13:22 (acai). 




**Familial Relationships of Ahiman**


* Gender: male
* Father: [Anak](Anak.md)
* Sibling(s): [Sheshai](Sheshai.md), [Talmai](Talmai.md)
