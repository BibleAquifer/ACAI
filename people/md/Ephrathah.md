# Ephrathah
**Ephrathah**. 
A woman of the tribe of Judah, one of the wives of Caleb the son of Hezron, and mother of Hur (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Ephratah*, *Ephrath*, *Ephrathite*. 
Ephrathah is also referred to as: 
group:Ephrathah (Group).

* Offspring: [Hur](Hur.2.md)
