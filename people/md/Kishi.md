# Kishi
**Kishi**. 
A Levite living during the divided kingdom, father of Jeduthun, also called Kushaiah in 1 Chronicles 15:17 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Kushaiah*. 
Kishi is also referred to as: 
[Kushaiah (Kishi)](Kushaiah.md).
