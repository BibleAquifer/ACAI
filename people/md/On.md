# On
**On**. 
On, mentioned in Numbers 16:1, is a man from the tribe of Reuben living during the time of Egypt and the wilderness, and is the son of Peleth (acai). 




**Familial Relationships of On**


* Gender: male
* Father: [Peleth](Peleth.md)
