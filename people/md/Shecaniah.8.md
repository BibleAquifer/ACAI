# Shecaniah (Nehemiah 6:18)
**Shecaniah (Nehemiah 6:18)**. 
Shecaniah, also known as Shekaniah or Shechaniah, was a man living during the exile and return, son of Arah, only mentioned in Nehemiah 6:18 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Shechaniah*, *Shekaniah*.
