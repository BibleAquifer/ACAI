# Shimea (1 Chronicles 6:30)
**Shimea (1 Chronicles 6:30)**. 
Shimea, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 6:30, was a man from the tribe of Levi living during the divided kingdom and the son of Uzzah and father of Haggiah (acai).
