# Zechariah (Nehemiah 8:4)
**Zechariah (Nehemiah 8:4)**. 
Zechariah was a man living at the time of exile and return who stood to the left of Ezra when he read from the book of the Law, mentioned in Nehemiah 8:4 (acai).
