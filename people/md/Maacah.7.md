# Maacah (1 Chronicles 11:43)
**Maacah (1 Chronicles 11:43)**. 
A man during the divided kingdom, mentioned only in 1 Chronicles 11:43, and father of Hanan (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Maachah*, *Maakah*.
