# Amaziah (1 Chronicles 4:34)
**Amaziah (1 Chronicles 4:34)**. 
A man of the tribe of Simeon living at the time of the divided kingdom, mentioned only in 1 Chronicles 4:34, and the father of Joshah (acai).
