# Rephaiah (1 Chronicles 3:21)
**Rephaiah (1 Chronicles 3:21)**. 
Rephaiah was a man from the tribe of Judah during the divided kingdom and the son of Hananiah, mentioned only in 1 Chronicles 3:21 (acai).
