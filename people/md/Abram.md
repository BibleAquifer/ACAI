# Abram (Abraham)
**Abram (Abraham)**. 
Man living at the time of the Patriarchs (tipnr-short). 
Other forms include: 
*Abraham*. 
Roles include: 
_Patriarch_, _Shepherd_. 
Abram (Abraham) is also referred to as: 
[Abraham](Abraham.md). 
Abram (Abraham) is a subentry. The primary entry is 
[Abraham](Abraham.md).
