# Eri
**Eri**. 
Eri, first mentioned in Genesis 46:16, was a man of the tribe of Gad and son of Gad (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Erite*. 
Eri is also referred to as: 
group:Eri (Group). 




**Familial Relationships of Eri**
