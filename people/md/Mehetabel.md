# Mehetabel
**Mehetabel**. 
A woman from the time of the patriarchs, daughter of Matred and wife of Hadad, king of Edom (acai). 




**Familial Relationships of Mehetabel**


* Gender: female
* Tribe/Nationality: [Edomites](../../../groups/md/acai/Edom.md)
