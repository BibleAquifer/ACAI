# Jehoshaphat (1 Kings 4:17)
**Jehoshaphat (1 Kings 4:17)**. 
A man living during Israel's united kingdom, son of Paruah, and an official in King Solomon's administration, mentioned only in 1 Kings 4:17 (acai).
