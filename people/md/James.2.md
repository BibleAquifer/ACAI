# James
**James**. 
One of the twelve apostles of Jesus and son of Alphaeus (acai). 
Roles include: 
_Apostle_, _Disciple_. 




**Familial Relationships of James**


* Gender: male
* Father: [Alphaeus](Alphaeus.md)
* Sibling(s): [Judas](Judas.md)
