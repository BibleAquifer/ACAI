# Shem
**Shem**. 
The son of the patriarch Noah, brother of Ham and Japheth. He was the father of Asshur, Elam, Arpachshad, Lud, and Aram. He is listed in the family line of Jesus (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Sem*. 
Roles include: 
_Patriarch_.
