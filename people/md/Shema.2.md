# Shema (1 Chronicles 5:8)
**Shema (1 Chronicles 5:8)**. 
Shema, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 5:8, was a man of the tribe of Reuben living during the time of the divided kingdom, the son of Joel and father of Azaz (acai).
