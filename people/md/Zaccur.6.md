# Zaccur (Nehemiah 3:2)
**Zaccur (Nehemiah 3:2)**. 
Zaccur, also known as Zakkur, was a man living during the time of the exile and return, the son of Imri, mentioned in Nehemiah 3:2 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Zakkur*.
