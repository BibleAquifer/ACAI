# Jeremoth (Ezra 10:29)
**Jeremoth (Ezra 10:29)**. 
A man from the time of exile and return, mentioned in Ezra 10:29 as a descendant of Bani who married a foreign wife (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Ramoth*, *and Ramoth*.
