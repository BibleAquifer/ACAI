# Beor
**Beor**. 
A man living during the time of the Egyptian slavery and wilderness wandering of the Israelites. He was the father of the prophet Balaam (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Bezer*, *Bosor*. 




**Familial Relationships of Beor**


* Gender: male
* Offspring: [Balaam](Balaam.md)
