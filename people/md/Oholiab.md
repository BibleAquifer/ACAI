# Oholiab
**Oholiab**. 
A skilled craftsman from the tribe of Dan who worked on the Tabernacle during the time of the wilderness wandering of the Israelites (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Aholiab*. 




**Familial Relationships of Oholiab**


* Gender: male
* Father: [Ahisamach](Ahisamach.md)
