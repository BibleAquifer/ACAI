# Reaiah (1 Chronicles 5:5)
**Reaiah (1 Chronicles 5:5)**. 
Reaiah, also known as Reaia, is a man from the tribe of Reuben, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 5:5, living during the time of the divided kingdom and the father of Baal (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Reaia*.
