# Zechariah (1 Chronicles 26:11)
**Zechariah (1 Chronicles 26:11)**. 
Zechariah, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 26:11, was the fourth son of Hosah, a Levite gatekeeper during the divided kingdom (acai).
