# Helek
**Helek**. 
Helek, a man from the tribe of Manasseh and son of Gilead, lived during the time of Egypt and the wilderness, first mentioned in Numbers 26:30 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Helekite*. 
Helek is also referred to as: 
group:Helek (Group).
