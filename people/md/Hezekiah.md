# Hezekiah
**Hezekiah**. 
King of the southern kingdom of Judah during the divided kingdom. He was the son of Ahaz and the father of Manasseh. He is mentioned in the family line of Jesus (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Ezekias*. 
Roles include: 
_King_, _Relative of Jesus_.
