# Mahli
**Mahli**. 
One of the sons of Merari from the tribe of Levi (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Mahali*, *Mahlite*. 
Roles include: 
_Priest_. 
Mahli is also referred to as: 
group:Mahli (Group).
