# Zedekiah
**Zedekiah**. 
A son of Chenaanah and false prohet who living during the time of the divided kingdom of Israel (acai). 
Roles include: 
_Prophet_. 




**Familial Relationships of Zedekiah**


* Gender: male
* Father: [Chenaanah](Chenaanah.md)
