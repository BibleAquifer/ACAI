# Muppim
**Muppim**. 
A son of the patriarch Benjamin (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Shephupham*, *Shephuphan*, *Shupham*, *Shuphamite*. 
Muppim is also referred to as: 
group:Shuphamite (Group), [Shephupham (Muppim)](Shephupham.md), [Shephuphan (Muppim)](Shephuphan.md).
