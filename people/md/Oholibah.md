# Oholibah
**Oholibah**. 
A woman who personifies Jerusalem and the southern kingdom of Judah (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Aholibah*. 




**Familial Relationships of Oholibah**


* Gender: female
* Sibling(s): [Oholah](Oholah.md)
