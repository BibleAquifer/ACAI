# Puah (Judges 10:1)
**Puah (Judges 10:1)**. 
Puah, mentioned in Judges 10:1, is a man living before Israel's Monarchy, father of Tola and son of Dodo (acai). 




**Familial Relationships of Puah (Judges 10:1)**


* Gender: male
* Father: [Dodo](Dodo.md)
* Offspring: [Elon (Judges 12:11)](Tola.2.md)
