# Ahisamach
**Ahisamach**. 
A man from the tribe of Dan, father of Oholiab, mentioned during the time of Egypt and the wilderness in Exodus 31:6 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Ahisamak*. 




**Familial Relationships of Ahisamach**


* Gender: male
* Offspring: [Oholiab](Oholiab.md)
