# Zohar
**Zohar**. 
Zohar was a man living during the time of the patriarchs, father of Ephron, mentioned in Genesis 23:8 (acai). 




**Familial Relationships of Zohar**


* Gender: male
* Offspring: [Ephron](Ephron.2.md)
