# Ribai
**Ribai**. 
Ribai, father of Ittai, was a man living during the time of Israel's united kingdom, mentioned in 2 Samuel 23:29 (acai). 




**Familial Relationships of Ribai**


* Gender: male
* Offspring: [Ittai (2 Samuel 23:29)](Ittai.2.md)
