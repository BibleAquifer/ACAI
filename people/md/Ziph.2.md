# Ziph (1 Chronicles 4:16)
**Ziph (1 Chronicles 4:16)**. 
Ziph, a man from the tribe of Judah during the time of the divided kingdom, was the son of Jehallelel and mentioned in 1 Chronicles 4:16 (acai).
