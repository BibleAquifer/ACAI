# Jehoiakim
**Jehoiakim**. 
Son of Josiah who was made king over Judah by Phraoah Neco (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Eliakim*, *Zedekiah*. 
Roles include: 
_King_. 
Jehoiakim is also referred to as: 
[Eliakim (Jehoiakim)](Eliakim.5.md), [Zedekiah (Jehoiakim)](Zedekiah.md).
