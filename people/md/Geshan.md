# Geshan
**Geshan**. 
Geshan, also known as Gesham, was a descendant of Caleb from the tribe of Judah living during the time of the divided kingdom, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 2:47 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Gesham*.
