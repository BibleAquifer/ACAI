# Sachia
**Sachia**. 
Sachia, also known as Shachia, was a Benjaminite living during the divided kingdom, son of Shaharaim and Hodesh, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 8:10 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Shachia*.
