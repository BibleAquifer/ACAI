# Egypt
**Egypt**. 
Second son of Ham, the patriarch Noah's son. Egypt is regarded as the founder of the Egyptian nation (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Mizraim*. 
Roles include: 
_Leader_, _Patriarch_.
