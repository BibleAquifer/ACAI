# Manasseh (Son of Joseph)
**Manasseh (Son of Joseph)**. 
A son of the patriarch Joseph, who founded the tribe of Manasseh (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Manasses*, *Manassite*. 
Roles include: 
_Patriarch_. 
Manasseh (Son of Joseph) is also referred to as: 
group:Manasseh (Group).
