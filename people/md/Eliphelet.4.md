# Eliphelet (1 Chronicles 8:39)
**Eliphelet (1 Chronicles 8:39)**. 
Eliphelet, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 8:39, was a man from the tribe of Benjamin during the time of the divided kingdom and the son of Eleasah (acai).
