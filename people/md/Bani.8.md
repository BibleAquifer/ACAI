# Bani
**Bani**. 
A Levite living at the time of exile and return of the the Israelites. He was also called Binnui (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Binnui*. 
Roles include: 
_Priest_, _Scribe_. 
Bani is also referred to as: 
[Binnui (Bani)](Binnui.md). 




**Familial Relationships of Bani**


* Gender: male
