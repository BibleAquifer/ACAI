# Pethuel
**Pethuel**. 
Pethuel, mentioned in Joel 1:1, was a man living during the time of the divided kingdom and the father of the prophet Joel (acai). 




**Familial Relationships of Pethuel**


* Gender: male
* Offspring: [Joel (Joel 1:1)](Joel.14.md)
