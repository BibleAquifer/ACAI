# Eliphelet
**Eliphelet**. 
Eliphelet, also known as Eliphalet, was a son of King David from the tribe of Judah during Israel's united kingdom, first mentioned in 2 Samuel 5:16 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Eliphalet*.
