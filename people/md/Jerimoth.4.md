# Jerimoth (1 Chronicles 25:4)
**Jerimoth (1 Chronicles 25:4)**. 
A Levite during the divided kingdom, son of Heman, and brother to Bukkiah, Mattaniah, and others, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 25:4 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Jeremoth*.
