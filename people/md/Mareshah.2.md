# Mareshah (1 Chronicles 4:21)
**Mareshah (1 Chronicles 4:21)**. 
A man of the tribe of Judah, living during the divided kingdom, and only mentioned in 1 Chronicles 4:21 as the son of Laadah (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Laadah*.
