# Hemam
**Hemam**. 
Hemam, also known as Homam, lived during the time of the patriarchs and was the son of Lotan and brother of Hori, first mentioned in Genesis 36:22 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Homam*. 




**Familial Relationships of Hemam**


* Gender: male
* Tribe/Nationality: [Edomites](../../../groups/md/acai/Edom.md)
* Father: [Lotan](Lotan.md)
* Sibling(s): [Hori](Hori.md)
