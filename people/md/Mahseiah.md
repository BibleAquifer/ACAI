# Mahseiah
**Mahseiah**. 
A man from the time of the divided kingdom, father of Neriah, mentioned in Jeremiah 32:12 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Maaseiah*. 




**Familial Relationships of Mahseiah**


* Gender: male
* Offspring: [Neriah](Neriah.md)
