# Jerimoth (1 Chronicles 27:19)
**Jerimoth (1 Chronicles 27:19)**. 
A man from the tribe of Naphtali and son of Azriel, an officer in charge of his tribe during King David's time, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 27:19 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Jeremoth*.
