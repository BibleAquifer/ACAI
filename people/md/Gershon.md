# Gershon
**Gershon**. 
One of the three sons of Levi. He was the founder of the Gershonite clan (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Gershom*, *Gershomite*, *Gershonite*. 
Roles include: 
_Leader_, _Patriarch_. 
Gershon is also referred to as: 
group:Gershon (Group).
