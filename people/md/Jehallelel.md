# Jehallelel
**Jehallelel**. 
A man from the tribe of Judah during the divided kingdom, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 4:16, and father of Ziph, Ziphah, Tiria, and Asarel (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Jehaleleel*. 




**Familial Relationships of Jehallelel**


* Gender: male
