# Mahalath (2 Chronicles 11:18)
**Mahalath (2 Chronicles 11:18)**. 
Mahalath, also known as Michaiah or Micaiah, was an official during the divided kingdom, mentioned in 2 Chronicles 17:7 during the time of Jehoshaphat (acai).
