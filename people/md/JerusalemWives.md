# Jerusalem Wives
**Jerusalem Wives**. 
The wives of King David during Israel's united kingdom and mothers to several of his children including Eliada, Eliphalet, Elpelet, Elishama, Elishua, Ibhar, Japhia, Jerimoth, Nepheg, and Nogah (acai).
