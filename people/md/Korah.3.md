# Korah
**Korah**. 
A Levitical priest and adversary of the high priest Aaron who lived during the time of the Egyptian slavery and wilderness wandering of the Israelites (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Core*, *Korahite*. 
Roles include: 
_Priest_. 
Korah is also referred to as: 
group:Korah (Group).
