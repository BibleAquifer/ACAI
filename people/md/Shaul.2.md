# Shaul
**Shaul**. 
The son of the partriarch Simeon and a Canaanite woman. He was founder of the Shaulite clan (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Shaulite*. 
Roles include: 
_Leader_, _Patriarch_. 
Shaul is also referred to as: 
group:Shaul (Group).
