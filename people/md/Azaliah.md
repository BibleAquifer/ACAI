# Azaliah
**Azaliah**. 
A man of the divided kingdom, son of Meshullam and father of Shaphan, first mentioned in 2 Kings 22:3 (acai). 




**Familial Relationships of Azaliah**


* Gender: male
* Father: [Meshullam](Meshullam.md)
* Offspring: [Shaphan](Shaphan.md)
