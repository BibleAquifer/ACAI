# Ishmael
**Ishmael**. 
A son of Nethaniah and royal family member. Ishmael killed the governor Gedaliah (acai). 
Roles include: 
_Royal Family Member_, _Warrior_. 




**Familial Relationships of Ishmael**


* Gender: male
* Father: [Nethaniah](Nethaniah.md)
