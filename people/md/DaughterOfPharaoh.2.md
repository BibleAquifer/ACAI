# Daughter of Pharaoh (1 Kings 3:1)
**Daughter of Pharaoh (1 Kings 3:1)**. 
The Daughter of Pharaoh, mentioned in 1 Kings 3:1, was an unnamed Egyptian woman who married King Solomon during Israel's united kingdom period (acai).
