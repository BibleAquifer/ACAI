# Malchi-shua
**Malchi-shua**. 
A son of King Saul, killed in battle by the Philistines (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Malki-Shua*, *Melchi-shua*. 
Roles include: 
_Royal Family Member_, _Warrior_.
