# Maacah (1 Chronicles 27:16)
**Maacah (1 Chronicles 27:16)**. 
A man during the divided kingdom, mentioned only in 1 Chronicles 27:16, and father of Shephatiah (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Maachah*, *Maakah*.
