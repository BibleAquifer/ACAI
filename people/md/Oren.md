# Oren
**Oren**. 
Oren is a man from the tribe of Judah, living during Israel's united kingdom, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 2:25 as the son of Jerahmeel and brother of Ram, Bunah, Ozem, Ahijah, and Onam (acai).
