# Hezekiah (Zephaniah 1:1)
**Hezekiah (Zephaniah 1:1)**. 
Hezekiah, also known as Hizkiah, was a man from the time of the divided kingdom, mentioned in Zephaniah 1:1, and an ancestor of the prophet Zephaniah (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Hizkiah*.
