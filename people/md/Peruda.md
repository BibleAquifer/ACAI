# Peruda
**Peruda**. 
Peruda, also known as Perida, was a man living during the time of exile and return, first mentioned in Ezra 2:55 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Perida*. 
Peruda is also referred to as: 
[Perida (Peruda)](Perida.md).
