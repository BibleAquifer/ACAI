# Shaul (1 Chronicles 6:24)
**Shaul (1 Chronicles 6:24)**. 
Shaul, also called Joel, was a man of the tribe of Levi, the son of Uzziah and father of Elkanah, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 6:24 (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Joel*.
