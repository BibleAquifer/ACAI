# Andrew
**Andrew**. 
First called of the twelve disciples of Jesus. Andrew was a brother of Simon Peter (acai). 
Roles include: 
_Apostle_, _Fisherman_, _Missionary_. 




**Familial Relationships of Andrew**


* Gender: male
* Father: [John](John.4.md)
* Sibling(s): [Peter](Peter.md)
