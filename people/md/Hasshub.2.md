# Hasshub (Nehemiah 3:11)
**Hasshub (Nehemiah 3:11)**. 
Hasshub, also known as Hashub, was a man who lived at the time of exile and return, mentioned only in Nehemiah 3:11 as the son of Pahath-Moab (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Hashub*.

* Gender: male
