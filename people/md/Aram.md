# Aram
**Aram**. 
Son of Shem and grandson of Noah. He was regarded as the founder of the ethnic group and land named Aram. The land of Aram is referred to as Syria (See Genesis 28:6 in the LXX) (acai). 
Roles include: 
_Patriarch_.
