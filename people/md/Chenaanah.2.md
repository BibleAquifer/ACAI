# Chenaanah (1 Chronicles 7:10)
**Chenaanah (1 Chronicles 7:10)**. 
Chenaanah (1 Chronicles 7:10), also known as Kenaanah, was a man from the tribe of Benjamin during the time of the divided kingdom, son of Bilhan (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Kenaanah*.
