# Shemaiah (1 Chronicles 3:22)
**Shemaiah (1 Chronicles 3:22)**. 
Shemaiah, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 3:22, was a man of the tribe of Judah living during the time of the divided kingdom, and the son of Shecaniah (acai).
