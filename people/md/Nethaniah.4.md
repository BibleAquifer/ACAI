# Nethaniah (Jeremiah 36:14)
**Nethaniah (Jeremiah 36:14)**. 
Nethaniah, the father of Jehudi and son of Shelemiah, was an official during the time of King Jehoiakim, mentioned in Jeremiah 36:14 (acai).
