# Tabeal
**Tabeal**. 
Tabeal, mentioned in Isaiah 7:6, was the father of a man whom enemies planned to place as a puppet ruler on the Judean throne during the divided kingdom era (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Tabeel*.
