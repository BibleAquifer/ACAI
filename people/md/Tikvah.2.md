# Tikvah (Ezra 10:15)
**Tikvah (Ezra 10:15)**. 
Tikvah, mentioned in Ezra 10:15, was a man living during the time of exile and return, and the father of Jahzeiah (acai). 




**Familial Relationships of Tikvah (Ezra 10:15)**


* Gender: male
* Offspring: [Jahzeiah](Jahzeiah.md)
