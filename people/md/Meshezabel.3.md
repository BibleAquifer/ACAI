# Meshezabel (Nehemiah 11:24)
**Meshezabel (Nehemiah 11:24)**. 
A man living during the time of exile and return, father of Pethahiah, mentioned in Nehemiah 11:24, also known as Meshezabeel (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Meshezabeel*.
