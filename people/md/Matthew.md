# Matthew
**Matthew**. 
An apostle of Jesus, former tax collector (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Levi*. 
Roles include: 
_Apostle_, _Disciple_, _Evangelist_. 
Matthew is also referred to as: 
[Levi (Matthew)](Levi.4.md).

* Gender: male
