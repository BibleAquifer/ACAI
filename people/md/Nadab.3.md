# Nadab (1 Chronicles 2:28)
**Nadab (1 Chronicles 2:28)**. 
Nadab, son of Shammai and a member of the tribe of Judah, is mentioned in 1 Chronicles 2:28 during the time of the divided kingdom (acai).
