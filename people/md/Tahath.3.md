# Tahath (1 Chronicles 7:20b)
**Tahath (1 Chronicles 7:20b)**. 
Tahath, mentioned in 1 Chronicles 7:20, was a man of the tribe of Ephraim, son of Eleadah, and father of Zabad, living during the divided kingdom period (acai).
