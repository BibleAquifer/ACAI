# Kadmiel
**Kadmiel**. 
Father of Jeshua who lived during the time of Israelite exile and return (acai). 
Roles include: 
_Leader_, _Priest_. 




**Familial Relationships of Kadmiel**


* Gender: male
* Offspring: [Jeshua (Nehemiah 12:24)](Jeshua.7.md)
