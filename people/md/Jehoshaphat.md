# Jehoshaphat
**Jehoshaphat**. 
A son of Ahilud and one of king David’s officials. He served as a recorder during the united kingdom of Israel (acai). 
Roles include: 
_Official_, _Scribe_. 




**Familial Relationships of Jehoshaphat**


* Gender: male
* Father: [Ahilud](Ahilud.md)
* Sibling(s): [Baana](Baana.md)
