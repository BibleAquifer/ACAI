# Nisroch
**Nisroch**. 
An Assyrian god worshipped by Sennacherib (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Adrammelech*. 


**Key References for Nisroch**: 
2KI 19:37, ISA 37:38. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Nisroch” are translated as 
*Nisroch* (2x).
