# Artemis
**Artemis**. 
A Greek fertility goddess associated with Ephesus (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Diana*. 


**Key References for Artemis**: 
ACT 19:24. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Artemis” are translated as 
*Artemis bringing* (1x), *Artemis* (4x).
