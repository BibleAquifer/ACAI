# Zeus
**Zeus**. 
The primary god of the Greeks. In Acts 14:12, Barnabas is mistakenly identified as Zeus (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Jupiter*. 


**Key References for Zeus**: 
ACT 14:12, ACT 14:13. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Zeus” are translated as 
*Zeus* (1x), *of Zeus* (1x).
