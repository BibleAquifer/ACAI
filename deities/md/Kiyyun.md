# Kiyyun
**Kiyyun**. 
An Assyrian/Babylonian astral god associated with the planet Saturn (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Kaiwan*. 


**Key References for Kiyyun**: 
AMO 5:26. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Kiyyun” are translated as 
*and Kaiwan* (1x).
