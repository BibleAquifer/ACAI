# Baal-Berith
**Baal-Berith**. 
A god worshipped by people of Shechem (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*El-Berith*. 


**Key References for Baal-Berith**: 
JDG 8:33, JDG 9:4. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Baal-Berith” are translated as 
*as* (1x), *of Baal-berith* (1x).
