# Sikkuth
**Sikkuth**. 
An Assyrian god associated with the planet Saturn (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*shrine*, *tabernacle*. 


**Key References for Sikkuth**: 
AMO 5:26. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Sikkuth” are translated as 
*Sakkuth* (1x).
