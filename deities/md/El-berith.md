# El-Berith
**El-Berith**. 
A god worshipped by people of Shechem (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Baal-Berith*, *god Berith*. 


**Key References for El-Berith**: 
JDG 9:46. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “El-Berith” are translated as 
*of El-berith* (1x).
