# Amon
**Amon**. 
An Egyptian deity (Jeremiah 46:25) (acai). 


**Key References for Amon**: 
JER 46:25, NAM 3:8. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Amon” are translated as 
*Amon* (1x), *than Thebes* (1x).
