# Ashima
**Ashima**. 
A deity worshiped by people of Hamath who were relocated to Samaria by the king of Assyria (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Ashmah*. 


**Key References for Ashima**: 
2KI 17:30. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Ashima” are translated as 
*Ashima* (1x).
