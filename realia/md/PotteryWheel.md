# Pottery Wheel
**Pottery Wheel**. 
A flat, horizontal wheel used by a potter to make pottery by spinning clay (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Potters Wheel*. 


**Key References for Pottery Wheel**: 
JER 18:3. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Pottery Wheel” are translated as 
*the wheel* (1x).
