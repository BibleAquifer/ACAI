# Purple Cloth
**Purple Cloth**. 
A cloth dyed with murex shellfish dye, often linked to wealth and royalty (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Crimson Yarn*, *Reddish-Purple*, *Scarlet Cloth*. 


**Key References for Purple Cloth**: 
EXO 25:4, JDG 8:26, MRK 15:17, REV 18:12.
