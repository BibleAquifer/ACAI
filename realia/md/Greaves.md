# Greaves
**Greaves**. 
Metal coverings for the lower legs used by soldiers for protection (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Armor*. 


**Key References for Greaves**: 
1SA 17:6. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Greaves” are translated as 
*and he had armor* (1x).
