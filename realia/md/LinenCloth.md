# Linen Cloth
**Linen Cloth**. 
A large piece of cloth, usually made from linen (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Linen Sheet*, *Sheet*. 


**Key References for Linen Cloth**: 
ACT 10:11, ACT 11:5. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Linen Cloth” are translated as 
*sheet* (2x).
