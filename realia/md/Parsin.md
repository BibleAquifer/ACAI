# Parsin
**Parsin**. 
Possibly a measure of weight indicating either half a mina or half a shekel (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Peres*. 


**Key References for Parsin**: 
DAN 5:25, DAN 5:28. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Parsin” are translated as 
*PARSIN* (1x), *PERES* (1x).
