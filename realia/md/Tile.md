# Tile
**Tile**. 
A thin slab or bent piece of baked clay; used to cover a roof (ubsdgnt). 
Other forms include: 
*Roof Tile*. 


**Key References for Tile**: 
LUK 5:19. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Tile” are translated as 
*tiles* (1x).
