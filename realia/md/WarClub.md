# War Club
**War Club**. 
A short wooden rod used for hunting (ubsdbh). 
Other forms include: 
*Club*, *Cudgel*, *Mace*. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “War Club” are translated as 
*the clubs* (2x), *club My* (1x), *of slaughter* (1x), *A club* (1x).
