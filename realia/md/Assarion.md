# Assarion
**Assarion**. 
A small Roman copper coin worth 1/16 of a denarius (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Penny*. 


**Key References for Assarion**: 
MAT 10:29, LUK 12:6. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Assarion” are translated as 
*for a penny* (1x), *pennies* (1x).
