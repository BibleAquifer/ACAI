# Rafts
**Rafts**. 
A floating platform made by tying logs or inflated animal skins together (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Floats*. 


**Key References for Rafts**: 
1KI 5:23. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Rafts” are translated as 
*as rafts* (1x).
