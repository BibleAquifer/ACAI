# Fortress
**Fortress**. 
A large, strong building or group of buildings built to protect a town, often made of stone (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Antonia Fortress*, *Refuge*, *Stronghold*. 


**Key References for Fortress**: 
2SA 22:2, PSA 18:3, ISA 25:4, 2CO 10:4.
