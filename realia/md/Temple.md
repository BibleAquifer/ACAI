# Temple
**Temple**. 
The building in Jerusalem where the Hebrew people worshipped God (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Door*, *Ezekiels Temple*, *Gate*, *House*, *Sanctuary*. 


**Key References for Temple**: 
MAT 21:12, MRK 11:15, LUK 19:45, JHN 2:14.
