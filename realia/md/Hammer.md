# Hammer
**Hammer**. 
A tool with a handle and a heavy head, used for breaking stones, pounding nails, or shaping metal (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Beaten Work*, *Hammered Sheets*, *Hammered Work*. 


**Key References for Hammer**: 
JDG 5:26, 1KI 6:7.
