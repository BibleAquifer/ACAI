# Ink
**Ink**. 
A dark liquid used for writing or marking (acai). 


**Key References for Ink**: 
2CO 3:3, 2JN 1:12, 3JN 1:13. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Ink” are translated as 
*with ink* (1x), *ink* (2x).
