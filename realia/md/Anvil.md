# Anvil
**Anvil**. 
A heavy piece of metal used as a base for hammering and shaping other metal pieces (acai). 


**Key References for Anvil**: 
ISA 41:7. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Anvil” are translated as 
*the anvil* (1x).
