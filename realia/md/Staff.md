# Staff
**Staff**. 
A wooden stick used for walking, herding, or as a symbol of authority (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Club*, *Rod*, *Scepter*, *Stick*. 


**Key References for Staff**: 
EXO 12:11, PSA 23:4.
