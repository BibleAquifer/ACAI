# Soap
**Soap**. 
A substance made from mixing ashes of soda plants with oils or fats, used for cleaning (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Lye*. 


**Key References for Soap**: 
JER 2:22, MAL 3:2. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Soap” are translated as 
*soap the* (1x), *soap* (1x).
