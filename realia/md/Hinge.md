# Hinge
**Hinge**. 
A pivot that allows a door or gate to open and close (acai). 


**Key References for Hinge**: 
PRO 26:14. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Hinge” are translated as 
*folding* (1x), *hinges* (1x), *its hinges* (1x).
