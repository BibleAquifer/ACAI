# Bed
**Bed**. 
A low platform with four legs, slightly longer than a person, used for sitting or sleeping (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Bier*, *Cot*, *Couch*, *Pallet*, *Stretcher*. 


**Key References for Bed**: 
2SA 11:2, PSA 6:7, MRK 2:11, JHN 5:8.
