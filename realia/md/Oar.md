# Oar
**Oar**. 
A long pole with a flat end used to row or steer a boat (acai). 


**Key References for Oar**: 
EZK 27:6, EZK 27:29. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Oar” are translated as 
*with oars* (1x), *your oars* (1x), *the oars* (1x).
