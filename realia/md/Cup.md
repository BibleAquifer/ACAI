# Cup
**Cup**. 
A container used for drinking liquids; the size can vary (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Bowl*. 


**Key References for Cup**: 
MAT 26:27, 1CO 11:25, REV 17:4. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Cup” are translated as 
*bowl* (1x), *of water* (1x).
