# A Half Shekel
**A Half Shekel**. 
A weight of about 5.75 grams, equal to 10 gerahs, used for measuring gold and silver (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Beka*. 


**Key References for A Half Shekel**: 
GEN 24:22, EXO 38:26. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “A Half Shekel” are translated as 
*a beka* (2x).
