# Wagon
**Wagon**. 
A vehicle with two or four wheels, used for carrying goods or people, pulled by animals (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Carriage*, *Cart*, *Chariot*, *Wheel*. 


**Key References for Wagon**: 
GEN 45:19, NUM 7:7, 1SA 6:7, 1CH 13:7.
