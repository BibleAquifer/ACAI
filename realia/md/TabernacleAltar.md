# Tabernacle Altar
**Tabernacle Altar**. 
A flat-topped structure used for burning sacrifices, originally made from rough stones and later also from metal (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Altar*, *Altar Tabernacle*. 


**Key References for Tabernacle Altar**: 
EXO 27:1, EXO 30:27, LEV 1:7, NUM 4:11.
