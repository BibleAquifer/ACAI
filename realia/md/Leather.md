# Leather
**Leather**. 
Animal skin prepared and preserved to be durable and flexible by removing the hair and preventing decay (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Hide*, *Maker of Leather*, *Of Leather*, *Tanner*. 


**Key References for Leather**: 
EXO 25:5, NUM 4:6, MAT 3:4.
