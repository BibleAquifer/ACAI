# Cage
**Cage**. 
A structure of bars or wires used to confine animals, with a top or door that can be closed (acai). 


**Key References for Cage**: 
EZK 19:9. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Cage” are translated as 
*they caged him* (1x).
