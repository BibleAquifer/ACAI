# Towel
**Towel**. 
A piece of cloth used for drying things, usually made of linen (acai). 


**Key References for Towel**: 
JHN 13:4, JHN 13:5. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Towel” are translated as 
*a towel* (1x), *towel* (1x).
