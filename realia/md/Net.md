# Net
**Net**. 
A tool made of interconnected ropes or strings, used for catching fish, birds, or other animals (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Dragnet*, *Snare*, *Trap*. 


**Key References for Net**: 
PSA 25:15, MIC 7:2, 1TI 3:7.
