# Kor
**Kor**. 
A Hebrew measure for liquids or dry goods, about 35 liters for liquids or 390 liters for dry goods (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Bath*, *Cor*. 


**Key References for Kor**: 
LUK 16:6, LUK 16:7. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Kor” are translated as 
*measures* (2x).
