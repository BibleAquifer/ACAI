# Arrow
**Arrow**. 
A slender, pointed weapon made of wood or iron, shot from a bow for hunting or warfare (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Dart*, *Flashing Arrows*, *Sons of the Quiver > Arrows*. 


**Key References for Arrow**: 
1SA 20:36, PSA 127:4, EZK 39:3, EPH 6:16.
