# Censer
**Censer**. 
A bowl used for burning incense or moving hot coals (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Censer*, *Firepan*. 


**Key References for Censer**: 
LEV 16:12, NUM 16:17, REV 8:3. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Censer” are translated as 
*censer* (2x).
