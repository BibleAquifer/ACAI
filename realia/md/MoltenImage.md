# Molten Image
**Molten Image**. 
An idol made by pouring melted metal into a mold, often used in the Bible in forbidden worship (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Female Calf*, *Heifer*, *Male Calf*. 


**Key References for Molten Image**: 
EXO 32:4, DEU 9:12, JDG 17:4, HOS 13:2.
