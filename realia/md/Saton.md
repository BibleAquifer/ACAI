# Saton
**Saton**. 
A Hebrew measure of grain, about twelve liters or a little less than half a bushel (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Batch*, *Measure*. 


**Key References for Saton**: 
MAT 13:33, LUK 13:21. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Saton” are translated as 
*measures* (2x).
