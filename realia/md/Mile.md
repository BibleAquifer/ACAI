# Mile
**Mile**. 
A Roman mile, which is about one and a half kilometers (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Kilometer*. 


**Key References for Mile**: 
MAT 5:41. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Mile” are translated as
