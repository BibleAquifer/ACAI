# Pin
**Pin**. 
A small, pointed object used in weaving to tighten threads (acai). 


**Key References for Pin**: 
JDG 16:14. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Pin” are translated as 
*with a pin* (1x), *the pin* (1x).
