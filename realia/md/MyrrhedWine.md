# Myrrhed Wine
**Myrrhed Wine**. 
Wine mixed with myrrh to make a strong, numbing drink (acai). 


**Key References for Myrrhed Wine**: 
MRK 15:23. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Myrrhed Wine” are translated as 
*wine* (1x), *mixed with myrrh* (1x).
