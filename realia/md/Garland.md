# Garland
**Garland**. 
A circular decoration made of leaves and flowers, worn on the head (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Wreath*. 


**Key References for Garland**: 
PRO 1:9, ACT 14:13, 1CO 9:25. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Garland” are translated as 
*wreaths* (1x).
