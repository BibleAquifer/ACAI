# Trap
**Trap**. 
A device for catching animals, often hidden and designed to ensnare (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Fowler*, *Hunter*, *Noose*, *Pitfall*, *Snare*, *To Ensnare*, *To Entrap*. 


**Key References for Trap**: 
JOS 23:13, JOB 18:10, ROM 11:9.
