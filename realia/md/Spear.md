# Spear
**Spear**. 
A long wooden rod with a sharp iron or metal point, used for piercing by thrusting (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Short Spear*, *Spearhead*. 


**Key References for Spear**: 
NUM 25:7, 1SA 17:7, 1SA 19:9, JHN 19:34.
