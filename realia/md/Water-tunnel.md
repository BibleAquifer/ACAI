# Water-Tunnel
**Water-Tunnel**. 
A channel cut in rock to bring water from a spring into a city (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Conduit*. 


**Key References for Water-Tunnel**: 
2SA 5:8. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Water-Tunnel” are translated as 
*must use the water shaft* (1x).
