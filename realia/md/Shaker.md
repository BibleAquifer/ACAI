# Shaker
**Shaker**. 
A musical instrument that makes a rattling sound when shaken (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Rattle*. 


**Key References for Shaker**: 
2SA 6:5. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Shaker” are translated as 
*sistrums* (1x).
