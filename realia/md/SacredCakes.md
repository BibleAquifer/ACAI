# Sacred Cakes
**Sacred Cakes**. 
Baked items made from bread dough that were offered to false gods (acai). 


**Key References for Sacred Cakes**: 
JER 7:18, JER 44:19. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Sacred Cakes” are translated as 
*cakes* (1x), *cakes in* (1x).
