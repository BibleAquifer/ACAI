# Chariot
**Chariot**. 
An open vehicle with two or four wheels, pulled by horses, and used in war or for travel (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Chariot Horses*, *Charioteer*, *Chariotry*, *Horseman*. 


**Key References for Chariot**: 
EXO 14:7, 2KI 2:11, ACT 8:38.
