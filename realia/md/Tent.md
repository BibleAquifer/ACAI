# Tent
**Tent**. 
A portable shelter made of cloth or skins, supported by poles and secured with cords to stakes (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Body*, *Inner Room*, *Tentmaker*. 


**Key References for Tent**: 
GEN 18:9, EXO 33:7, NUM 24:5, 2CO 5:1.
