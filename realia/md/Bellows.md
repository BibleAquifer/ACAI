# Bellows
**Bellows**. 
A tool with leather and cylinders used to blow air into a furnace to make it hotter (acai). 


**Key References for Bellows**: 
JER 6:29. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Bellows” are translated as 
*The bellows* (1x).
