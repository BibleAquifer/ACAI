# Pound
**Pound**. 
A Roman unit of weight, about twelve ounces or 325 grams (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Pint*. 


**Key References for Pound**: 
JHN 12:3, JHN 19:39. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Pound” are translated as 
*about a pint* (1x), *seventy-five pounds* (1x).
