# Gerah
**Gerah**. 
A small unit of weight, about 0.6 grams, used for measuring gold and silver (acai). 


**Key References for Gerah**: 
EXO 30:13, LEV 27:25, NUM 3:47. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Gerah” are translated as 
*gerahs* (5x).
