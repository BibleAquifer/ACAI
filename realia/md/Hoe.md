# Hoe
**Hoe**. 
A tool with a flat metal blade and a long wooden handle, used for loosening soil and removing weeds (acai). 


**Key References for Hoe**: 
1SA 13:20, ISA 7:25. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Hoe” are translated as 
*by the hoe* (1x).
