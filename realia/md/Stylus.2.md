# Stylus
**Stylus**. 
A tool with a pointed end used for writing or marking surfaces; its meaning is uncertain (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Marker*. 


**Key References for Stylus**: 
ISA 44:13. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Stylus” are translated as 
*with a stylus* (1x).
