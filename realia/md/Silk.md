# Silk
**Silk**. 
A type of expensive cloth (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Expensive Cloth*. 


**Key References for Silk**: 
EZK 16:10, EZK 16:13. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Silk” are translated as 
*you with silk* (1x), *silk* (1x).
