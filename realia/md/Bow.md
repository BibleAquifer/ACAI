# Bow
**Bow**. 
A curved piece of wood with a string, used to shoot arrows for hunting or in battle (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Archer*, *Bowman*. 


**Key References for Bow**: 
GEN 21:20, 2KI 13:15, PSA 7:13, ZEC 9:10.
