# Belt
**Belt**. 
A strip of linen or leather worn around the waist to hold a robe in place and sometimes used to carry money or other items (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Girdle*, *Waistband*. 


**Key References for Belt**: 
2KI 1:8, JER 13:1, MAT 3:4, ACT 21:11.
