# Birthstool
**Birthstool**. 
A set of two stone pieces used by women in Egypt to sit or kneel on while giving birth (acai). 


**Key References for Birthstool**: 
EXO 1:16. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Birthstool” are translated as 
*the birthstools* (1x).
