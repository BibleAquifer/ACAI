# Cross
**Cross**. 
A wooden pole with a crosspiece, used in ancient times for executing people by nailing them to it (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*To Crucify*, *To Crucify Together With*. 


**Key References for Cross**: 
MAT 27:32, LUK 23:33, JHN 19:17.
