# Plate
**Plate**. 
A flat dish for prepared food (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Dish*, *Platter*. 


**Key References for Plate**: 
MAT 23:25, MRK 6:28, LUK 11:39. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Plate” are translated as 
*dish* (2x), *a platter* (4x).
