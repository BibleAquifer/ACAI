# Oil of Nard
**Oil of Nard**. 
A fragrant oil made from the nard plant, used as perfume (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Perfume of Nard*. 


**Key References for Oil of Nard**: 
MRK 14:3, JHN 12:3. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Oil of Nard” are translated as 
*nard* (2x).
