# Anointing Oil
**Anointing Oil**. 
High quality oil from olives, used in food, medicine, and religious rituals (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Oil*, *Ointment*, *Olive Oil*. 


**Key References for Anointing Oil**: 
GEN 35:14, EXO 30:31, 1SA 10:1.
