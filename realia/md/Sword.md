# Sword
**Sword**. 
A weapon with a long iron blade and a hilt with a hand-guard, used for cutting or thrusting in warfare (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Drawn Sword*. 


**Key References for Sword**: 
GEN 3:24, JOS 5:13, MAT 26:51, REV 19:15.
