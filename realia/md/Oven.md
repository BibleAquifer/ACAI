# Oven
**Oven**. 
A dome-shaped clay structure heated with wood or dried grass to bake bread (acai). 


**Key References for Oven**: 
MAT 6:30, LUK 12:28. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Oven” are translated as 
*the furnace* (2x).
