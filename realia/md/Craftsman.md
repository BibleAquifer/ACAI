# Craftsman
**Craftsman**. 
A person skilled in making objects from materials like stone, wood, or metal often by carving or engraving (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Carving*, *Engraving*. 


**Key References for Craftsman**: 
DEU 27:15, ISA 44:12.
