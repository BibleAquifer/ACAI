# Rudder
**Rudder**. 
A large plank at the back of a ship used to steer and direct its course (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Boat*. 


**Key References for Rudder**: 
ACT 27:40, JAS 3:4. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Rudder” are translated as 
*rudders* (1x), *rudder* (1x).
