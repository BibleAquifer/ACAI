# Pinnacle
**Pinnacle**. 
The tip or highest point of a building (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Summit*. 


**Key References for Pinnacle**: 
MAT 4:5, LUK 4:9. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Pinnacle” are translated as 
*pinnacle* (2x).
