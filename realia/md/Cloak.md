# Cloak
**Cloak**. 
A long, loose outer garment that reached to the feet and was held in place by a sash or fasteners (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*A Robe Reaching to the Feet*, *Coat*, *Long Robe*, *Mantle*, *Robe*. 


**Key References for Cloak**:
