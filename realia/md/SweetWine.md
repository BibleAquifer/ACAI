# Sweet Wine
**Sweet Wine**. 
A new, sweet wine that is still fermenting (acai). 


**Key References for Sweet Wine**: 
ACT 2:13. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Sweet Wine” are translated as 
*on new wine* (1x).
