# Apron
**Apron**. 
A cloth or leather garment worn around the waist to protect clothes (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Leather Apron*. 


**Key References for Apron**: 
EZK 23:15, MAT 3:4, ACT 19:12. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Apron” are translated as 
*aprons* (1x).
