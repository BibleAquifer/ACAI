# Wristband
**Wristband**. 
The wristband was a strip of sewn cloth worn on the wrist (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Magic Charm*. 


**Key References for Wristband**: 
EZK 13:18, EZK 13:20. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Wristband” are translated as 
*magic charms* (1x), *the magic charms* (1x).
