# Step
**Step**. 
A flat surface you step on as part of a staircase (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Flight of Steps*, *Stairs*, *Steps*. 


**Key References for Step**: 
ACT 21:35, ACT 21:40. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Step” are translated as 
*steps* (2x).
