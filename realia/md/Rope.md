# Rope
**Rope**. 
A thick cord made by twisting or braiding several strands of material like palm fibers, linen, or reeds, used for tying and securing things (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Bands*, *Cord*, *String*. 


**Key References for Rope**: 
JOS 2:15, 2SA 17:13, JHN 2:15.
