# Mount Hor (of Edom)
**Mount Hor (of Edom)**. 
A mountain on the Edomite border where Aaron died (Numbers 33:38–39). 
Other forms include: 
*Hor*, *Mount Hor*. 




Other information about Mount Hor (of Edom):


* Is located within the following region(s): 
[Edom](Edom.md).
