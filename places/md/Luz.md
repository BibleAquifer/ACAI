# Luz (of Israel)
**Luz (of Israel)**. 
A town, later known as Bethel, on the boundary of the territories of Ephraim and Benjamin. 
Other forms include: 
*Bethel*, *Luz*. 
Luz (of Israel) is also referred to as: 
[Bethel (of Ephraim)](Bethel.md). 
Luz (of Israel) is a subentry. The primary entry is 
[Bethel (of Ephraim)](Bethel.md).
