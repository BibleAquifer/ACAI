# Water Gate
**Water Gate**. 
An eastern gate in Jerusalem where Ezra read the Law before the returned exiles (Nehemiah 8:1–3). 
Other forms include: 
*Gate*, *Water*. 




Other information about Water Gate:


* Is located within the following region(s): 
[Jerusalem](Jerusalem.md).
