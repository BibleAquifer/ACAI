# Avva
**Avva**. 
A town, also known as Ivvah; its inhabitants were relocated to Samaria by the king of Assyria (2 Kings 17:24). 
Other forms include: 
*Arva*, *Ava*. 
Avva is also referred to as: 
[Ivvah](Ivvah.md). 
Avva is a subentry. The primary entry is 
[Ivvah](Ivvah.md). 




Other information about Avva:

[Ivvah](Ivvah.md).
