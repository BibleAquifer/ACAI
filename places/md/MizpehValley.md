# Valley of Mizpeh
**Valley of Mizpeh**. 
A location near Mount Hermon inhabited by the Hivites. 
Other forms include: 
*Mizpah*, *Mizpah Valley*, *Mizpeh*, *Valley of Mizpah*. 




Other information about Valley of Mizpeh:
