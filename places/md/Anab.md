# Anab
**Anab**. 
A town in the hill country of the territory of Judah where Joshua eliminated the Anakim (Joshua 11:21). 
Located in the tribal area of [Tribe of Judah](../../../groups/md/acai/Judah.md). 




Other information about Anab:
