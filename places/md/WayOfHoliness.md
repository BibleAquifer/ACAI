# Way of Holiness
**Way of Holiness**. 
A road envisioned in Isa 35:8 that leads to Zion. 
Other forms include: 
*Highway of Holiness*, *Holy Way*, *The way of holiness*. 




Other information about Way of Holiness:


* Is associated with the following location(s): 
[Zion](Zion.md).
