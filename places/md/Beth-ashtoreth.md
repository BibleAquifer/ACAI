# Beth-ashtoreth
**Beth-ashtoreth**. 
= town, possibly to be identified with Beeshterah, Ashtaroth and Karnaim; located in Bashan; place where Chedor-Laomer defeated the Rephaim. 
Other forms include: 
*house of Ashtoroth*, *temple of Ashtoroth*, *temple of the Ashoreths*.
