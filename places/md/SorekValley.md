# Valley of Sorek
**Valley of Sorek**. 
A valley west of Jerusalem; home of Delila, Samson's lover (Judges 16:4). 
Other forms include: 
*Sorek Valley*, *wadi of Sorek*. 




Other information about Valley of Sorek:


* Is located near the following area(s): 
[Jerusalem](Jerusalem.md).
