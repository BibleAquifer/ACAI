# Ariel
**Ariel**. 
Another name for the city of Jerusalem. 
Other forms include: 
*Jerusalem*. 
Ariel is also referred to as: 
[Jerusalem](Jerusalem.md). 
Ariel is a subentry. The primary entry is 
[Jerusalem](Jerusalem.md).
