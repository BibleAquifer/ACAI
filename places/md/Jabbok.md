# Jabbok
**Jabbok**. 
A river flowing west from its source near Rabbath-Ammon to empty into the Jordan River about 40 km north of the Dead Sea. 
Other forms include: 
*Jabbok River*, *Jabbok Valley*, *River Jabbok*, *Wadi Jabbok*.
