# Gadara (of the Decapolis)
**Gadara (of the Decapolis)**. 
A Decapolis city southwest of the Sea of Galilee; modern-day Umm Qais. 
Other forms include: 
*Gadara*, *Gadarenes*, *Gerasa*, *Gergesene*, *Gergesenes*, *Var*. 
Gadara (of the Decapolis) is also referred to as: 
[Gerasa](Gerasa.md).

[Gerasa](Gerasa.md).
