# Ham (of Zuzim)
**Ham (of Zuzim)**. 
A location inhabited by the Zuzim, which was subdued by Chedorlaomer and the allied kings. 
Other forms include: 
*Ammon*, *Ham*. 
Ham (of Zuzim) is also referred to as: 
[Ammon](Ammon.md). 
Ham (of Zuzim) is a subentry. The primary entry is 
[Ammon](Ammon.md).
