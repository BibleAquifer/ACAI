# En-mishpat
**En-mishpat**. 
= spring; near Dead Sea.

*En Mishpat*, *En-mishpat*, *Kadesh-Barnea*. 
En-mishpat is also referred to as: 
[Kadesh-barnea](Kadesh-barnea.md). 
En-mishpat is a subentry. The primary entry is 
[Kadesh-barnea](Kadesh-barnea.md).
