# Shihor
**Shihor**. 
A river in the northeastern delta region of Egypt. 
Other forms include: 
*Brook of Egypt*, *Egypt*, *River*, *Sihor*. 
Shihor is also referred to as: 
[Brook of Egypt](BrookOfEgypt.md).
