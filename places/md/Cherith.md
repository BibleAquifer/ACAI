# Cherith
**Cherith**. 
A wadi east of the Jordan River where the prophet Elijah hid from king Ahab and was fed by ravens (1KI 17:2–7). 
Other forms include: 
*Brook Cherith*, *Kerith Brook*, *Kerith Ravine*, *Kerith Valley*, *Wadi Cherith*, *Wadi Kerith*.
