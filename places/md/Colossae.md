# Colossae
**Colossae**. 
A city in Phrygia in Asia Minor near Hierapolis and Laodicea; the letter to the Colossians was addressed to the church there. 
Other forms include: 
*Colosse*, *Colossians*, *Var*, *[ ]*. 
Colossae is also referred to as: 
Colossae (Group). 




Other information about Colossae:


* Is located within the following region(s): 
[Phrygia](Phrygia.md). 
* Is located near the following area(s): 
[Laodicea](Laodicea.md), [Hierapolis](Hierapolis.md).
