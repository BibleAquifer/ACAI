# Succoth (of Egypt)
**Succoth (of Egypt)**. 
A location in eastern Egypt, between Ramees and Etham; first campsite of the Israelites on their way from Egypt to Canaan. 
Other forms include: 
*Succoth*, *Sukkoth*. 




Other information about Succoth (of Egypt):


* Is located within the following region(s): 
[Egypt](Egypt.md).
