# Cush (Nation)
**Cush (Nation)**. 
An ethnic group and their territory; regarded as descendants of Cush, son of Ham. 
Other forms include: 
*Cush*, *Cushite*, *Cushitess*, *Ethiopia*, *Ethiopians*. 
Cush (Nation) is also referred to as: 
Cush (Group), [Cush (Nation)](Cush.2.md).
