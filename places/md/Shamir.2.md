# Shamir (of Ephraim)
**Shamir (of Ephraim)**. 
A town in the hill country of the territory of Ephraim; residence of Tola, son of Puah, judge of Israel. 
Located in the tribal area of [Tribe of Ephraim](../../../groups/md/acai/Ephraim.md).
