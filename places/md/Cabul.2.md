# Cabul (of Galilee)
**Cabul (of Galilee)**. 
A region in Galilee given to Hiram king of Tyre by Solomon (1 Kings 9:13). 
Other forms include: 
*Cabul*, *Land of Cabul*, *Land of Kabul*. 




Other information about Cabul (of Galilee):


* Is located within the following region(s): 
[Galilee](Galilee.md).
