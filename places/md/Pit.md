# Pit (Hell)
**Pit (Hell)**. 
A non-physical place. 
Other forms include: 
*Hell*, *or pit*. 
Pit (Hell) is also referred to as: 
[Hell](Hell.md). 
Pit (Hell) is a subentry. The primary entry is 
[Hell](Hell.md).
