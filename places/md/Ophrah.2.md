# Ophrah (of Manasseh)
**Ophrah (of Manasseh)**. 
A town in the territory of Manasseh. 
Other forms include: 
*Hapharaim*, *Ophrah*. 
Ophrah (of Manasseh) is also referred to as: 
[Hapharaim](Hapharaim.md).
