# Zeboiim
**Zeboiim**. 
A town near the Dead Sea; destroyed with Sodom and Gomorrah. 
Other forms include: 
*Zeboim*, *Zeboyim*. 




Other information about Zeboiim:


* Is located near the following area(s): 
[Dead Sea](SaltSea.md).
