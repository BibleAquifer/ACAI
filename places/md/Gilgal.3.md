# Gilgal (near Jericho)
**Gilgal (near Jericho)**. 
A town close to Jericho where the Israelites first camped after crossing the Jordan River and set up twelve stones as a monument; it later became the location of a cultic center. 
Other forms include: 
*Gilgal*.
