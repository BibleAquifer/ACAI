# Gate of Jeshanah
**Gate of Jeshanah**. 
A gate in Jerusalem which was repaired by Johoida and Meshullam (Nehemiah 3:6). 
Other forms include: 
*Ancient Gate*, *Gate of Yeshanah*, *Jeshanah Gate*, *Old City Gate*, *Old Gate*, *Yeshanah Gate*.
