# Wilderness of Sin
**Wilderness of Sin**. 
A desert in the Sinai Peninsula, between the Sea of Reeds and Dophkah, where the Israelites encamped on their way from Egypt to Canaan. 
Other forms include: 
*Desert of Sin*, *Sin*.
