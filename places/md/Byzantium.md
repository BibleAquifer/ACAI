# Byzantium
**Byzantium**. 
A Greek city at the southern end of the Bosphorus; modern-day Istanbul. In 330, Emperor Constantine moved the capital of the Roman Empire here and renamed the city Constantinople. 
Other forms include: 
*Constantinople*, *Istanbul*.
