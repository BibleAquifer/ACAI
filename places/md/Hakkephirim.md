# Hakkephirim
**Hakkephirim**. 
A location in the valley of Ono where Nehemiah was to meet Sanballat and Geshem. 
Other forms include: 
*Chephirim*, *Kephirim*, *Ono*, *villages*. 
Hakkephirim is also referred to as: 
[Ono](Ono.md). 
Hakkephirim is a subentry. The primary entry is 
[Ono](Ono.md).
