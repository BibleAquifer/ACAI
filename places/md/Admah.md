# Admah
**Admah**. 
A town near the Dead Sea destroyed with Sodom and Gomorrah (Deuteronomy 29:23). 
Other forms include: 
*Adman*. 




Other information about Admah:


* Is located near the following area(s): 
[Dead Sea](SaltSea.md).
