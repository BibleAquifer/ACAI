# Bozkath
**Bozkath**. 
A town in the foothills of the territory of Judah; home of Jedidah, mother of Josiah. 
Located in the tribal area of [Tribe of Judah](../../../groups/md/acai/Judah.md). 
Other forms include: 
*Boscath*.
