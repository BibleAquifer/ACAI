# Sharon Plain
**Sharon Plain**. 
A plain along the coast of the Mediterranean. 
Other forms include: 
*Saron*, *Sharon*, *Sharonite*. 
Sharon Plain is also referred to as: 
Sharon (Group), [Sharon Plain](Sharon.2.md).
