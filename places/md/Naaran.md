# Naaran
**Naaran**. 
A location in the Jordan Valley, in the territory of Ephraim; also known as Naarah. 
Located in the tribal area of [Tribe of Ephraim](../../../groups/md/acai/Ephraim.md).

*Naarah*, *Naarath*.
