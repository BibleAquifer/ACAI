# Sharuhen
**Sharuhen**. 
A town in the territory of Simeon. 
Located in the tribal area of [Tribe of Simeon](../../../groups/md/acai/Simeon.md). 
Other forms include: 
*Shaaraim*, *Shilhim*. 
Sharuhen is also referred to as: 
[Shaaraim (of Simeon)](Shaaraim.2.md), [Shilhim](Shilhim.md).
