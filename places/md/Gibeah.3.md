# Gibeah (of Phinehas)
**Gibeah (of Phinehas)**. 
A hill or town in the hill country of Ephraim were Eleazar, son of Aaron, was buried. 
Other forms include: 
*Gibeah of Phinehas*, *Hill of Phinehas*.
