# Hephzibah
**Hephzibah**. 
= new name of Zion, given to her by God after the exile. 
Other forms include: 
*Jerusalem*, *My Delight Is in Her*. 
Hephzibah is also referred to as: 
[Jerusalem](Jerusalem.md). 
Hephzibah is a subentry. The primary entry is 
[Jerusalem](Jerusalem.md).
