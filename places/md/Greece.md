# Greece
**Greece**. 
A nation on the southern end of the Balkan Peninsula. 
Located in the tribal area of [Greeks](../../../groups/md/acai/Greek.md). 
Other forms include: 
*Hellenist*, *Javan*. 
Greece is also referred to as: 
Hellenist (Group), [Javan](Javan.md).
