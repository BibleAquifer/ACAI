# Nazareth
**Nazareth**. 
A town in south central Galilee, where Jesus lived as a child (Matthew 2:23). 
Other forms include: 
*Nazarene*. 




Other information about Nazareth:


* Is located within the following region(s): 
[Galilee](Galilee.md).
