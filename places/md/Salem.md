# Salem
**Salem**. 
A town ruled by Melchizedek during the time of Abraham; to be identified with Jerusalem. 
Other forms include: 
*Jerusalem*. 
Salem is also referred to as: 
[Jerusalem](Jerusalem.md). 
Salem is a subentry. The primary entry is 
[Jerusalem](Jerusalem.md).
