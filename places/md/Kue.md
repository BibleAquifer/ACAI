# Kue
**Kue**. 
A kingdom located in southeastern Turkey, known as Cilicia in Greco-Roman period; famous for its horses. 
Other forms include: 
*Cilicia*, *Keveh*, *Que*. 




Other information about Kue:


* May refer to the same location as: 
[Cilicia](Cilicia.md).
