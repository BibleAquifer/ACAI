# Persia
**Persia**. 
A nation and its territory, which was greatly expanded during the reign of Cyrus I. 
Other forms include: 
*Apharsite*, *Apharsites*, *Paras*, *Persian*, *Persians*. 
Persia is also referred to as: 
Persia (Group).
