# Bamoth-baal
**Bamoth-baal**. 
A location in Moab from where Balaam was to curse the Israelites. 
Other forms include: 
*citizens of heights of*, *heights*, *high places of Baal*, *lords of the high places*. 
Bamoth-baal is also referred to as: 
[Baal-bamoth](Baal-bamoth.md).
