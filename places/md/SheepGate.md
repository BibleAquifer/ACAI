# Sheep Gate
**Sheep Gate**. 
A gate in the walls of Jerusalem, near the pool of Bethesda, which was rebuilt by the high priest Eliashib and his fellow priests (Nehemiah 3:1). 
Other forms include: 
*Gate*, *Sheep*.
