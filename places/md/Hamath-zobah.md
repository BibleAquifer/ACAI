# Hamath-zobah
**Hamath-zobah**. 
= town, area; possibly regions of Hamath and Zoba together; in Syria. 
Other forms include: 
*Hamath*, *Hamath Zobah*, *Hamath-zobah*, *Zobah*, *Zobah-Hamath*. 
Hamath-zobah is also referred to as: 
[Zobah](Zobah.md). 
Hamath-zobah is a subentry. The primary entry is 
[Zobah](Zobah.md).
