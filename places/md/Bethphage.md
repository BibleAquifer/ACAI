# Bethphage
**Bethphage**. 
A village east of Bethany (on the Mount of Olives), where Jesus sent two of his disciples to retrieve a donkey for him to ride upon as he entered Jerusalem (Matthew 21:1–11; Mark 11:1–11; Luke 19:28–40).
