# Meshech
**Meshech**. 
A nation named after Meshech, son of Japheth and grandson of Noah. 
Located in the tribal area of [Asians](../../../groups/md/acai/Asia.md). 
Other forms include: 
*Mesech*, *Meshach*, *Meshech-Tubal*, *Meshek*, *Tubal*.
