# Dibon (of Moab)
**Dibon (of Moab)**. 
A town in Moab said to be built by the tribe of Gad, but also attributed to the tribe of Reuben. 
Other forms include: 
*Dibon*, *Dibon-Gad*. 




Other information about Dibon (of Moab):


* Is located within the following region(s): 
[Moab](Moab.md).
