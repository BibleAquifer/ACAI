# Horonaim (of Moab)
**Horonaim (of Moab)**. 
A town in Moab mentioned in prophetic oracles against Moab (Isa 15:5; Jer 48:3–5; Jer 48:34). 
Other forms include: 
*Horonaim*, *Horonaim [LXX]*, *–*. 




Other information about Horonaim (of Moab):

[Moab](Moab.md).
