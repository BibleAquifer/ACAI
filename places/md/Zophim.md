# Zophim
**Zophim**. 
A field at the top of Mount Pisgah in Moab; one of the places from where Balaam was to curse Israel. 
Other forms include: 
*Lookout Field*. 




Other information about Zophim:


* Is located within the following region(s): 
[Pisgah](Pisgah.md), [Moab](Moab.md).
