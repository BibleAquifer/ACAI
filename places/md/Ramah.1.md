# Ramah (of Benjamin)
**Ramah (of Benjamin)**. 
A town in the territory of Benjamin, northwest of Jerusalem, where the prophetess Deborah would judge Israel (Judges 4:5). 
Other forms include: 
*Rama*, *Ramah*.
