# Beautiful Gate
**Beautiful Gate**. 
A gate of Herod's Temple in Jerusalem where a lame man was healed by Peter and John (Acts 3:1–10). 
Other forms include: 
*Beautiful*, *Gate*, *gate called Beautiful*.
