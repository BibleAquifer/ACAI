# Leb-kamai
**Leb-kamai**. 
A cryptic name for Chaldea, a region in southern Babylonia. 
Other forms include: 
*Babylonia*, *Leb-qamai*, *in the midst*. 
Leb-kamai is also referred to as: 
[Babylonia](Babylonia.md), [Chaldea](Chaldea.md).
