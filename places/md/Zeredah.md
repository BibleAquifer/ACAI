# Zeredah (in the Jordan Valley)
**Zeredah (in the Jordan Valley)**. 
A town in the Jordan Valley, in the territory of Ephraim. 
Located in the tribal area of [Tribe of Ephraim](../../../groups/md/acai/Ephraim.md).
