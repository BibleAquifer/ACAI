# Harosheth-hagoyim
**Harosheth-hagoyim**. 
= location in north of Israel; residence of Sisera, army commander of Jabin king of Hazor. 
Other forms include: 
*Harosheth of the Gentiles*, *Harosheth of the Nations*, *Harosheth-Haggoyim*, *Harosheth-ha-goiim*.
