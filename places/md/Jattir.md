# Jattir
**Jattir**. 
A Levitical town in the territory of Judah; assigned to the clan of Aaron. 
Located in the tribal area of [Tribe of Judah](../../../groups/md/acai/Judah.md). 
Other forms include: 
*Ithrite*. 




Other information about Jattir:
