# Libya
**Libya**. 
A country in North Africa; Libyans were among those present at Pentecost (Acts 2:10). 
Other forms include: 
*Chub*, *Kub*, *Libyan*, *Libyans*, *Lubim*. 




Other information about Libya:


* Is located near the following area(s): 
[Egypt](Egypt.md).
