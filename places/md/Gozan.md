# Gozan
**Gozan**. 
A town along the Habor River in Assyria; one of the places where the exiles from Samaria were relocated. 
Other forms include: 
*Gozan River*, *River of Gozan*. 




Other information about Gozan:


* Is located within the following region(s): 
[Assyria](Assyria.md).
