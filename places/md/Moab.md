# Moab
**Moab**. 
A nation and its territory situated east of the Dead Sea; regarded as founded by a son of Lot. 
Located in the tribal area of [Edomites](../../../groups/md/acai/Edom.md). 
Other forms include: 
*Moabite*, *Moabites*, *Wilderness of Moab*.
