# Rehob (in the North)
**Rehob (in the North)**. 
A town on the border between Dan and Syria; also known as Beth-Rehob. 
Other forms include: 
*Beth-Rehob*, *Rehob*. 
Rehob (in the North) is also referred to as: 
[Beth-rehob](Beth-rehob.md).

[Beth-rehob](Beth-rehob.md).
