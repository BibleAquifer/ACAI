# Beth-millo
**Beth-millo**. 
A location near or in Shechem where Abimelech was crowned king. 
Other forms include: 
*Millo*, *house of Millo*, *house of the Millo*. 




Other information about Beth-millo:


* Is located near the following area(s): 
[Shechem](Shechem.md).
