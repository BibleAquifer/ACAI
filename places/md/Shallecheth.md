# Shallecheth
**Shallecheth**. 
A gate on the western side of the temple in Jerusalem; also known as the West Gate. 
Other forms include: 
*Shallecheth Gate*, *Shalleketh*, *Shalleketh Gate*. 
Shallecheth is also referred to as: 
[West Gate](WestGate.md).
