# The Hollow
**The Hollow**. 
Literally "mortar"; exact meaning unknown; possibly a relatively low part of Jerusalem used as market area, referred to as such because of its mortar-like shape. 
Other forms include: 
*Hollow*, *Maktesh*, *Mortar*.
