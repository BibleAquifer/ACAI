# Arimathea
**Arimathea**. 
A city in Judea, northwest of Jerusalem; home to Joseph, who provided a tomb for Jesus after he was crucified (Matthew 27:57–61; Mark 15:42–47; Luke 23:50–56; John 19:38–42). 
Other forms include: 
*Arimathaea*.
