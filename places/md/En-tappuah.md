# En-tappuah
**En-tappuah**. 
= town, also known as Enam; territory of Judah. 
Other forms include: 
*Tappuah*, *spring of Tappuah*. 
En-tappuah is also referred to as: 
[Tappuah (of Ephraim)](Tappuah.md).

[Tappuah (of Ephraim)](Tappuah.md).
