# Dedan
**Dedan**. 
A location in the Arabian Peninsula; home of an ethnic group with the same name. 
Other forms include: 
*Dedanim*, *Dedanite*, *Dedanites*, *Rhodes*, *Rhodians*. 
Dedan is also referred to as: 
Dedan (Group).
