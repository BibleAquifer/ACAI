# Sela (of the Amorites)
**Sela (of the Amorites)**. 
A location on the border of the territory of the Amorites. 
Other forms include: 
*Cliff*, *Joktheel*, *Sela*, *Selah*, *rock*. 
Sela (of the Amorites) is also referred to as: 
[Cliff](Cliff.md), [Sela (of Edom)](Joktheel.2.md).
