# Ethiopia
**Ethiopia**. 
A country south of Egypt; approximately equivalent to Cush. 
Other forms include: 
*Cush*, *Cushites*, *Ethiopian*, *Ethiopians*. 
Ethiopia is also referred to as: 
Ethiopia (Group).
