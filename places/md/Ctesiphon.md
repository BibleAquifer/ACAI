# Ctesiphon
**Ctesiphon**. 
A city in Mesopotamia on the east bank of the Tigris River, southeast of modern-day Baghdad, Iraq. It was the capital of the Parthian and Sasanian empires. 
Other forms include: 
*Taysafun*, *Tusbun*.
