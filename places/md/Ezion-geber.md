# Ezion-geber
**Ezion-geber**. 
A seaport town on the Gulf of Aqaba, near Elath, where the Israelites camped on their journey from Egypt to Canaan. 
Other forms include: 
*Ezion-gaber*. 




Other information about Ezion-geber:


* Is located near the following area(s): 
[Elath](Elath.md).
