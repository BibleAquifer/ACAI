# Sea of Egypt
**Sea of Egypt**. 
Possibly a reference to the Red Sea which is shaped like a tongue; literally "sea of Egypt.". 
Other forms include: 
*Egyptian Sea*, *Gulf of Suez*, *Red Sea*. 




Other information about Sea of Egypt:


* May refer to the same location as: 
[Red Sea](RedSea.md).
