# Valley of Aven
**Valley of Aven**. 
A valley mentioned along with Damascus in Amos 1:5; probably to be identified with the Biqah Valley in present-day Lebanon (Andersen/Freedman). 
Other forms include: 
*Aven*, *Plain of Aven*, *Wicked Valley*.
