# Arabia Petraea
**Arabia Petraea**. 
The northwestern Arabian Peninsula, Sinai Peninsula, and the former territory of Nabatea; Paul traveled here after his conversion (Galatians 1:17). 
Other forms include: 
*Arabia*, *Arabia Petrea*.
