# Gilgal (near Shechem)
**Gilgal (near Shechem)**. 
A location close to Shechem and the Oak of Moreh. 
Other forms include: 
*Beth-Gilgal*, *Geliloth*, *Gibeath-Haaraloth*, *Gilgal*, *hill of the foreskins*, *house of Gilgal*.
