# Gischala
**Gischala**. 
A village in Upper Galilee, northwest of the Sea of Galilee; home to John ben Levi of Giscala, a leader in the First Jewish Revolt. 
Other forms include: 
*Gush Halav*, *Jish*, *el-Jish*.
