# Chalcedon
**Chalcedon**. 
A maritime city in the province of Bithynia, Asia Minor. Several church councils were held here, including the Fourth Ecumenical Council, the Council of Chalcedon, in 451. 
Other forms include: 
*Chalkedon*.
