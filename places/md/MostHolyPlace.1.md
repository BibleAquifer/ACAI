# Most Holy Place (of the Tabernacle)
**Most Holy Place (of the Tabernacle)**. 
The innermost room at the back of the tabernacle; location of the ark of the covenant; regarded as the holiest place. 




**Key References for Most Holy Place (of the Tabernacle)**: 
EXO 26:33, EXO 26:34.
