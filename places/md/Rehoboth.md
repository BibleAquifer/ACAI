# Rehoboth (Well)
**Rehoboth (Well)**. 
A well, dug by herdsmen of Isaac, which, unlike the wells of Esek and Sitnah, did not become a source of contention between them and the herdsmen of Gerar. 
Other forms include: 
*Rehoboth*.
