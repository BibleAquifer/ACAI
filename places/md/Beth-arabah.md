# Beth-arabah
**Beth-arabah**. 
A town in the wilderness on the boundary between the territories of Judah and Benjamin. 
Other forms include: 
*Arabah*, *Arbathite*. 
Beth-arabah is also referred to as: 
Arbathite (Group).
