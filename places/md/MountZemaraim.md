# Mount Zemaraim
**Mount Zemaraim**. 
A mountain in the Ephraimite hill country, in the northern kingdom of Israel, where Abijah, king of Judah, addressed Jeroboam, king of Israel, before battle (2 Chronicles13:4). 
Other forms include: 
*Zemaraim*.
