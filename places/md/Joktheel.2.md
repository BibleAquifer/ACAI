# Sela (of Edom)
**Sela (of Edom)**. 
A town in Edom where Amaziah defeated the Edomites; renamed Joktheel. 
Other forms include: 
*Jokteel*, *Jokthe-El*, *Sela*, *Selah*.

[Sela (of the Amorites)](Sela.md).
