# Hor-haggidgad
**Hor-haggidgad**. 
A location in the Sinai Peninsula, between Bene-Jaakan and Yotbathah, where the Israelites encamped on their way from Egypt to Canaan; probably to be identified with Gudgodah. 
Other forms include: 
*Gudgodah*, *Hor-Hagidgad*.
