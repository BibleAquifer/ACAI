# Laban
**Laban**. 
A location in the Sinai Peninsula, between Rimmon-Perez and Rissah, where the Israelites encamped on their way from Egypt to Canaan, not far from the place where Moses repeated the law; possibly to be identified with Libnah. 




Other information about Laban:


* May refer to the same location as: 
[Libnah (Campsite)](Libnah.md). 
* Is located near the following area(s): 
[Rimmon-perez](Rimmon-perez.md), [Rissah](Rissah.md).
