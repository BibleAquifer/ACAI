# En-gannim (of Issachar)
**En-gannim (of Issachar)**. 
A Levitical town in the territory of Issachar, assigned to the clan of Gershon; also known as Anem. 
Other forms include: 
*Anem*, *En-Gannim*. 
En-gannim (of Issachar) is also referred to as: 
[Anem](Anem.md).
