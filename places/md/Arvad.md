# Arvad
**Arvad**. 
A town on a small island off the coast of Phoenicia; its people are considered descendants of Canaan (ABD). 
Other forms include: 
*Arvadites*, *Arvan*. 




Other information about Arvad:


* Is located near the following area(s): 
[Phoenicia](Phoenicia.md).
