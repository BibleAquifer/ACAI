# Bealoth (North of Israel)
**Bealoth (North of Israel)**. 
A town north of Israel where Baana, one of Solomon's twelve governors, lived (1 Kings 4:16). 
Other forms include: 
*Aloth*. 




**Key References for Bealoth (North of Israel)**:
