# Rimmono
**Rimmono**. 
A Levitical town in the territory of Zebulun; also known as Rimmon. 
Located in the tribal area of [Tribe of Zebulun](../../../groups/md/acai/Zebulun.md).

*Dimnah*, *Rimmon*.

[Dimnah](Dimnah.md), [Rimmon (of Zebulun)](Rimmon.2.md).
