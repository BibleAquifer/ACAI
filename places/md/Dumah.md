# Dumah (of Arabia)
**Dumah (of Arabia)**. 
A region in north-central Arabia, inhabited by an ethnic group that were regarded as descendants of Dumah, the son of Ishmael, the son of Abraham. 
Other forms include: 
*Dumah*, *Edom*.
