# Ephraim Gate
**Ephraim Gate**. 
A gate in the wall of Jerusalem which Jehoash destroyed (2 Kings 14:13). 
Other forms include: 
*Gate*, *Ephraim*, *Gate of Ephraim*. 




Other information about Ephraim Gate:


* Is located within the following region(s): 
[Jerusalem](Jerusalem.md).
