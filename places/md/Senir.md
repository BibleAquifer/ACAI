# Senir
**Senir**. 
A mountain of the Anti-Lebanon range, located north-east of Israel; also known as Hermon, Sion, and Sirion. 
Other forms include: 
*Mount Hermon*, *Shenir*. 
Senir is also referred to as: 
[Mount Hermon](HermonMount.md). 
Senir is a subentry. The primary entry is 
[Mount Hermon](HermonMount.md).
