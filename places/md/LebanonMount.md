# Mount Lebanon
**Mount Lebanon**. 
A range of mountains north of Israel known for the cedar and other trees growing there. 
Other forms include: 
*Lebanese mountains*, *Lebanon*, *Lebanon mountains*, *mountains of Lebanon*.
