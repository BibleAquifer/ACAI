# Muster Gate
**Muster Gate**. 
A gate in Jerusalem opposite the house of the temple servants and the merchants (Nehemiah 3:31). 
Other forms include: 
*Enrollment Gate*, *Gate Miphkad*, *Inspection Gate*, *Miphkad*, *Miphkad Gate*.
