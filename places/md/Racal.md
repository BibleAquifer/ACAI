# Racal
**Racal**. 
A town in the territory of Judah where David sent some plunder. 
Located in the tribal area of [Tribe of Judah](../../../groups/md/acai/Judah.md). 
Other forms include: 
*Carmel*, *Rachal*, *Rakal*. 
Racal is also referred to as: 
[Carmel (of Judah)](Carmel.md).
