# Aram-zobah
**Aram-zobah**. 
= Aramean kingdom, also known as Zobah; located north of Damascus; ruled by Hadadezer; defeated and subjugated by David and then controlled by Solomon. 
Other forms include: 
*Aram Zobah*, *Aram-zobah*.
