# Asshur
**Asshur**. 
A city on the Tigris River and first capital of Assyria. 
Other forms include: 
*Ashur*, *Assur*, *Assyria*. 
Asshur is also referred to as: 
[Assyria](Assyria.md). 
Asshur is a subentry. The primary entry is 
[Assyria](Assyria.md). 




Other information about Asshur:


* Is located within the following region(s): 
[Assyria](Assyria.md).
