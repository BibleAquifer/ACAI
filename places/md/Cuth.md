# Cuth
**Cuth**. 
A town in northern Babylonia; its inhabitants were relocated to Samaria by the king of Assyria. 
Other forms include: 
*Cush*, *Cuthah*, *Kuthah*. 
Cuth is also referred to as: 
[Cuth](Cuthah.md).
