# Aram-maacah
**Aram-maacah**. 
= Aramean kingdom; located south of Mt. Hermon, east of the Jordan Valley and north of the Yarmuk. 
Other forms include: 
*Aram Maakah*, *Aram-maacah*, *Syria-maachah*.
