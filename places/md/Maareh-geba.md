# Maareh-geba
**Maareh-geba**. 
A location from which the Israelites led an ambush against Gibeah. 
Other forms include: 
*meadows of Gibeah*, *plain of Geba*, *vicinity of Gibeah*, *west of Geba*, *west of Gibeah*.
