# Cyrene
**Cyrene**. 
A city on the coast of North Africa and its people; home of Simon, who was forced to carry the cross of Christ (Matthew 27:32–44). 
Other forms include: 
*Cyrenian*, *Cyrenians*. 




Other information about Cyrene:


* Is located within the following region(s): 
[Libya](Libya.md).
