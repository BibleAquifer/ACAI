# Aphek (of Lebanon)
**Aphek (of Lebanon)**. 
A town in Lebanon, which was left unconquered by Joshua (Joshua 13:4). 
Other forms include: 
*Aphek*, *Aphik*. 
Aphek (of Lebanon) is also referred to as: 
[Aphek (of Lebanon)](Aphek.2.md).
