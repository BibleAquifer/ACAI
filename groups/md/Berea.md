# Bereans
**Bereans**. 
People who live in Berea (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Bereans*. 
The group _Bereans_ originates with `place:Berea`. 


**Key References for Bereans**: 
ACT 20:4. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Bereans” are translated as 
*from Berea* (1x).
