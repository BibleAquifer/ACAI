# Shiphmites
**Shiphmites**. 
An unknown ethnic group used to describe Zabdi (1 Chronicles 27:27) (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Shimei*, *Shiphmite*. 


**Key References for Shiphmites**: 
1CH 27:27. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Shiphmites” are translated as 
*the Shiphmite* (1x).
