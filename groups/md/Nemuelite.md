# Nemuelites
**Nemuelites**. 
People descended from Jemuel (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Jemuel*. 
The group _Nemuelites_ originates with `person:Jemuel`. 


**Key References for Nemuelites**: 
NUM 26:12. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Nemuelites” are translated as 
*The Nemuelite* (1x).
