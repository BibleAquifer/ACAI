# Perezites
**Perezites**. 
People descended from Perez (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Perez*. 
The group _Perezites_ originates with `person:Perez`. 


**Key References for Perezites**: 
NUM 26:20. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Perezites” are translated as 
*the Perezite* (1x).
