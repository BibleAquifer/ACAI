# Dedanites
**Dedanites**. 
People who live in Dedan (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Dedan*. 
The group _Dedanites_ originates with `place:Dedan`. 


**Key References for Dedanites**: 
ISA 21:13. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Dedanites” are translated as 
*of Dedanites* (1x).
