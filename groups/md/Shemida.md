# Shemidaites
**Shemidaites**. 
People descended from Shemida (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Shemida*. 
The group _Shemidaites_ originates with `person:Shemida`. 


**Key References for Shemidaites**: 
NUM 26:32. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Shemidaites” are translated as 
*the Shemidaite* (1x).
