# Sucathites
**Sucathites**. 
A family of scribes who lived at Jabez (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Hemath*. 


**Key References for Sucathites**: 
1CH 2:55. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Sucathites” are translated as 
*and Sucathites* (1x).
