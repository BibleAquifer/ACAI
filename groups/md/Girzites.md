# Girzites
**Girzites**. 
A group of people located between Philistia and Egypt that were attacked by David (1 Samuel 27:8) (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Gezrite*, *Girzite*. 


**Key References for Girzites**: 
1SA 27:8. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Girzites” are translated as 
*the Girzites* (1x).
