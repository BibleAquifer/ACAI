# Aroerites
**Aroerites**. 
People who live in Aroer (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Aroer*. 
The group _Aroerites_ originates with `place:Aroer`. 


**Key References for Aroerites**: 
1CH 11:44. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Aroerites” are translated as 
*the Aroerite* (1x).
