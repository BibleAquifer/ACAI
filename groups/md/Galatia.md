# Galatians
**Galatians**. 
People who live in Galatia (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Galatia*. 
The group _Galatians_ originates with `place:Galatia`. 


**Key References for Galatians**: 
GAL 3:1, GAL 6:18. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Galatians” are translated as 
*Galatians* (1x).
