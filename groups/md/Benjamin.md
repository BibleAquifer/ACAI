# Tribe of Benjamin
**Tribe of Benjamin**. 
People descended from Benjamin, the son of Jacob (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Benjamin*, *Benjaminites*. 
The group _Tribe of Benjamin_ originates with `person:Benjamin`. 


**Key References for Tribe of Benjamin**: 
JDG 19:16, PSA 7:0.
