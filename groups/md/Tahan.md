# Tahanites
**Tahanites**. 
People descended from Tahan (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Tahan*. 
The group _Tahanites_ originates with `person:Tahan`. 


**Key References for Tahanites**: 
NUM 26:35. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Tahanites” are translated as 
*and the Tahanite* (1x).
