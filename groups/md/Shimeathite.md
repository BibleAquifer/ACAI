# Shimeathites
**Shimeathites**. 
A family of scribes who lived at Jabez (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Hemath*. 


**Key References for Shimeathites**: 
1CH 2:55. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Shimeathites” are translated as
