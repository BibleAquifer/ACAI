# Punites
**Punites**. 
People descended from Puah (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Puah*. 
The group _Punites_ originates with `person:Puah`. 


**Key References for Punites**: 
NUM 26:23. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Punites” are translated as 
*the Punite* (1x).
