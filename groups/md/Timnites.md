# Timnites
**Timnites**. 
People who live in Timnah (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Timnite*. 
The group _Timnites_ originates with `place:Timnah`. 


**Key References for Timnites**: 
JDG 15:6. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Timnites” are translated as 
*of the Timnite* (1x).
