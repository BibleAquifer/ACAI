# Nicolaitans
**Nicolaitans**. 
A heretical group. One of the letters of Revelation was written to them (Revelation 2:6, 15) (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Nicolaitan*. 


**Key References for Nicolaitans**: 
REV 2:6, REV 2:15. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Nicolaitans” are translated as 
*Nicolaitans* (2x).
