# Shechemites
**Shechemites**. 
People descended from Shechem (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Shechem*. 
The group _Shechemites_ originates with `person:Shechem.2`. 


**Key References for Shechemites**: 
NUM 26:31. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Shechemites” are translated as 
*the Shechemite* (1x).
