# Avvim
**Avvim**. 
People who lived in Avva (modern Gaza) (acai). 


**Key References for Avvim**: 
DEU 2:23, JOS 13:3. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Avvim” are translated as 
*And the Avvim* (1x), *as well as that of the Avvites* (1x).
