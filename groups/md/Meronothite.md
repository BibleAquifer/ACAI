# Meronothites
**Meronothites**. 
People who live in Meronoth (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Meronothite*. 


**Key References for Meronothites**: 
1CH 27:30, NEH 3:7. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Meronothites” are translated as 
*the Meronothite* (2x).
