# Athenians
**Athenians**. 
People who live in Athens (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Athenians*, *Athens*. 
The group _Athenians_ originates with `place:Athens`. 


**Key References for Athenians**: 
ACT 17:21. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Athenians” are translated as 
*the Athenians* (1x).
