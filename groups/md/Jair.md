# Jairites
**Jairites**. 
People descended from Jair (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Jair*. 
The group _Jairites_ originates with `person:Jair.2`. 


**Key References for Jairites**: 
2SA 20:26. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Jairites” are translated as 
*the Jairite* (1x).
