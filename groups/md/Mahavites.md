# Mahavites
**Mahavites**. 
An unknown ethnic group used to describe Elie, one of David's mighty men (1 Chronicles 11:46) (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Joshaviah*, *Mahavite*. 


**Key References for Mahavites**: 
1CH 11:46. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Mahavites” are translated as 
*the Mahavite* (1x).
