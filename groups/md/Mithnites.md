# Mithnites
**Mithnites**. 
People from the town of Mithna. Joshaphat, one of King David's mighty men, was from Mithna (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Mithnite*. 


**Key References for Mithnites**: 
1CH 11:43. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Mithnites” are translated as 
*the Mithnite* (1x).
