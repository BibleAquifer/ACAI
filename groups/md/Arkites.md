# Arkites
**Arkites**. 
People from one of the nations that inhabited the land of Canaan before the Israelite conquest (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Arkite*. 


**Key References for Arkites**: 
GEN 10:17, 1CH 1:15. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Arkites” are translated as 
*the Arkites* (2x).
