# Zealots
**Zealots**. 
An anti-Roman political faction (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Canaanite*, *Simon*, *Simon Zelotes*. 


**Key References for Zealots**: 
MAT 10:4, MRK 3:18. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Zealots” are translated as 
*Zealot* (2x).
