# Tribe of Judah
**Tribe of Judah**. 
People descended from Judah, the son of Jacob (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Judah*, *Judahite*, *Judeans*. 
Tribe of Judah is also referred to as: 
[Jews](group:Jews.md). 
The group _Tribe of Judah_ originates with `person:Judah.4`. 


**Key References for Tribe of Judah**: 
2KI 16:6, JER 43:9.
