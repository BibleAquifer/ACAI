# Kenizzites
**Kenizzites**. 
People descended from Kenaz (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Kenizzite*. 
The group _Kenizzites_ originates with `person:Kenaz.3`. 


**Key References for Kenizzites**: 
GEN 15:19. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Kenizzites” are translated as 
*Kenizzites* (1x).
