# Lehabim
**Lehabim**. 
A group of people associated with Egypt (Genesis 10:13), possibly the same as the Lubim or Lydians (acai). 


**Key References for Lehabim**: 
GEN 10:13, 1CH 1:11. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Lehabim” are translated as 
*the Lehabites* (2x).
