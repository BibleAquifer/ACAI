# Ammonites
**Ammonites**. 
People who live in Ammon (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Ammonite*, *Ammonitess*. 
The group _Ammonites_ originates with `place:Ammon`. 


**Key References for Ammonites**: 
GEN 19:38, NUM 21:24, DEU 23:3.
