# Bichrites
**Bichrites**. 
People descended from Bichri (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Bichri*, *Bikrites*. 
The group _Bichrites_ originates with `person:Bichri`. 


**Key References for Bichrites**: 
2SA 20:14. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Bichrites” are translated as 
*the Berites* (1x).
