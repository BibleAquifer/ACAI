# Ashterathites
**Ashterathites**. 
People who live in Ashtaroth (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Aroer*, *Ashtaroth*. 
The group _Ashterathites_ originates with `place:Ashtaroth`. 


**Key References for Ashterathites**: 
1CH 11:44. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Ashterathites” are translated as 
*the Ashterathite* (1x).
