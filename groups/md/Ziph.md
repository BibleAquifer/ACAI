# Ziphites
**Ziphites**. 
People who live in Ziph (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Ziph*, *Ziphites*. 
The group _Ziphites_ originates with `place:Ziph.2`. 


**Key References for Ziphites**: 
1SA 23:19, 1SA 26:1. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Ziphites” are translated as 
*Then the Ziphites* (2x), ** (1x).
