# Zuzim
**Zuzim**. 
A people in the region of Ham defeated by Chedorlaomer (Genesis 14:5) (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Zuzites*. 


**Key References for Zuzim**: 
GEN 14:5. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Zuzim” are translated as 
*the Zuzites* (1x).
