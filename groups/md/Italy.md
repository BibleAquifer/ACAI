# Italians
**Italians**. 
People who live in Italy (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Italy*. 
The group _Italians_ originates with `place:Italy`. 


**Key References for Italians**: 
ACT 10:1. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Italians” are translated as 
*the Italian* (1x).
