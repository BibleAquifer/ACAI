# Zemarites
**Zemarites**. 
People descended from Egypt, a son of Ham (Genesis 10:18), a son of Noah (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Zemarite*. 


**Key References for Zemarites**: 
GEN 10:18, 1CH 1:16. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Zemarites” are translated as 
*the Zemarites* (2x).
