# Arvadites
**Arvadites**. 
People who lived in Arvad (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Arvadite*. 
The group _Arvadites_ originates with `place:Arvad`. 


**Key References for Arvadites**: 
GEN 10:18, 1CH 1:16. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Arvadites” are translated as 
*the Arvadites* (2x).
