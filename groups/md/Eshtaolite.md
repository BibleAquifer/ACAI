# Eshtaolites
**Eshtaolites**. 
People who live in Eshtaol (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Eshtaol*. 
The group _Eshtaolites_ originates with `place:Eshtaol`. 


**Key References for Eshtaolites**: 
1CH 2:53. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Eshtaolites” are translated as 
*and Eshtaolites* (1x).
