# Naphtuhim
**Naphtuhim**. 
People descended from Egypt, a son of Ham (Genesis 10:13), a son of Noah (acai). 


**Key References for Naphtuhim**: 
GEN 10:13, 1CH 1:11. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Naphtuhim” are translated as 
*the Naphtuhites* (2x).
