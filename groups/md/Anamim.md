# Anamim
**Anamim**. 
People of unknown descent associated with Egypt (acai). 


**Key References for Anamim**: 
GEN 10:13, 1CH 1:11. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Anamim” are translated as 
*the Anamites* (2x).
