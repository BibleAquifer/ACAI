# Letushim
**Letushim**. 
People descended from Letushim, a son of Dedan, a descendant of Abraham by Keturah (Genesis 25:3) (acai). 


**Key References for Letushim**: 
GEN 25:3. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Letushim” are translated as 
*the Letushites* (1x).
