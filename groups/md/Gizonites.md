# Gizonites
**Gizonites**. 
An unknown ethnic group used to describe Hashem, one of David's mighty men (1 Chronicles 11:34) (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Gizonite*. 


**Key References for Gizonites**: 
1CH 11:34. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Gizonites” are translated as 
*the Gizonite* (1x).
