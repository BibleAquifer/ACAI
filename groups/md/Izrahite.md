# Izrahites
**Izrahites**. 
People descended from Irzah (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Shamhuth*, *Zerah*. 
The group _Izrahites_ originates with `person:Zerah.3`. 


**Key References for Izrahites**: 
1CH 27:8. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Izrahites” are translated as 
*the Izrahite* (1x).
