# Rodanim
**Rodanim**. 
People descended from Noah's son Japheth (Genesis 10:4) (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Dodanim*, *Rodanites*. 
Rodanim is also referred to as: 
[Dodanim](group:Dodanim.md). 
Rodanim is a subentry. The primary entry is 
[Dodanim](Dodanim.md). 


**Key References for Rodanim**: 
1CH 1:7. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Rodanim” are translated as 
*and the Rodanites* (1x).
