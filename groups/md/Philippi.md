# Philippians
**Philippians**. 
People who live in Philippi (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Philippi*, *Philippian*. 
The group _Philippians_ originates with `place:Philippi`. 


**Key References for Philippians**: 
PHP 4:15, PHP 4:23. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Philippians” are translated as 
*Philippians* (1x).
