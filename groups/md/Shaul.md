# Shaulites
**Shaulites**. 
People descended from Shaul (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Shaul*. 
The group _Shaulites_ originates with `person:Shaul.2`. 


**Key References for Shaulites**: 
NUM 26:13. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Shaulites” are translated as 
*and the Shaulite* (1x).
