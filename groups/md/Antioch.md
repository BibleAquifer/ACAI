# Antiochians
**Antiochians**. 
People who live in Antioch (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Antioch*. 
The group _Antiochians_ originates with `place:Antioch`. 


**Key References for Antiochians**: 
ACT 6:5. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Antiochians” are translated as 
*from Antioch* (1x).
