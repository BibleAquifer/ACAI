# Sharonites
**Sharonites**. 
People who live in Sharon (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Sharon*. 
The group _Sharonites_ originates with `place:Sharon.2`. 


**Key References for Sharonites**: 
1CH 27:29. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Sharonites” are translated as 
*the Sharonite* (1x).
