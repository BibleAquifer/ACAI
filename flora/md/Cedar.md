# Cedar
**Cedar**. 
A tall evergreen tree with wide branches, known for its strong wood used in building (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Cedar Wood*. 
Cedar is categorized under _Wild Trees and Shrubs_. 


**Key References for Cedar**: 
JDG 9:15, 1KI 5:13, ZEC 11:1.
