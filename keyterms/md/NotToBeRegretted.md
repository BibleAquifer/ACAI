# Not to Be Regretted
**Not to Be Regretted**. 
Not feeling sorry or sad about something that has been done (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Not Regretful*. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Not to Be Regretted” are translated as 
*are irrevocable* (1x), *without regret* (1x).
