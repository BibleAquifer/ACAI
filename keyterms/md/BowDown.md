# Bow Down
**Bow Down**. 
To show respect and honor to God by lowering the body or changing posture (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Worship*. 


**Key References for Bow Down**: 
MAT 2:11, MAT 4:10, LUK 24:52, REV 5:14.
