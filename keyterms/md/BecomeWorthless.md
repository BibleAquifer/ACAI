# Become Worthless
**Become Worthless**. 
To stop being useful or important, becoming like nonsense or having no value (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Become Nonsense*. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Become Worthless” are translated as 
*they became futile* (1x).
