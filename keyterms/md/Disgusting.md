# Disgusting
**Disgusting**. 
Something that causes strong dislike or a feeling of sickness because it is wrong or not allowed (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Bad*. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Disgusting” are translated as 
*unlawful* (1x), *detestable* (1x).
