# Agitation
**Agitation**. 
A strong feeling of upset that can show as anger, fear, or alarm in people or God (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Alarm*, *Rage*, *Shock*. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Agitation” are translated as 
*and* (1x), ** (1x).
