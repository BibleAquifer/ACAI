# Justice (Right)
**Justice (Right)**. 
Doing what is fair and correct according to God's standards (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Righteousness*. 
Justice (Right) is also referred to as: 
[Be (Or Show Oneself) Just](Just.md). 
Justice (Right) is a subentry. The primary entry is 
[Be (Or Show Oneself) Just](Just.md).
