# Liking What Is Good
**Liking What Is Good**. 
Enjoying and choosing what is good and right (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Loving What Is Good*. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Liking What Is Good” are translated as 
*a lover of good* (1x).
