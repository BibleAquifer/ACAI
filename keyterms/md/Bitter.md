# Bitter (Thing)
**Bitter (Thing)**. 
Something with a sharp, unpleasant taste or something that causes sadness and pain (acai). 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Bitter (Thing)” are translated as 
*bitter* (1x), *bitter accusations* (1x).
