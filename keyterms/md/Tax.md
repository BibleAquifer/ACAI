# Tax
**Tax**. 
Money paid to the government to help run the country (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Revenue*. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Tax” are translated as 
*customs* (1x), *revenue is due* (1x), *revenue* (1x).
