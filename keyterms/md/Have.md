# Express Mercy
**Express Mercy**. 
Showing kindness and care to someone who needs help (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Or Receive Mercy*, *Show*. 
Express Mercy is also referred to as: 
[Mercy (Act)](Mercy.md). 
Express Mercy is a subentry. The primary entry is 
[Mercy (Act)](Mercy.md). 


**Key References for Express Mercy**: 
MAT 5:7, LUK 1:50, ROM 9:15, HEB 4:16. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Express Mercy” are translated as 
*mercy* (24x), *have mercy on* (11x), *show mercy* (1x), *will be shown mercy* (1x), *Have mercy on* (1x), *have had mercy* (1x), *had* (1x), *what mercy He has shown* (1x), *I will have mercy on* (1x), *I have mercy* (1x), *God has mercy* (1x), *have now received mercy* (1x), *receive mercy* (1x), *He may have mercy on* (1x), *mercy let* (1x), *had mercy on* (1x), *I was shown mercy* (2x), *had not received mercy* (1x), *you have received mercy* (1x), *to be merciful* (1x), *of His mercy* (1x), *been merciful Mercy triumphs* (1x), *over* (1x), *of mercy* (1x), *Mercy* (1x).
