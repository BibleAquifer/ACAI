# Oracle
**Oracle**. 
A way of trying to learn the future or hidden things, often seen as wrong or harmful (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Divination*. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Oracle” are translated as 
*within* (1x), *divination* (1x).
