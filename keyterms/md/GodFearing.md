# God-Fearing
**God-Fearing**. 
Having deep respect and reverence for God, living in a way that honors Him (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Devout*, *Pious*. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “God-Fearing” are translated as 
*worships Him* (1x).
