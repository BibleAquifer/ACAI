# Smear
**Smear**. 
To spread oil or another substance on someone, often as a sign of being chosen by God (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Anoint*. 


**Key References for Smear**: 
EXO 29:2, LEV 8:10, 1SA 16:13, PSA 45:8.
