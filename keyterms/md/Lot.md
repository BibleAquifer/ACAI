# Lot (Object)
**Lot (Object)**. 
A small marked object, like a pebble or pottery piece, used to make a decision (acai). 


**Key References for Lot (Object)**: 
LEV 16:8, JOS 14:2, PSA 16:5, PRO 16:33.
