# Gehenna
**Gehenna**. 
A place where people are punished after death (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Hell*. 


**Key References for Gehenna**: 
MAT 5:22, MAT 10:28, MAT 23:33, LUK 12:5. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Gehenna” are translated as 
*of hell* (3x), *hell* (9x).
