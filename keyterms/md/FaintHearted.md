# Faint-Hearted
**Faint-Hearted**. 
Feeling weak or afraid, and lacking the courage to keep trying (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Discouraged*. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Faint-Hearted” are translated as 
*fainthearted* (1x).
