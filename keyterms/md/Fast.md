# Abstain from Food
**Abstain from Food**. 
To not eat food for a certain time to show respect or pray to God (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Fast*. 
Abstain from Food is also referred to as: 
[Fasting](Fasting.md). 


**Key References for Abstain from Food**: 
MAT 6:16, LUK 18:12, ACT 13:2, ACT 14:23. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Abstain from Food” are translated as 
*After fasting* (1x), *you fast* (1x), *they are fasting* (1x), *fast* (7x), *your fasting* (1x), *they will fast* (3x), *fasting* (4x), ** (1x), *fast like* (1x), *I fast* (1x), *after they had fasted* (1x), *Fast* (1x), *and hunger* (1x), *without food* (1x).
