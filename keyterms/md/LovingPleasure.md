# Loving Pleasure
**Loving Pleasure**. 
Enjoying and wanting to have fun and good times a lot (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Lover of Pleasure*. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Loving Pleasure” are translated as 
*lovers of pleasure* (1x).
