# Be Unequally Yoked
**Be Unequally Yoked**. 
Being joined with someone in a way that is not good or fair, especially in close relationships (acai). 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Be Unequally Yoked” are translated as 
*unequally yoked* (1x).
