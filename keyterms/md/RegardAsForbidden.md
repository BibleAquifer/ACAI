# Regard as Forbidden
**Regard as Forbidden**. 
To think of something as not allowed or ritually unclean (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Consider Unclean*. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Regard as Forbidden” are translated as 
*you shall regard* (1x).
