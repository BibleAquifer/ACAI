# Tranquil
**Tranquil**. 
Calm and peaceful in feelings or surroundings (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Peaceful*, *Quiet*. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Tranquil” are translated as 
*tranquil* (1x).
