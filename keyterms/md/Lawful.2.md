# Lawful
**Lawful**. 
Following the rules or laws that are set (acai).

*Within the Law*. 
Lawful is also referred to as: 
[Be Lawful](Lawful.md). 
Lawful is a subentry. The primary entry is 
[Be Lawful](Lawful.md).

*legal* (1x), *under the law* (1x).
