# To Dedicate
**To Dedicate**. 
To set a building apart for God's use by making it special and ready for worship (acai). 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “To Dedicate” are translated as 
*it Let* (1x), *it* (1x), *the* (1x), *dedicated* (1x).
