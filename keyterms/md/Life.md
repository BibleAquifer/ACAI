# Life (Existence)
**Life (Existence)**. 
The time a person is alive from the beginning until the end (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Existence*. 
Life (Existence) is also referred to as: 
[Inner Self](InnerSelf.md), [Life (State)](Life.2.md), [Life (Span)](Life.3.md), [To Live](Live.2.md).
