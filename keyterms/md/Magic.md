# Magic
**Magic**. 
Trying to control events or people by using supernatural powers or spells, often based on false beliefs (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Witchcraft*. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Magic” are translated as 
*magic arts* (1x).
