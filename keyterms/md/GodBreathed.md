# God-Breathed
**God-Breathed**. 
Inspired by God, showing that the message comes from Him (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*God-Breathed*. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “God-Breathed” are translated as 
*God-breathed* (1x).
