# Godhead
**Godhead**. 
The true nature and being of God as divine (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Divine Nature*. 
Godhead is also referred to as: 
[Divine](Divine.md). 
Godhead is a subentry. The primary entry is 
[Divine](Divine.md). 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Godhead” are translated as 
*Deity* (1x).
