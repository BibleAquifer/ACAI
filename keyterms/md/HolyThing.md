# Holy Thing
**Holy Thing**. 
An item or offering set apart as sacred and dedicated to God (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Holiness*. 
Holy Thing is also referred to as: 
[Holy](Holy.md). 
Holy Thing is a subentry. The primary entry is 
[Holy](Holy.md).
