# Kindness (Solidarity)
**Kindness (Solidarity)**. 
A commitment to keep promises and do good for others, especially when bound by a special agreement or moral duty (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Grace*, *Joint Liability*, *Lovingkindness*, *Solidarity*. 
Kindness (Solidarity) is also referred to as: 
[Be Kind](Kind.md), [Kindness (Graciousness)](Kindness.2.md), [Kindness (Affection)](Kindness.3.md), [Kindness (Benevolence)](Kindness.4.md). 


**Key References for Kindness (Solidarity)**: 
EXO 34:6, DEU 7:9, PSA 103:8, ROM 2:4.
