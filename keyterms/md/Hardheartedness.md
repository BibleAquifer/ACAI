# Hardheartedness
**Hardheartedness**. 
Refusing to listen or change, showing a stubborn and unkind attitudes (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Stubbornness*. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Hardheartedness” are translated as 
*hardness of heart* (3x).
