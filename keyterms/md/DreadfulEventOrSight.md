# Dreadful Event
**Dreadful Event**. 
Something scary or shocking that makes people feel fear (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Dreadful Sight*. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Dreadful Event” are translated as 
*fearful sights* (1x).
