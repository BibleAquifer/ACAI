# Be Glorified
**Be Glorified**. 
To be highly honored and praised by others (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Be Honored*. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Be Glorified” are translated as 
*to be glorified* (1x), *will be glorified* (1x).
