# Week
**Week**. 
A period lasting seven days (acai). 


**Key References for Week**: 
MAT 28:1, LUK 24:1, ACT 20:7, 1CO 16:2. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Week” are translated as 
*of the week* (3x), *week* (6x).
