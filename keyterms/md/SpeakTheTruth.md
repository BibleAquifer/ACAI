# Speak the Truth
**Speak the Truth**. 
To say what is real and honest without lying or hiding facts (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Hold to the Truth*. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Speak the Truth” are translated as 
*by telling you the truth* (1x), *speaking the truth* (1x).
