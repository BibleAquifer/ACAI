# Double, Tongued
**Double, Tongued**. 
Saying one thing but doing something different, showing a false or fake attitude (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Double-Tongued*, *Hypocritical*. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Double, Tongued” are translated as 
*double-tongued* (1x).
