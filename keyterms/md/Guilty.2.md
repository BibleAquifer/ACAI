# Guilty
**Guilty**. 
Having done something wrong and being responsible for it (acai). 
Guilty is also referred to as: 
[To Sin](Sin.md), [Sin (Guilty)](Sin.4.md). 
Guilty is a subentry. The primary entry is 
[To Sin](Sin.md). 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Guilty” are translated as 
*are being punished* (1x), *does he not convict himself* (1x), *and for their guilt* (1x).
