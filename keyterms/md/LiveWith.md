# Live With
**Live With**. 
To share a place and life with another person or people (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Live Together*. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Live With” are translated as 
*we will also live with* (1x), *we live* (1x), *we will also live with Him* (1x).
