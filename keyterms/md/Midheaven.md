# Midheaven
**Midheaven**. 
The point in the sky that is directly above a person or place on the earth (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*High in the Sky*, *Midair*. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Midheaven” are translated as 
*overhead* (3x).
