# Unrighteous Act
**Unrighteous Act**. 
An action that is wrong or unfair according to God's laws and rules (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Crime*. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Unrighteous Act” are translated as 
*wrongdoing* (1x), *crime* (1x), *iniquities* (1x).
