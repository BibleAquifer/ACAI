# Lawfully
**Lawfully**. 
Doing things in the right way by following the rules (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*According to the Rules*. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Lawfully” are translated as 
*legitimately* (1x), *according to the rules* (1x).
