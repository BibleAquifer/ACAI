# Instruction
**Instruction**. 
Teaching or rules given to help people learn how to live wisely and do what is right (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Law*. 


**Key References for Instruction**: 
NEH 8:8, PSA 119:1, PRO 1:8, ISA 2:3.
