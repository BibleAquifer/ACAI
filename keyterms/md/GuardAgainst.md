# To Guard Against
**To Guard Against**. 
To watch carefully and protect oneself from possible harm or danger (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*To Keep Under Watch*, *To Watch Over*. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “To Guard Against” are translated as 
*watch* (1x).
