# Vow (Promise)
**Vow (Promise)**. 
A serious promise made to God to do something special or important (acai). 
Vow (Promise) is also referred to as: 
[Vow (Act)](Vow.md). 
Vow (Promise) is a subentry. The primary entry is 
[Vow (Act)](Vow.md). 


**Key References for Vow (Promise)**: 
NUM 30:3, JDG 11:30, 1SA 1:11, PSA 50:14.

*a vow* (11x), *a solemn vow* (1x), *is a vow* (1x), *to fulfill a vow or* (1x), *in fulfillment of a vow* (1x), *your vow offerings* (2x), *vow* (5x), *the vow* (2x), *of his vow* (1x), *freewill* (1x), *your vow* (1x), *her vow* (1x), *the vows* (3x), *of the vows* (1x), *while under a vow* (1x), *then the vows* (1x), *whether her vows* (1x), *you* (1x), *of the offerings* (1x), *and your vow offerings* (1x), *this vow* (1x), *as he had vowed* (1x), *his vow* (1x), *your vows* (4x), *my vows* (7x), *Your vows* (1x), *our vows* (1x), *his vows* (1x), *of my vows* (1x), *vows* (1x), *what you have promised* (1x), *vows to Him* (1x).
