# Irreconcilable
**Irreconcilable**. 
Not willing to make peace or agree with others (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Unwilling to Be at Peace With Others*. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Irreconcilable” are translated as 
*unforgiving* (1x).
