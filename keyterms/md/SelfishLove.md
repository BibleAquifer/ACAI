# Selfish Love
**Selfish Love**. 
Caring mostly about yourself instead of others (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Self-Centered Love*. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Selfish Love” are translated as 
*lovers of themselves* (1x).
