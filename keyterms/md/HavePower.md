# Have Power
**Have Power**. 
Being strong enough to do something or to control what happens (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Be Strong Enough To*. 
Have Power is also referred to as: 
[Be Able To](AbleTo.md), [Authority (Control)](Authority.2.md). 
Have Power is a subentry. The primary entry is 
[Authority (Control)](Authority.2.md). 


**Key References for Have Power**: 
ACT 19:20, PHP 4:13, JAS 5:16. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Have Power” are translated as 
*could* (1x), *with the strength* (1x), *I am too weak* (1x), *they were unable* (1x), *have been able* (1x), *The attack* (1x), *prevail* (1x), *I can do* (1x), *to prevail* (1x).
