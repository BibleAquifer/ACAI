# Obstacle
**Obstacle**. 
Something that makes it hard to do or go forward (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Cause for Stumbling*, *Difficulty*. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Obstacle” are translated as 
*obstacle* (1x).
