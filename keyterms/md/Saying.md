# Proverb
**Proverb**. 
A short, simple statement that teaches a lesson or truth (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Parable*. 


**Key References for Proverb**: 
PSA 78:2, PRO 1:1, PRO 10:1, PRO 25:1.
