# Unable to Speak Well
**Unable to Speak Well**. 
Having difficulty speaking clearly or expressing ideas well (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Not Eloquent*. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Unable to Speak Well” are translated as 
*am unskilled* (2x).
