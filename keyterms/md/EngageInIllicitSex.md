# Engage in Illicit Sex
**Engage in Illicit Sex**. 
To have sexual relations that are wrong or forbidden, often involving prostitution (acai). 
Engage in Illicit Sex is also referred to as: 
[Fornication](Fornication.md), [Sexual Immorality](Fornication.2.md). 
Engage in Illicit Sex is a subentry. The primary entry is 
[Fornication](Fornication.md). 


**Key References for Engage in Illicit Sex**: 
MAT 5:32, 1CO 6:18, GAL 5:19, EPH 5:3. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Engage in Illicit Sex” are translated as 
*sins sexually* (1x), *We should not commit sexual immorality* (1x), *did* (1x), *commit sexual immorality* (1x), *to be sexually immoral* (1x), *were immoral* (2x), *committed sexual immorality* (1x), *sexual immorality* (14x), *are not illegitimate children* (1x), *the meat* (1x), *from sexual immorality* (1x), *of a kind* (1x), *for sexual immorality* (1x), *of sexual immorality* (1x), *immorality* (5x).
