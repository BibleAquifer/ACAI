# Be at Peace
**Be at Peace**. 
To live quietly and happily with others without fighting (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Live in Peace*. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Be at Peace” are translated as 
*be at peace* (1x), *live at peace* (1x), *live in peace* (1x), *Live in peace* (1x).
