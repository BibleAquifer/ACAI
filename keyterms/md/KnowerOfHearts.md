# Knower of Hearts
**Knower of Hearts**. 
One who understands what people truly think and feel deep inside (acai). 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Knower of Hearts” are translated as 
*know everyone’s heart* (1x), *knows the heart* (1x).
