# Certification
**Certification**. 
Something that shows or proves that something is real or true (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Evidence of Genuineness*, *Proof*, *Validation*. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Certification” are translated as 
*seal* (1x).
