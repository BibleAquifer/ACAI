# Disobey
**Disobey**. 
To choose not to follow a rule or law on purpose (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Break the Law*, *Violate the Law*. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Disobey” are translated as 
*you yourself violate the law* (1x).
