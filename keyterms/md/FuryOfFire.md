# Fury of Fire
**Fury of Fire**. 
A strong, burning force used as a symbol for God's punishment (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Raging Fire*. 
Fury of Fire is also referred to as: 
[Eternal Fire](EternalFire.md). 
Fury of Fire is a subentry. The primary entry is 
[Eternal Fire](EternalFire.md). 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Fury of Fire” are translated as 
*fire* (1x).
