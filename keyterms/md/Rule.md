# To Rule
**To Rule**. 
To be in charge and make decisions for people (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Govern*. 
To Rule is also referred to as: 
[Decided](Decided.md). 
To Rule is a subentry. The primary entry is 
[Decided](Decided.md). 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “To Rule” are translated as 
*judging* (2x).
