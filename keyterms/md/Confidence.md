# Confidence
**Confidence**. 
A feeling of trust and surety in someone or something (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Assurance*, *Trust*. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Confidence” are translated as 
** (1x), *confident* (1x), *assurance* (1x), *the assurance* (1x).
