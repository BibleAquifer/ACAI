# Pitiful
**Pitiful**. 
Deserving sympathy because of sadness or a hard situation (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Be Pitied*. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Pitiful” are translated as 
*to be pitied more* (1x), *pitiful* (1x).
