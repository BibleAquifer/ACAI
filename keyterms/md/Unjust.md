# Unjust
**Unjust**. 
Not fair or right in how people are treated or decisions are made (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Unjustly*, *Unrighteous*. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Unjust” are translated as 
*of unjust* (1x).
