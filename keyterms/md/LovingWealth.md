# Loving Wealth
**Loving Wealth**. 
Wanting money and riches more than anything else (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Lover of Money*. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Loving Wealth” are translated as 
*lovers of money* (2x).
