# Happiness
**Happiness**. 
A feeling of joy that comes when good things happen in life (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Blessedness*. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Happiness” are translated as 
*blessedness* (1x), *blessing* (2x).
