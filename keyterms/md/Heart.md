# Heart (Figurative)
**Heart (Figurative)**. 
The center of a person's thoughts, feelings, and inner self (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Inner Self*, *Mind*. 
Heart (Figurative) is also referred to as: 
[Rely On](RelyOn.md). 


**Key References for Heart (Figurative)**: 
MAT 5:8, MAT 22:37, ROM 10:10, EPH 3:17.
