# Set Free
**Set Free**. 
To be let go from control or made free from problems like sin, sickness, or being trapped (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Release*, *Settle With*. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Set Free” are translated as 
*to reconcile* (1x), ** (1x), *free* (1x).
