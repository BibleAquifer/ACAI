# Bring to the Attention Of
**Bring to the Attention Of**. 
To tell someone about something so they notice it, usually in a casual way (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Speak About To*. 




In the New Testament (BSB), words representing “Bring to the Attention Of” are translated as 
*you are bringing* (1x).
