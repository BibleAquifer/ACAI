# Goose
**Goose**. 
A large bird with webbed feet, and sometimes served as food (acai). 
Goose is categorized under _Birds_. 


**Key References for Goose**: 
1KI 5:3. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Goose” are translated as 
*poultry* (1x).
