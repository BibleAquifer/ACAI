# Lion
**Lion**. 
A huge cat with yellowish fur and a mane on males, known for its strength and fearlessness (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Lioness*. 
Lion is categorized under _Mammals_.

GEN 49:9, 1KI 13:24, REV 5:5.
