# Cattle
**Cattle**. 
Domesticated animals used for milk, plowing, and sacrifice (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Bull*, *Calf*, *Cow*, *Heifer*, *Ox*. 
Cattle is possibly the same as or similar to: 
[Wild Ox](WildOx.md). 
Cattle is categorized under _Mammals_.

GEN 1:24, GEN 32:6, LUK 15:23.
