# Bird
**Bird**. 
A small flying animal with feathers and wings. Some birds were offered as sacrifices to God in worship (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Fowl*. 
Bird is categorized under _Birds_. 


**Key References for Bird**: 
GEN 1:20, LEV 1:14, MAT 6:26.
