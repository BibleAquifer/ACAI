# Sheep
**Sheep**. 
A young sheep, often used for sacrifices, meat, and wool (acai). 
Other forms include: 
*Ewe*, *Lamb*, *Ram*. 
Sheep is categorized under _Mammals_.

GEN 22:13, LEV 9:18, JHN 1:29.
