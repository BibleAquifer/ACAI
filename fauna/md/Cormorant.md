# Cormorant
**Cormorant**. 
A sea bird that often perches by the water (acai). 
Cormorant is categorized under _Birds_. 


**Key References for Cormorant**: 
LEV 11:17, DEU 14:17. 


In the Old Testament (BSB), words representing “Cormorant” are translated as 
*the cormorant* (2x).
